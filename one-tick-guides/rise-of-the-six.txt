> **__Rotation__**
.tag:Rotation
⬥ Do the entire rotation on the <:dummy:656844963522281473>
⬥ Turning off revolution <:necroauto:1137809137401602109> will help.

Build 12 Necrosis Stacks with the following rotation:
.
⬥ <:dummy:656844963522281473> + <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> → <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:necroauto:1137809137401602109> → <:necroauto:1137809137401602109>

The person starting the fight should signal they are ready with an ability such as <:freedom:535541258240786434>, the starter will also have to do it slightly differently. They also must have <:spec:537340400273195028> bound somewhere on their actionbar.

Starter Rotation is as follows:
*Note: make sure you press 1 + click <:spec:537340400273195028> 4 ticks after <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933>*
⬥ Signal with <:freedom:535541258240786434> → <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> + <:undeadslayer:641339922019516416> → click Disrupt Shadow portal → space → 1 + click <:spec:537340400273195028> in the same tick.

Once the person who is starting has signalled they are about to use <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> do the following rotation 3 ticks after their signal:
⬥ <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> + <:undeadslayer:641339922019516416> → wait 4 ticks → <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:spec:537340400273195028>
