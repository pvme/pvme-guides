> **__K'ril Tsutsaroth, General Graardor, Commander Zilyana and King Black Dragon__**
.tag:Intro
This guide will cover the basic strategy to get your first 1 tick boss kills. These 1 tick kills are fairly consistent, and are a good entry point to make progress towards minimum tick feats roles.

⬥ These Bosses have been grouped together due to them using the same setup and rotation. This works as they are in a similar HP range (40-60k), K'ril and Zilyana will need better luck or more gmauls due to having slightly more hp.
    • You may have to incorporate a <:gmaul:796989663051120640> <:eof:787526151978614824> end with <:ingen:641339234111848463>
⬥ For general 1 tick kill information, and some useful tips and tricks make sure to read <#1132322878323441674>.

This is the basic rotation for these speed kills, many other rotations can be done instead.

**War's Retreat Build**
<:dba:603979368850653216> <:spec:537340400273195028> → Use the crystal til 120% adren → <:lengmh:883134308146098227> <:spec:537340400273195028> + click crystal → 4t <:zerk:535532854004678657> → Enter portal after charging back to full adrenaline → Join Instance

**In Instance**
⬥ At any point after entering the instance switch to Grim <:grim:568262896375824385> and activate.

**__Rotation__**
This is the basic rotation for these speed kills, many other rotations can be done instead.

**War's Retreat Build**
Build 12 <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> stacks with the following rotation:
<:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> → <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:necroauto:1137809137401602109> → <:necroauto:1137809137401602109>
Off and wait 10 seconds to drop combat:
Equip <:excal:641337999170207763> + (tc) + <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> + click floor

**In Instance**
⬥ At any point after entering the portal switch to <:grim:568262896375824385> and activate.
⬥ You only need the second rotation including <:gmaul:796989663051120640> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> for Commander Zilyana.
⬥ <:dragonslayer:641339921814126594> / <:demonslayer:641339921675845633> can be used for extra damage on KBD / K'ril after <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118>

Wait until <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> has 10 seconds or less on its cooldown then enter the instance:
Go through door + <:dive:1049378668197195808> → wait until <:dive:1049378668197195808> has 11 seconds on its cooldown remaining (1s if mobile is on your gear) →
⬥ ( tc + <:smokecloud:856635090641879050> + <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> )
