## __Introduction__
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

Raksha is a dinosaur dubbed the "Shadow Colossus" which is located in Anachronia. Raksha can be fought either solo or duo, with linear hp scaling for duo mode. The drop rates for duo are also exactly the same as solo.

Each Raksha kill is worth <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:gpkill_raksha$, with <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:gpkill_raksha_commons$ of that coming from commons. Advanced players can get up to $data_pvme:kph_raksha_gm$ kph, earning on average $data_pvme:gphour_raksha_gm$ gp/hr.

This guide assumes you are already familiar with Raksha's mechanics, phasing, and attack rotations. If not, please read <#1020559920669663304>.

## __Tips for the Fight__
### General Tips
⬥ Kill times vary a lot and sometimes you will get specials you normally skip, pay attention to the phases hp and adapt accordingly.
⬥ Vuln bombs are easy to use during the rockfall special because Raksha will not move.
⬥ Spamming ripper demon specials is important to phase the boss at desired times.
