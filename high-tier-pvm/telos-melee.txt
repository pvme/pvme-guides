> **__Introduction__**

.tag:intro
**__Why Melee?__**
Using Melee <:Attack:689509250472476758> against Telos is generally more dangerous and risky than Magic. However, you can gain some benefits such as less RNG needed on volcanic golems and KO protection.

This guide assumes 250%+ and work best at 1000%+, but these rotations still work below 250% with some tweaks. Adjust accordingly from there.

.
**__Notable Enrage Checkpoints and Changes past 999%__**
⬥ **1000%:** P4 one rock will fall, P5 multiple rocks will fall, immortality no longer works P5, and Telos will freedom stuns and binds with a 16 tick cooldown
⬥ **2000%:** "So you think you can guard?" Achievement, aka Silver Warden
⬥ **2700%:** Tendrils damage required caps at 30,000
⬥ **3300%:** The first prayer drain on P3 will smite you from full unless Telos touches the beam
⬥ **4000%:** Hard enrage cap; "The True Warden" Achievement, aka Gold Warden

> __**Phase 1**__
.tag:p1
Throughout 250% to 4000%, the P1 rotation will always remain the same. The big objectives are to block and reduce as much damage as possible without using many defensives and to apply a Barge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> bleed + Hurricane <:cane:535532878969438210> on tendrils perfectly.

Target cycle + <:swh:641670143197446182> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> + <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> → [<:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:slaughter:535532879237873666> if you get a Relentless proc on <:swh:641670143197446182> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>] → <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:dismember:535532879376023572> → <:cleave:535532878616985610> → <:deci:535532879325822986> → <:sever:535532879577612298> [Skip if you got a Relentless <:relentless5:712244800920748092> proc] → <:zerk:535532854004678657> → <:divert:787904334377648130> → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:cleave:535532878616985610> → <:deci:535532879325822986> → s<:assault:535532855191928842> → r<:assault:535532855191928842> + <:cane:535532878969438210> → <:cleave:535532878616985610>

If your Divert <:divert:787904334377648130> splashes completely, just use an adrenaline renewal <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538>. Divert <:divert:787904334377648130> also works much better at higher enrages, but it's still good enough for lower enrages above 500% or so. This lets you save your adrenaline renewal for emergencies if it works out. Take off your platebody for a better chance of Divert <:divert:787904334377648130> working.

That stalled Assault <:assault:535532855191928842> is important so you line up two Assault <:assault:535532855191928842> hits and Hurricane <:cane:535532878969438210> all on top of each other for tendrils, nearly guaranteeing an instant free or a tick-late free even past 2700% enrage. This is less important at lower enrages and you don't need to stall at lower enrages, but if you want to do enrages above 2000% it is highly recommended you learn how to do it.

.
During the Cleave <:cleave:535532878616985610> and Decimate <:deci:535532879325822986> after the initial Berserk <:zerk:535532854004678657>, you should be running out of range to bait out Telos’s Magic attacks. This is super important because Telos’s Magic attacks hit far less than his Melee attacks, which makes it much easier to survive when under Berserk <:zerk:535532854004678657>, especially as you approach 4000%.

.
After the Cleave <:cleave:535532878616985610> after Hurricane <:cane:535532878969438210>, it depends entirely on how much LP Telos has. If he hits 450k right after Cleave <:cleave:535532878616985610>, wait until the tick Telos phases, then use Quake <:quake:535532879506309150> + Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> to the drop-off point. That Quake <:quake:535532879506309150> is important because it lets you carry over that damage to the next phase immediately, giving you a noticeable headstart for P2. This works because AoE abilities like Quake <:quake:535532879506309150>, Bombardment <:bombard:535541306391265284>, and Chain <:chain:535533833056026624> calculate their damage late enough to be forced into the next phase if you use them on the same tick as phasing to the next phase. Practically speaking, only Quake <:quake:535532879506309150> can be used like this as Cleave <:cleave:535532878616985610> hits too quickly.

If Telos is not immediately phased after that Cleave <:cleave:535532878616985610>, do strong basics like Decimate <:deci:535532879325822986> and Sever <:sever:535532879577612298> to get it down before uppercut happens, then Quake <:quake:535532879506309150> prephase if it lines up nicely. If you do get uppercut, phase him immediately after and prephase with a Quake <:quake:535532879506309150>.

If you have over 77% adrenaline, you will want to ZGS spec <:zgs:626465964325601290> <:spec:537340400273195028> before bleeding Greater Flurry <:gflurry:535532879283879977>. Otherwise, reverse that order.

Wait a tick → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → bleed <:gflurry:535532879283879977> → <:zgs:626465964325601290> <:spec:537340400273195028> → <:cleave:535532878616985610> + <:escape:535541258832052231> same tick → s<:deci:535532879325822986> + <:bd:535532854281764884> back into ZGS spec → r<:deci:535532879325822986> + <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:dismember:535532879376023572> + throw <:stickybomb:655341074306301964> right as Telos autos → <:sever:535532879577612298> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → basics → <:assault:535532855191928842> → <:freedom:535541258240786434> → <:res:535541258844635148> → basics until 300k → <:quake:535532879506309150> prephase

Waiting that one tick before Barge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> is extremely important, because if you just Barge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> instantly you will deal no damage. So wait a tick for that damage immunity to pass by. An easy way to time this is to spam click a <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787>, then Barge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> immediately after you see your prayer points be restored.

The Escape <:escape:535541258832052231> and Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> back in are done to ensure Telos leaves the black beam with zero RNG. It must be perfectly done on the same tick as your other ability though, as anything less than perfect play like being late will cause the lure to fail and you will have to end up blocking the black beam. Focus on getting the Escape <:escape:535541258832052231>, as the Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> back is far more lenient.

**__If after uppercut on P1 OR no ZGS:__**

Wait a tick → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → (5taa) + <:backhand:535532854302605333> → <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> + <:escape:535541258832052231> → <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:dismember:535532879376023572> → <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:slaughter:535532879237873666> + walk → <:assault:535532855191928842> → <:dismember:535532879376023572> → <:freedom:535541258240786434> → <:res:535541258844635148> → basics and thresholds until 300k → <:quake:535532879506309150> prephase

Ideally you should not get this, but the above rotation deals with it correctly. If you don't have a ZGS, just use more basics between Assault <:assault:535532855191928842> and Freedom <:freedom:535541258240786434>. 

The autoattack with 2h <:zgs:626465964325601290> after Destroy <:destroy:535532879330148352> is known as “5TAA”, or “5T” for short. Basically, after a dual wield <:khopeshmh:513206794844110858> <:khopeshoh:513206794752098327> ability, you can switch to your 2h <:zgs:626465964325601290> weapon and force out a quick autoattack two ticks after that ability. Because of the stun duration, this 5T wastes just enough time yet still gains damage to allow for the longest stun duration from a bled Destroy <:destroy:535532879330148352> to Backhand <:backhand:535532854302605333> to Forceful Backhand <:fbackhand:535532879346794516>. This will get used in both P3 and P4, so even if you don’t get uppercut on P1 to force this rotation, it’s worth learning this.

> **__Phase 3__**
.tag:p3
The goal in Phase 3 is to burst down Telos as quickly and consistently as possible.

Wait a tick → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → (5taa) + <:backhand:535532854302605333> → <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> + <:bd:535532854281764884> into the beam → <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:dismember:535532879376023572> → <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> → <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:slaughter:535532879237873666> + walk → <:cleave:535532878616985610> → <:assault:535532855191928842> → basics to 150k

You don’t necessarily need to Quake <:quake:535532879506309150> prephase here because P4 is somewhat time-locked anyway, but it's helpful to. If you don’t have enough adrenaline to fit in both Blood Tendrils <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> and Slaughter <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:slaughter:535532879237873666> before Assault, just skip Blood Tendrils <:mwspear:694566917456789554> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> and do some basics instead.

In order to make P4 better at higher enrages so you aren’t scrambling for adrenaline, you can lure Telos into the beam and balance the bar. That way, when Telos drains you right after Assault <:assault:535532855191928842>, it won't completely deplete your adrenaline. Should the beam spawn on Telos, do this conservatively, else you will have to delay your Assault <:assault:535532855191928842> by one ability so you can use Disruption Shield <:disrupt:535614336207552523> on the explosion losslessly.

> **__Phase 4__**
.tag:p4
The goal here is to do as much damage as possible to Telos before he can fire off any special attacks, so rocks are completely skipped. 

.
Wait a tick → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → (5taa) + <:backhand:535532854302605333> → <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> → <:swh:641670143197446182> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>

Use Limitless <:limitless:641339233638023179> here if you can. If your adrenaline is really dire, skip Statius’s warhammer <:swh:641670143197446182> <:eofor:745279787471470713> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> and Quake <:quake:535532879506309150> instead.

For font 1, Resonance <:res:535541258844635148> + Reflect <:reflect:535541258786177064> and build to 100% adrenaline. Wait for the chatbox message “Telos: You dare to defy me?” and throw a sticky bomb <:stickybomb:655341074306301964> on the golems right when that happens. Get out of range from the golems, then Barge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> Telos right as he gets up and throws the instant-kill.

> **__Phase 5__**
.tag:p5
Make sure you have Disruption Shield <:disrupt:535614336207552523> active and are praying Melee <:DeflectMelee:544195488447201300> at the start of the phase. If you do the rest of this phase perfectly, Telos and golems cannot KO you at the start of the phase no matter what, as Disruption Shield <:disrupt:535614336207552523> will always prioritize blocking Telos's Magic attack.

Make sure that when using defensive abilities before zerk <:zerk:535532854004678657>, that you are clicking on telos with your mouse in order to deal lossless auto damage. Click away from telos one defensive ability before greater barge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> to not lose the bleed.
