> **__Special Attack Information__**
.tag:spec
__**Tail Swipe**__
Raksha will do a Tail Swipe special attack if the player is in Raksha's melee distance. Raksha does a spin animation and hits a 3 square radius around it. Players will get stunned and hit around 6,000 to 8,000 damage. In order to avoid this special attack, move 2 squares back from scythe distance. Alternatively you can Escape <:escape:535541258832052231> or Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884>. Raksha also spawns 3 anima blobs at 0% shadow absorption when he does this special attack. Getting hit by this causes <:antitheism:787523880281047040>, which disables protection prayers.

.
__**Charge**__
Raksha will do a Charge special attack if the player is not in melee distance. Raksha charges towards the player, stunning them and hitting around 6,000 to 8,000 damage. In order to avoid this special attack, players will need to move sideways. Raksha also spawns 3 anima blobs at 0% shadow absorption when he does this special attack. Getting hit by this causes <:antitheism:787523880281047040>, which disables protection prayers.
