__**North Tank**__
Also known as NT or Sand Tank. This person must voke and tank the Sand pool during mirage. Since the DPS will be focused on the other 3 pools first, the NT will be tanking mirage for the longest amount of time. That being said, a defensive rotation is advised as well as using <:freedom:535541258240786434>, <:disrupt:535614336207552523>, and shield dome as effectively as possible. A good way of dealing with this pool is using <:devo:513190158728953857>, then using <:res:535541258844635148> once a good number of stacks are built in order to receive a good heal, then using <:freedom:535541258240786434> and other defensive thresholds/ultimates as needed.

__**Jelly Wrangler**__
This person is to tag, voke and drag jellyfish away from the other players during mirage phase. A maximum number of 10 jellyfish can be present in the instance at one point. Using ice barrage is a good way of dealing with the jellyfish as it will affect multiple targets. Using melee prayer and <:devo:513190158728953857>  is also critical in reducing damage taken. If enough jellyfish surround the JW, it is very easy to get KO'ed. Using defensives is crucial as killing jellyfish in your party will force Yakamaru to spawn another jellyfish near the team. Bolas can also be used if you are ranging.
