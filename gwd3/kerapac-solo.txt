> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
This guide assumes you are already familar with the basic mechanics of Kerapac which can be seen here <#869082904842604604>.

Zamorak staff <:zamorakstaff:873142795706179651> can be used instead of guthix staff as you will not be having any hitchance issues during the fight and it lowers Kerapac's magic level and applies a 1-minute accuracy debuff making it less likely for him to hit you. It also does the same damage and has the same adrenaline cost along with a cheap cost to purchase.

It is recomended to have a dragonkin slayer task <:slayer:797896049548066857> as the helmet boost does work. You may also use the permier artefact <:premierartefact:918517285176623104> to have a 10% chance to not decrease the slayer task count with each kill.

__**Additional Considerations/Info**__
⬥ An Amulet of zealots <:zealots:926021352626982942> can be used alongside an <:eofpurple:780401412936040478> for phases 1 through 3, switching to the <:eofpurple:780401412936040478> for special attacks and for phase 4. This should be around a 1% damage increase in exchange for extra effort.

The former will be more consistent and generally more useful, but the latter should result in faster kills alongside the aforementioned Amulet of zealots.

__**Additional Considerations/Info**__
⬥ Inspiration <:inspiration:643505652634746881> should be used over Reckless <:reckless:643505179378974748>. The increased damage taken from <:reckless:643505179378974748> compromises the practicality of using the Eldritch Crossbow spec <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>.

    • Fury of the Small <:furyofthesmall:697808773917573233>

    • Avoid warping back to low HP at all costs when using ranged.
