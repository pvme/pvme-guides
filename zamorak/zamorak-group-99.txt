> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
⬥ 99% Zamorak is currently the best known enrage for killing Zamorak for money due to its low effort and relatively good drop rate on rares, making it very popular to farm.

⬥ 20 kills per hour/sub 2:30 kills can be expected for fast 5man 99% teams using the rotations in this guide. These suggested strategies are not the most optimal, but we do our best.

⬥ Hybrid using 2 combat styles is most suited for the desired kill times. This guide mostly focuses on mage-melee hybrid.

⬥ 3 roles are used in this guide: witch killer, pad charger, and free roles.

⬥ <:conservationofenergy:697808773711921195> Conservation of energy and your choice of other relics should be used.

⬥ This is an advanced guide. For learning the how to defeat Zamorak refer to <#992218608602189854> and <#996607815370145882>

> __**Roles Layout**__
.tag:roles
__**Free Role**__
⬥ 3 players have this role. One should have the boss aggro on them and keep it centered the whole kill without moving around. The free role's job is to do a burst damage rotation on Zamorak each time a new pad is charged to skip mechanics.

__**Witch Killer Role**__
⬥ 1 player has this role. Their job is to kill the witch in the Infernus everytime Zamorak's HP section is depleted as fast as possible.

__**Pad Charger Role**__
⬥ 1 player has this role. Their job is to charge the pads following the order 216453 or 214653 every time the witch dies to open the next phase.

*Note: both the witch killer and pad charger are required to deal damage to the boss at different times in between witch killing/pad charging.*

.
> __**Pre-Fight**__
.tag:prefight
⬥ Split in groups of 2/3 on each side, and kill the first witch on each side.
⬥ Don't kill the 2nd witch until everyone is in the instance, then <:nat:535541258131865633> on the 2nd witch and kill it slowly building to 100% with defensive autos.
⬥ <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> / <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> between the last 2 witches if free/witch, and a bit closer to pad2 if pad charger, then kill them with basics.

> __**Free Role**__
.tag:free
.
__**First Grey Bar**__
.
<:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> + (tc)<:smokecloud:856635090641879050> + <:tsunami:535533809995874304> → <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> → improvise with <:gconc:869285393223254107>, <:wm:535533809978966037>, <:asphyx:535533833072672778>, <:armadylbattlestaff:881962727705280512> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> until pad is 80% charged and stay over 60% adrenaline
.
__**First Edict **__
At 80% charge <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> → <:armadylbattlestaff:881962727705280512> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:armadylbattlestaff:881962727705280512> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:smoketendrils:536257336130404372> → improvise and keep 100% adren when grey bar is under 100k and witch dies (pad starts charging).

__**Second Edict**__
On pad charge starting or slightly before if predictable <:Berserk:513190158468907012> → <:divert:787904334377648130> → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → (0-2 basics depends on pad charge) → at 80% charge <:assault:535532853979512842> → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> → <:dragonclaw:779048041088024606> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:cane:535532878969438210> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977>(fully channel) → improvise to 100% and <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> + <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> as it comes off cooldown.

.
__**Third Edict**__
Timing is sometimes tight, <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> + <:tsunami:535533809995874304> asap and if good adren <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> on 80% pad charge, if not <:limitless:641339233638023179> + <:smoketendrils:536257336130404372> → <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200>, followed by a good fsoa rotation.

__**Fourth Edict**__
Depending on team and speed, there are 2 options:

**Option 1**
If you have about 12 seconds on <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> when pad starts charging,

<:deto:535533833358016512> → on 80% pad charge release <:bloodbarrage:537338981747261446> + <:deto:535533833358016512> + <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> → <:wm:535533809978966037> → <:asphyx:535533833072672778> → <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> or a similar rotation, depending on cooldowns and time left on <:gsunshine:994644352871714836>. The goal here is to deplete the green hp bar in <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> to skip mechanics.

Then <:Berserk:513190158468907012> and deplete the greybar + next greenbar in that <:Berserk:513190158468907012> rotation.

**Option 2**
On pad charge starting or slightly before if predictable <:Berserk:513190158468907012> → <:divert:787904334377648130> → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → (0-2 basics depends on pad charge) → at 80% charge <:assault:535532853979512842> → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> → <:dragonclaw:779048041088024606> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:cane:535532878969438210> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977>(fully channel) → improvise to 100% and <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> + <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> as it comes off cooldown.

.
__**Fifth and Sixth Edict**__
If Option 1 used on 4th edict, <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> after <:zerk:535532854004678657> is over, and do the grey bar + last phase in <:gsunshine:994644352871714836>.

If Option 2 used on the 4th edict, <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> at 100k grey bar. Your <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> will be on cooldown, so <:deto:535533833358016512> on edict charge and release with <:bloodbarrage:537338981747261446> + <:deto:535533833358016512> + <:wm:535533809978966037> → <:asphyx:535533833072672778> → <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> to clear the green bar quickly, then improvise and <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> as soon as it come off cooldown.

Depends on speed you can clear the 6th edict in that fsoa, if not, you can <:zerk:535532854004678657> for the 6th edict charge.

__**Extra notes for Free Role**__
⬥ Keep the boss <:Vuln:537349530551582720> and <:smokecloud:856635090641879050> at all times.
⬥ If you're on a <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714>, juice your <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787> and <:ripperscroll:703581275155464203> at all times. Rippers can offset the healing of grey bar when preparing for a <:zerk:535532854004678657>.
⬥ Beware that shadow traps can spawn on top of you on 5th edict charge. Using <:anti:535541306475151390> here and <:DeflectMage:544195487926845462> with high HP is advised to avoid the large magic hit and stun.

__**Extra notes for Witch Killers**__
⬥ Make sure to call your ripper demon and <:ripperscroll:703581275155464203> on Witch.
⬥ Make sure to pre <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> the witch.
⬥ Can bring a ring swap for better accuracy on the witch.
⬥ Killing the witch as fast as possible is essential for consistently fast kills and less error margins on the rotations of the rest of the team.
⬥ Adjust your rotations depend on team speed. You might need to do extra abilities before entering, or you might have to enter instantly on charge.

> __**Pad Charger Role (PC)**__
.tag:pc
.
__**First Grey Bar**__
On fast teams, charge pad 2 right after <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> on the boss.
Build with defensives on the pad and stall <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200>.

__**First Edict **__
<:bd:535532854281764884> on edict charge + release <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> + <:armadylbattlestaff:881962727705280512> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:armadylbattlestaff:881962727705280512> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:smoketendrils:536257336130404372> → <:wm:535533809978966037> → go to pad 1 at about 100k grey bar hp, or earlier depends on witch kill speed.

__**Second Edict**__
Build with defensives on pad if needed, <:Berserk:513190158468907012> at 80% pad charge → (mid gcd) <:surge:535533810004262912> + <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:deci:535532879325822986>→ <:assault:535532853979512842> → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> → <:cane:535532878969438210> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977> (all hits) → improvise and go to pad at 100k grey bar.

.
__**Third Edict**__
If you are able to pre-<:gsunshine:994644352871714836> before going to pad, do so.

Build with defensives/ <:divert:787904334377648130> on pad and on charge (if not pre-<:gsunshine:994644352871714836> then <:meta:535533811304497183>) → stall <:dbreath:535533833391702017> + <:bd:535532854281764884> and release with <:gconc:869285393223254107> → <:tsunami:535533809995874304> → <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> → <:soa:869284271595069451> <:spec:537340400273195028> (if off cooldown) → <:wm:535533809978966037> → improvise and get to pad at 100k hp left.

__**Fourth Edict**__
Build with defensives on pad if needed, <:Berserk:513190158468907012> at 80% pad charge → (mid gcd) <:surge:535533810004262912> + <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:deci:535532879325822986> → <:assault:535532853979512842> → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> → <:cane:535532878969438210> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977> (all hits) → improvise and go to pad at 100k grey bar.

.
__**Fifth and Sixth Edict**__
Repeat Third and Fourth Edict, dump extra hard on the last <:zerk:535532854004678657>.

__**Extra notes for Pad Chargers**__
⬥ Pad order: Starting from twinshot as 1, 216453 can also do 214653 but you want minions last and traps 2nd last preferably.
⬥ Depending on adren/relent you can <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> after <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> and before <:cane:535532878969438210> on melee phases.
⬥ <:anti:535541306475151390> + <:DeflectMage:544195487926845462> on last pad because trap can hurt and will stun you.
⬥ The most important thing is just adapting to your team. On slower teams you may need to stay back longer and do extra abilities, while on faster teams you might barely get anything off before next pad.
⬥ There is a bug where the pad won't charge if you step on it the same tick as the witch dies, so you need to step off and on.
