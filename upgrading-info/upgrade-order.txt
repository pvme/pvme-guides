> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
This channel lists the **general** natural combat style progression template for players to follow.
**Last Revised: 2022/01/18**

.
> **__Disclaimer__**
.tag:disclaimer
Priorities can of course change depending on the content being done by the player. You should note that when you upgrade weapons, you can sell the weapon being upgraded to deduct cost from the new weapon.

For example, once you are ready to obtain Blightbound Crossbows <:bbc:626714879218155521> <:bbcoh:626714879230738434>, you can sell one or both of your Ascensions <:ascmh:513190158468775936> <:ascoh:513190159362031631> for a good chunk of change. Keep in mind that the old off-hand Ascension Crossbow <:ascoh:513190159362031631>can be used as a Flanking <:flank4:712073088296157185> switch, so you may want to keep that specifically to Flank.

You should also feel free to buy items that are stated as less important in the upgrade order, such as buying Laceration Boots <:Laceration:602581988599398400> which costs <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Miscellaneous!B2$ before the Zaros Godsword <:zgs:626465964325601290>, which currently costs  <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Miscellaneous!B1$.

.
