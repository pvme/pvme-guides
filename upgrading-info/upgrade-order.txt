> __**General Upgrade Path**__
⬥ This channel lists the natural combat style progression template for players to follow
⬥ Priorities can differ based on content being done by the player. 
⬥ You should note that when you upgrade gear, you can sell away the inferior versions of what you are upgraded to help fund it, e.g, selling Ascension main-hand when upgrading to Blightbounds 
    • Keep in mind that the old off-hand ascension crossbow can be used as a flanking switch,
⬥ You should also feel free to also buy items that are ahead in the list, e.g., buying a Zaros Godsword before Lacerations should you be able to afford it, though if you are unsure if you should ask in #pvm-help 
⬥Feel free to ask in #pvm-help if you have any questions!
