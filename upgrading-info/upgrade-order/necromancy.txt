> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
This channel lists the **general** natural combat style progression template for players to follow.
**Last Revised: September 9th 2023**

.
> **__Disclaimer__**
.tag:disclaimer
Priorities can of course change depending on the content being done by the player. You should note that **__when you upgrade weapons, you can sell the weapon being upgraded__** to deduct cost from the new weapon.

If an item is not in the order __**it was deemed not worth buying**__.

As prices change, updates occur, and knowledge expands, **__the order is subject to change at any time.__**

.
**Feel free to ask in <#656898197561802760> for a tailored list for the content that you do!**

.
> **__Early Money Making__**
.tag:moneymaking
To make money to fund your first style early on, it is recommended to do either Croesus or easy clue scrolls.

> __**Necromancy Specific Upgrades**__
.tag:special
Necromancy hosts a few upgrade paths which are not purchaseable, but impact how fast you will progress.

__**Kili's Tasks**__
Upon reaching level 70 you have the option to choose your path between power and tank armour. Completing either branch allows you to create the weaponry.

⬥ For less experienced players it is suggested to take the tank armour path.
    • These tasks are easier albeit more time consuming.

It is **strongly advised to avoid upgrading armour too much** as it delays purchasing The First Necromancer equipment <:firstnecrorobetop:1142416986190655549> .

.
