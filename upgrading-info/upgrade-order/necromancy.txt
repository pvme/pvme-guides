## __Necromancy Specific Upgrades__
.tag:special
Necromancy hosts a few upgrade paths which are not purchaseable, but impact how fast you will progress.

.
### Kili's Tasks
Upon reaching level 70 you have the option to choose your path between power and tank armour. Completing either branch allows you to create the weaponry.

⬥ For less experienced players it is suggested to take the tank armour path.
    • These tasks are easier albeit more time consuming, and will make obtaining T90 weapons easier.

It is **strongly advised to avoid upgrading armour too much** as it delays purchasing The First Necromancer equipment <:firstnecrorobetop:1206664820682657822> .

.
### Nexuses
Nexuses are items used in Necromancy that can store ectoplasm <:ectoplasm:1151165582650122350> and necrotic runes <:bonerune:1138808701029982319> <:fleshrune:1138808695703207967> <:miasmarune:1138808692087730206> <:spiritrune:1138808705362710680>, similar to rune pouches for Magic. However, unlike the pouch, a nexus can hold up to a maximum stack (2,147,483,647) of each necrotic rune. They can be worn in the ammunition slot.

The unattuned deathwarden nexus <:deathwardennexus:1179716442816532540> is crafted using the soul forge within the City of Um in the tier 60 section, requiring an ensouled bar, a silver bar and a cut sapphire. Before the deathwarden nexus can be used to store ectoplasm and necrotic runes, it must be attuned at the dark portal located in the City of Um.

A superior nexus, Zemouregal's nexus <:zemouregalsnexus:1179716261681307728>, has an additional passive effect that increases the level of Bone Shield by 15 levels. It is obtained by killing 100 Vorkath.

.
