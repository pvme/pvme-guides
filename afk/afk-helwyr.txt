> **__AFK Melee Strategy (~51 kph)__ (Courtesy of <@!108682527304060928> and <@!683763716394975294>)**
.tag:afkmelee
**__Explanation__**
⬥ This method will net less kills per hour but may be used if you do not wish to put high amounts of effort into a mid level boss.
⬥ It is important to pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> as this method relies on constant healing.

.
__**Aura Choice**__
Aura choice can vary, but will affect how afk kills can be. They are listed in order of effectiveness:
⬥ <:vamp:643505653079343144> Vampyrism Aura provides extra safety through its healing and allows more chances for poison damage. Due to the constant damage being taken while fighting Helwyr, this is by far the safest and most reliable aura choice to afk with.
⬥ <:penance:643505653062565907> Penance Aura allows for the most afk experience, but is less safe to use and doesn't benefit the usage of a Blood Reaver familiar.
⬥ <:dm:643505652571963417> Dark Magic provides extra damage, but you must pay more attention to both your HP and your prayer points. This should be an effective aura for fast kills, but is the least afk choice.
⬥ <:mahj:643148943856762890> Mahjarrat Aura provides extra damage, but does not give any extra poison procs. Still, it is a viable option as a secondary aura.
⬥ <:berserker:643505116347105290> **Do __not__ use Berserker** for afking, as it will get you killed very easily.

> **__AFK Ranged Strategy (~56-58 kph)__ (Courtesy of <@145884917627224065>, <@275626039579901952> and <@502132499271385098>)**
.tag:afkrange

.
**__Explanation__**
⬥ This method will net less kills per hour but may be used if you do not wish to put high amounts of effort into a mid level boss.
⬥ It is important to pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> as this method relies on constant healing. It is also important to use the **Animate Dead** spell, as the method heavily relies on its usage.
⬥ A couple times per hour you will need to drop a Prism of Restoration <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> to ensure your reaver survives, when repotting check its hp.
⬥ Post Animate Dead changes this strategy is riskier, and Attuned portents of restoration <:attunedportentofrestorationx:1027212535121850400> need to be used. To reduce the number of <:attunedportentofrestorationx:1027212535121850400> used per hour, several steps can be taken. These include:
    • Damage reducing perks ( Absorbative 4 <:absorbative4:890542428556230666> and Crystal Shield 4 <:crystalshield4:921410343606034443> )
    • Using the Death's ward <:deathward:697808774068699286> relic.
    • Inreasing Blood Reaver autofire speed (increase Prisms of Restoration <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> casts over the hour accordingly).
    • Shifting debilitate <:debil:535541278264393729> forward in the revolution bar.
⬥ While this setup is mostly reliable for full hour sessions, you may need to bank for more portents due to bad mushroom spawns.
    • Check that you have <:attunedportentofrestorationx:1027212535121850400> when re-potting.

.
⬥ Aura choice can vary, but will affect how afk kills can be. They are listed in order of effectiveness:
    • <:vamp:643505653079343144> Vampyrism Aura provides extra safety through its healing and allows more chances for poison damage. Due to the constant damage being taken while fighting Helwyr, this is by far the safest and most reliable aura choice to afk with.
    • <:reckless:643505179378974748> **Do __NOT__ use Reckless** for afking, as it will get you killed very easily.

.
⬥ For fastest kill speeds, a Bow of the Last Guardian <:bolg:994189289623662702> should be used. A Seren Godbow <:sgb:626466665848242186> or Noxious Bow <:noxbow:513190159425208342> are slightly slower substitutes.
