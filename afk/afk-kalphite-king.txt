__**Explanation**__
⬥ Kills are an average of 1:30.
⬥ You will need to bank between kills to reset cooldowns, get full adrenaline, and restore <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> HP.
⬥ This method has a high startup cost and a moderately high upkeep cost.
⬥ You **MUST be on a Kalphite slayer task for this to work.**
⬥ Due to the nature of Kalphite King's special attacks, this method relies on starting with low damage and ramping up to deal with mechanics whilst also using a <:RoD:513190159462825984>

*__**Note: This method can result in death but has a very high success rate.**__*
