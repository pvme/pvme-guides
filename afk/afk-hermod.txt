> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
In order to fight Hermod you must complete [The Spirit of War](<https://runescape.wiki/w/The_Spirit_of_War>) quest.

Notable drops:
⬥ <:hermodicplate:1139533828629741668> **Hermodic plates**, which are used for the Necromancy power armour, dropped at a rate of 1/10.
⬥ <:drumsticks:1139533737617539235> **Animated drumsticks**, which are used for [That Old Black Magic](<https://runescape.wiki/w/That_Old_Black_Magic>) quest.

These are all highly recommended.
⬥ <:undeadslayerperk:689502804720615441> Undead Slayer Perk
⬥ <:salveamulet:797899945730244648> Salve Amulet (e)
⬥ <:rov:513201065877831680> Ring of Vigour passive upgrade
