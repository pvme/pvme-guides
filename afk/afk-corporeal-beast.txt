**__Explanation__**
This method will net a lower number of kills per hour than actively killing Corp but may be used for minimal effort. It is useful for title/pet hunting however there are a few prerequisites to sustain yourself. <:kineticcyclone:798285651405438997> is used over the <:oldakcoil:798285651387613194> as it heals you through <:VampScrim:513201294262009874>. 2 pieces of Vestments armour is used for the adrenaline refund passive effect. Use a legendary pet to pick up your loot.
