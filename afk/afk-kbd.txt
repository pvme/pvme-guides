## __AFK Melee Strategy (~120 KPH)__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
### Explanation
⬥ This method will allow for completely AFK 15 minutes at a time.
⬥ Kill times will between 4-5 seconds, based mostly on <:overpower:535532879334080517> cooldown.
⬥ You may use far lower gear, as shown in the example video, at the cost of kills per hour.

.
### Aura Choice
.tag:aura
⬥ Aura choice can vary, but will affect how afk kills can be. They are listed in order of effectiveness:
    • <:equilibriumaura:1233722649834684416> **Equilibrium**
    • <:berserker:643505116347105290> **Berserker**
    • <:mahj:643148943856762890> **Mahjarrat**
    • <:darkmagic:643505652571963417> **Dark Magic**
    • <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> **Penance** - May result in lost KPH

.
