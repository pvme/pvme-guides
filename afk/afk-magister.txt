.tag:positioning
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/C2MBKJpjDW.png
⬥ Enter instance and stand on the west tile against the Soul Obelisk.
⬥ Use <:keytothecrossing:1048592046409138217> to start the encounter.
__**Specifically for**__ <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>:
⬥ Use <:keytothecrossing:1048592046409138217> and <:escape:535541258832052231>
    • If Magister spawns adjacent to you, take **1 step** north.
⬥ After the kill use <:escape:535541258832052231> again. Repeat (See video for example).
*Note: the 3x3 area on the map shows the possible spawn tiles for the Magister.*
