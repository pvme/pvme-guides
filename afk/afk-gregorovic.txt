**__Introduction__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

⬥ The methods below will net a lower number of kills per hour than actively killing Gregorovic but may be used if you do not wish to put in high effort into farming a mid-level boss.
⬥ It is very effective for low-effort title/pet hunting. However, there are a few prerequisites to sustain yourself.
⬥ **Take note of the Mandatory AFK Requirements and do not cut corners as any lack of damage and/or healing can easily cause methods to fail.**

.
> **__AFK Melee Strategy (~41-54 kph) (Courtesy of <@!108682527304060928>, <@!683763716394975294>, and <@!218995247986769921>)__**
.tag:intromelee
.
__**Explanation**__
The AFK <:melee:1096130867279171706> strategy utilises the synergy between various bleeds <:dismember:535532879376023572> <:abyssalscourge:947871842469834832> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> <:slaughter:535532879237873666> <:massacre:535532879384543253> with healing such as <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> <:VampScrim:513201294262009874> to out sustain Greg's consistent damage, as well as dealing additional damage via <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive and <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> <:weppoison:689525476158472288> resulting in fast kills. A legendary pet can be used to automatically pick up all loot, but doing so will sacrifice 6-10 kph.

> **__AFK Ranged Strategy (60 kph) (Courtesy of <@!193847159697702912>)__**
.tag:introranged
.
__**Explanation**__
The AFK <:range:580168050121113623> strategy utilises the synergy between ranged's ability to generate many hitsplats via <:grico:787904334812807238> <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> <:rapid:535541270521708566> <:mds:535541259033378827> to deal massive amounts of additional damage with <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive and <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> <:weppoison:689525476158472288> resulting in the fastest AFK kills. This also generates enough healing through <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> to counter Gregorovic's sustained damage.
