## __Definitions__
.tag:Definitions
Within these guides there are two levels of AFK you will see mentioned:

**AFK**
⬥ These do not usually require any attention to the game
⬥ You may still need to interact infrequently, for example: clicking to prevent lobbying, drinking potions, using <:enhancedexcalibur:513200949016264727> or <:ancientshard:656426717505650708>, collecting loot, healing familiars with <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070>
    • Alt1 can usually be used to notify you of these

**Low Effort**
⬥ These usually do require you to pay attention to the game, and are instead used to heavily reduce inputs required to kill a boss
⬥ In addition to those listed above under AFK, example interactions include: queuing an ability before the fight, repositioning every kill, banking between kills, moving out of the way of mechanics, eating food at low health

.

## __Common Items__
.tag:CommonItems
You may also see the below items commonly used across AFK methods - these are **not** assumed by default:

⬥ [Spring Cleaner](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Spring_cleaner>) <:springcleaner:1130170723130937427> to automatically alch / disassemble drops
⬥ [Holy Wrench](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Holy_wrench>) <:holywrench:841409588922417192> to boost prayer restoration
⬥ [Potion Reservoir](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Potion_reservoir>) <:potionreservoir:878739200407654431> to automatically drink potions
⬥ [Enhanced Excalibur](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Enhanced_Excalibur>) <:enhancedexcalibur:513200949016264727> to provide free healing every 5 minutes
⬥ [Ancient Elven Ritual Shard](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Ancient_elven_ritual_shard>) <:ancientshard:656426717505650708> to provide free prayer points every 5 minutes

.
## __Top tips: Using Alt1's AFK Warden__
.tag:Alt1AFKWarden
To set up alt1 AFK Warden you can include several useful warnings.
⬥ HP Alert
⬥ Prayer Alert
⬥ Loot Beam
⬥ Inventory Full
⬥ Animate Dead
⬥ Overload
⬥ Weapon Poison
⬥ Summoning Familiar HP Alert
⬥ Kerapac Kill
⬥ Lobby timer

Depending on the boss you are doing, you can activate different ones of these, and set the HP threshold to a suitable level for the risk of your setup. Doing this will allow you to eat / excal to stop a death.

You can add all of these by clicking the save icon on alt1, and then importing the following text:
<https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pvme/pvme-guides/master/afk/PVME-Alt1-Setup.txt>
