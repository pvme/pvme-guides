__**Explanation and Notes**__
.tag:intro
Consistency and safety are attained via <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> damage and healing through specs, taking advantage of <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004>, and proper positioning of <:surge:535533810004262912> on the ability bar.

⬥ This is a **5 minute** AFK method due to needing to heal <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> with <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070>
    • Use an [Alt1 toolkit alarm](<https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pvme/pvme-guides/master/afk/PVME-Alt1-Setup.txt>) to monitor <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> HP.

⬥ Slayer task (with Slayer Helm <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> on your Anachronia slayer lodge stand) is only required for lower level gear setups.
    •  A **T88+** bow is recommended for the method to take advantage of Pthen's weakness.
    •  <:bolg:994189289623662702> is the best in slot weapon for this method.
        ⬩ This reaches **49 KPH**.
    • Use **Splintering Arrows** <:splinteringarrows:900758234246299649>
    • Other viable weapons (non-bow) (**These will require both <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> and <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004> accuracy boosts**):
        ⬩ <:ascmh:513190158468775936> <:bbc:626714879218155521> **Dual-wield Crossbows** - Ascension or better works. Use **Emerald or Onyx Bakriminel Bolts (e)** <:emeraldbakri:866551637714665484> <:OnyxBakri:565726489362956308>

⬥ <:vamp:643505653079343144> is the most reliable aura as it helps with healing and deals extra poison damage through the <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive.
⬥ <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> can be used instead of <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>, but will require increasing familiar autofire rate to 8-12 depending on gear.
    • May not be feasible for non-BIS gear.
.
⬥ If using <:Desolation:513190159018098713>, you may need <:ancientshard:656426717505650708> to upkeep Prayer.
⬥ <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> is used to heal your <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>. If it dies, summon another and load it with scrolls.
