__**Explanation and Notes**__
.tag:intro
Consistency and safety are attained via <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> damage and healing through specs, taking advantage of <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004>, and proper postion of <:surge:535533810004262912> on the ability bar.

⬥ Slayer task (with Slayer Helm on your Anachronia slayer lodge stand) is only required for lower level gear setups.
    •  A T88+ bow is recommended for the method to take advantage of Pthen's weakness.
    •  <:bolg:994189289623662702> is the best in slot weapon for this method.
        ⬩ This reaches **49 KPH**.
    • Use **Splintering Arrows** <:splinteringarrows:900758234246299649>
    • Other viable weapons (non-bow) (**IMPORTANT NOTE:** These will require both Slayer task and <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004> accuracy boosts):
        ⬩ <:ascmh:513190158468775936> <:bbc:626714879218155521> **Dual-wield Crossbows** - Ascension or better works. Use **Emerald or Onyx Bakriminel Bolts (e)** <:emeraldbakri:866551637714665484> <:OnyxBakri:565726489362956308>.

⬥ <:vamp:643505653079343144> provides safety through healing and extra poison damage through the <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive.
⬥ <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> can be used instead of <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>, but will require increasing familiar autofire rate to 8-12 depending on gear. Low level gear may not be able to use penance at all and will require vampirism aura.
.
⬥ If using <:Desolation:513190159018098713>, you may need <:ancientshard:656426717505650708> to upkeep Prayer.
⬥ <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> is used to heal your <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>. If it dies, summon another and load it with scrolls.
⬥ To pick up loot, use <:SBSStandards:565726489526534154> <:telekeneticgrab:866551637970255872> to not move from your position.
    • Moving can cause you to get killed on the next Overcharge.

**__Method and Setup__**
.tag:pos

⬥ Ensure all buffs/prayers <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532> <:weppoison:689525476158472288> <:kwuarmsticks:565726489341984779> <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> <:scriptureofwen:883134307902816297> <:desolation:513190159018098713> <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> <:vampaura:643505653079343144> are active, along with <:autoretaliate:1084799717726228530>
