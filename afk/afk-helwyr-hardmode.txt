**__Positioning__**
.tag:positioning
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/wU3zbtwBmj.png
**__Notes__**
⬥ **__Pink "Other" Tiles__ - Mushroom buds that you should only stand directly on top of are marked pink above.** This is because some adjacent tiles have an increased chance of death due to the possibility of getting hit by multiple Mushroom Barrage attacks at once.
⬥ Position yourself on the tile shown above (the adjacent tile north of the mushroom bud) and avoid gas clouds when they spawn by moving to any safe tile if necessary. Once you are safe from the first set of gas clouds you do not need to move again for the rest of the fight.
⬥ A quick and easy way to remember which tile is safe is to never stand 2 tiles from mushroom buds, but on top of or directly adjacent to mushroom buds as they are normally safe.

**__The fight__**
.tag:fight
⬥ This method requires teleporting to War's Retreat after every kill to replenish prayer, adrenaline, familiar and player health, and reset cooldowns.
⬥ Cross the barrier to enter the boss room → <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> → <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:commandghost:1159434409913634816> → (tc) <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> → <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> +  <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> + Activate <:enhancedexcalibur:513200949016264727>
⬥ Turning Auto-Retaliate <:autoretaliate:1084799717726228530> on is recommended. If you have Auto-Retaliate <:autoretaliate:1084799717726228530> off you will need to manually use soul sap at the beginning to start revolution.
⬥ It is not recommended to click on Helwyr to begin attacking due to the delay compared to target cycle. There is a small window to kill the boss before you are overwhelmed by the Mushroom Barrage.
