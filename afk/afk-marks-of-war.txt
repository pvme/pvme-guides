> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Marks of War <:marksofwar:704701746869567551> are an essential resource needed to PvM effectively, as they allow you to reset your auras. Auras provide various benefits which range from increasing your damage output, to increasing your survivability. Obtaining Marks of War <:marksofwar:704701746869567551> is time-gated, as you're limited to 1000 marks per hour. This guide focuses on the fastest AFK method to reach this cap. For convinience, this method does not require the usage of auras.

⬥ Commonly used auras in PvM include: [Berserk](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Berserker_aura>) <:berserker:643505116347105290>, [Maniacal](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Maniacal_aura>) <:mani:643505153709965322>, [Reckless](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Reckless_aura>) <:reckless:643505179378974748>, [Penance](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Penance_aura>) <:penanceaura:643505653062565907>, and [Vampyrism](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Vampyrism_aura>) <:vampaura:643505653079343144>
⬥ In order to reach the cap of Marks of War <:marksofwar:704701746869567551>, 5 normal mode Kerapac kills are required; with each kill giving 200 marks
    • This takes around 13 minutes, 1:55 per kill + ~30s respawn
⬥ You can opt to not loot, and only click to prevent yourself from lobbying
