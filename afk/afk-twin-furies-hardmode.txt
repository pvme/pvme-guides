### The fight
.tag:fight
⬥ This method requires teleporting to War's Retreat after every kill to replenish prayer, adrenaline, familiar and player health, and reset cooldowns.
⬥ Outside of the instance <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> → Rejoin instance → Enter boss room + <:commandghost:1159434409913634816> → <:surge:535533810004262912> or <:dive:1049378668197195808> north → wait for boss timer to appear → (tc) <:deathskullsigneous:1179505729367969863>
⬥ If you are not using the complete First Necromancer's robe set then use <:lifetransfer:1137809128136388819> after <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348>
⬥ Your target cycle should be targeting Nymora at the beginning of the fight, not Avaryss. This is because attacking Avaryss while she is dashing will cause her to reflect 90% of the damage dealt. If you target cycle onto Avaryss you are doing it too late and should adjust accordingly.
⬥ On the 4th and final push from Avaryss (from the North East corner to the North West corner) switch targets and attack Avaryss for the rest of the fight + activate <:enhancedexcalibur:513200949016264727> + optionally <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858>
⬥ It is not recommended to click on Nymora to start attacking. Due to the delay compared to target cycling, if you click to start the fight, your Death Skulls will lose bounces before Avaryss begins to dash around the arena.
