> **__Ring of Death benefits__**
**TL;DR: The reduced death cost only becomes relevant when your risk of death is high, and your regular death cost is more expensive than <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Consumables!E9$**

**__How it works__**
Recharged by 50% per Onyx used, the Ring of Death <:RoD:513190159462825984> consumes 15% (<:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Consumables!E9$) of its charge upon death for the following benefits:
⬥ You are sent to your last Respawn Point without having to visit Death's Office.
⬥ Familiar and inventory/scrolls are kept on death.
⬥ You can overcharge the Ring of Death to have 5x the capacity for the total cost of <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Miscellaneous!H8$ using:
    • 5 Onyxes
    • 25 Black Stone hearts
    • 150 Reaper points
⬥ Can be upgraded for 15,000 Teci <:teci:641332337451204608> by speaking with the Armoursmith at Mazcab, increasing its damage bonuses by 2.5

More in-depth information in: <https://runescape.wiki/w/Ring_of_Death>
