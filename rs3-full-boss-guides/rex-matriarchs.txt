.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/k1pBa3wBhP.png

## __Introduction__
The Rex Matriarchs are a group of 4 dinosaur bosses found on Anachronia, representing one for each combat style. Their rare drops include:

⬥ Components for best-in-slot rings <:reaverring:839903943018283050> <:stalkerring:839903943601029120> <:championring:839903943630520350> <:channellerring:839903943404027914> <:occulistring:1245442140318863490>
⬥ Spear parts to craft [Laniakea's Spear](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Laniakea%27s_spear>) <:lanispear:839903893177106454>
⬥ [Jail cell key](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Jail_cell_key>) used to unlock one of three baby dinosaur pets

.

## __Active Strategies__
.tag:methods
There are two ways to take down the Matriarchs:

1. Repeatedly killing a single Matriarch
2. Killing 3 one-after-another with a hybrid gear setup (Orikalka, Rathis and Pthentraken)

.

⬥ Camp <:incitefear:856635090783567902> until <:tsunami:535533809995874304> then switch to <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258>
    • Remember to swap back to autocasting <:incitefear:856635090783567902> before starting the next Orikalka kill.
    • Use basics to build when low adren
    • Use <:wrackandruin:1257439387474722836> whenever available

⬥ Use <:fularrow:971025696958853180> when <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> on cooldown.
⬥ Use <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> proc on any of these: <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> <:incend:535541258429661215> <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386>
    • Rotations will vary based on <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> stacks
    • Swap to <:fularrow:971025696958853180> after consuming <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> stacks
⬥ Remember to incorporate <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> and <:snap:535534127131394088> <:rapid:535541270521708566> into your rotation

⬥ Alternate kills between EZK <:zekkil:903244090953588787> and Berserk <:zerk:535532854004678657> rotations:
    • Kill 1 rotation:  <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:chaosroar:994644356671737966> → <:zekkil:903244090953588787> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> → (<:limitless:641339233638023179>) bleed <:destroy:535532879330148352> → <:assault:535532855191928842> → <:cane:535532878969438210>

        ⁃ Remember to re-equip <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004> after <:zekkil:903244090953588787> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099>
