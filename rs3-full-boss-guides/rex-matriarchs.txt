> **__Introduction__**
The Rex Matriarchs are a trio of dinosaur bosses found on Anachronia. They drop components required to make the <:reaverring:839903943018283050>, <:stalkerring:839903943601029120>, <:championring:839903943630520350> and <:channellerring:839903943404027914> rings. Additionally, they also drop the 4 spear parts used to craft <:lanispear:839903893177106454>, a halberd-range stab weapon with T90 damage, T75 accuracy and a passive that increases weapon poison frequency and damage by 5%.

.
**__General Information__**
The Rex Matriarchs have high <:Defence:689509250979987525> levels of 96, making them usually not worthwhile doing for mid-level players. Thus, this guide will be focused on high level strategies for end game players farming logs.

⬥ Mid-level players trying out matriarchs for Reaper tasks are encouraged to come up with their own strategies after going through mechanics or refer to beginner friendly youtube guides.
    • Rex Matriarchs with mid level weapons and T70 armour by <@!135502088049393664> - <https://youtu.be/FqLB0iwkUYk>
⬥ While <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> bonuses are applicable to them, enhanced <:Tuskas:513201065513058306> is not.
⬥ It is recommended getting <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004> before going for the matriarch logs for a huge accuracy and damage boost of 15%.

**__Hybrid Rotations__**
Below are suggested rotations based on what was used in the example videos. Basics usage are mostly omitted.

⬥ Due to the number of variables present feel free to come up with your own rotations based on your gear.
    • You will likely still be able to get ~85 KPH or more even if not bridding optimally.
⬥ The <:Magic:689504724159823906> rotations for Mage-Ranged and Mage-Melee are usable for both methods, although the naked <:fsoa:869284271595069451> rotation generally works better to start Rathis/Pthen on 100% adren.
⬥ Use <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> on <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> to use your desired ability for <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> if killing Pthentraken.
    • For Rathis/Ori, use it to reach 100% for <:zerk:535532854004678657> if ending Orikalka on low adren.
