### <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> __Rotation__
⬥ This rotation requires <:igneouskalmor:1139200072438005851>. If you don't have one, remove <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> from the rotation and use <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> and <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> where possible.
⬥ Darkness <:darkness:1137809209782698024> is optional to reduce damage taken.
⬥ This rotation consistently hits sub-25-second kills and seeks to hit sub-15-seconds. It requires teleporting to Wars Retreat between kills for adren.
    • If using T95 gear, you will be consistently hitting sub-20-second kills and may wish to add in a <:omniguard:1138809234922934282> <:spec:537340400273195028> before your t90 special.
