### <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> __Rotation__
⬥ You may <:lifetransfer:1137809128136388819> twice per set of conjures.
⬥ If your <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> hits during Vindicta's phase transition it will deal significantly less damage.
    • You may opt to <:commandzombie:1137809191231295549> before it at the cost of higher <:ectoplasm:1151165582650122350> usage.
    • Alternatively adjust your <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> timing.
⬥ Darkness <:darkness:1137809209782698024> is optional to reduce damage taken.
⬥ This rotation consistently hits 15-16 second kills. It requires teleporting to Wars Retreat between kills for adrenaline.

.
