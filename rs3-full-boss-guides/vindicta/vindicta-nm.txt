## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Vindicta, the Zarosian general residing in the Heart of Gielinor, requires 80 attack (boostable) and 40 Zaros killcount to fight. Her drops include the t85 Dragon Rider Lance <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> which disassembles for Ilujankan components <:Ilujankancomp:513190158674427906> needed for the Aftershock <:as4:712074245202772009> perk, and the Crest of Zaros <:crestzaros:643161512177041428> which is used to make the t80 Anima Core of Zaros set <:animahelmzaros:643120081790042163>

### Rotation
⬥ This rotation requires <:igneouskalmor:1139200072438005851>. If you don't have one, remove <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> from the rotation and use <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> and <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> where possible.
⬥ This rotation consistently hits sub-25-second kills and seeks to hit sub-15-seconds. It requires teleporting to Wars Retreat between kills for adren.
⬥ If using T95 gear, you will be consistently hitting sub-20-second kills and may wish to add in a <:omniguard:1138809234922934282><:spec:537340400273195028> before your t90 special.

**Outside instance**
<:conjureskeleton:1159434516658651147> → <:conjurezombie:1137809200219684924> → <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> → <:kalgscroll:841409588954923049> → <:commandskeleton:1137809194423160883> → <:commandghost:1159434409913634816> + right-click 'rejoin'

**Inside instance**
<:splitsoul:1137809168368148490> + right-click 'quick enter' → <:bd:535532854281764884>+<:surge:535533810004262912> to Vindicta spawn point (this will bring your conjures inside) → <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548>

**Main fight**
(tc) + <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> + <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:deathguard70:1138809262194294864><:spec:537340400273195028> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> → <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> → improvise → as Vindicta dies equip <:excal:641337999170207763>
