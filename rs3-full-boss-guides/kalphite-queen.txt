## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

As a very old boss, Kalphite Queen is relatively unrewarding and relatively simple. Typically only done for the pet or collection log, there is no further reason for a high level account to do this boss. The only notable mechanic is her form change. She will spawn into the instance immune to <:range:580168050121113623> and <:Magic:689504724159823906>. After the first 40k damage is dealt, she will change forms to her flying form, which is immune to <:melee:1096130867279171706>. More information available here: <https://runescape.wiki/w/Kalphite_Queen>

If doing the boss on a low level account for a Reaper task, bring your best <:melee:1096130867279171706> and <:range:580168050121113623> / <:Magic:689504724159823906> equipment, along with some food and poison protection.

If farming the boss for log on a high level account, you can do it actively for **88+ KPH** or AFK for **40-45 KPH**.

## __Active Strategy__
.tag:active
⬥ You will be instance hopping between the uninstanced encounter and an instanced encounter.
    • This method will work better if you have a friend in the instance to eliminate the wait for the initial spawn.

⬥ Uninstanced encounter rotation:
    • **First form:** <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:assault:535532855191928842> → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> → <:chaosroar:994644356671737966> / <:deci:535532879325822986> → <:destroy:535532879330148352>
        ⬩ Use basics <:dismember:535532879376023572> <:sever:535532879577612298> in between if entering with low adrenaline.
    • Waiting for second form spawn: Use basic on minion → Swap to <:sirenicmask:643846959454617610> <:ecb:615618531937222657> + <:grico:787904334812807238> minion → <:mds:535541259033378827> / <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:spec:537340400273195028> if 100% adrenaline → <:voke:535541259465392143> minion
    • **Second form:** Auto + <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> → <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:snap:535534127131394088> and/or <:mds:535541259033378827>
        ⬩ Immediately climb out once KQ HP hits 0. KC and loot in the uninstanced encounter will register even if you are not in it.
    • (tc) + <:Tuskas:513201065513058306> / <:piercing:535541258538450944> minion + rejoin instanced encounter

.
⬥ Instanced encounter rotation:
    • Before spawn (if entering on empty instance): <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:freedom:535541258240786434> → <:bd:535532854281764884> for adren if needed.
        ⬩ (tc) just after <:anti:535541306475151390> runs out.
    • The first form and second form rotations are the same as above.
    • Upon KQ death, do your <:melee:1096130867279171706> switch and (tc) + <:voke:535541259465392143> before climbing out if you're the only one in the encounter.
        ⬩ **Climbing out immediately and leaving the instance empty before KQ finishes the dying animation will cause the kill to not count and no loot will register.** This is avoided if you have a friend in the instance.
    • If missing pet, wait for the loot to appear first before climbing up.
    • (tc) + <:anti:535541306475151390> → join uninstanced encounter

⬥ Sip <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> whenever needed.
