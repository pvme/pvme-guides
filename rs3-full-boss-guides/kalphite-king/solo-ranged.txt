## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
This guide assumes you are already familiar with the basic mechanics of Kalphite King, which can be found in <#1350536914289168427>

Being on a Kalphite Slayer task <:slayer:797896049548066857> for slayer helm <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> and Genocidal <:genocidal:689503091539705870> is **completely optional**, but will result in slightly faster kills.

This guide assumes no green shields happen, but they are very rare and generally won't ruin kills.

.
### Preset and Relics
.tag:preset
⬥ [Ranged](https://presets.pvme.io?id=f3c53c1f-0fcf-42b8-a329-8e8e20b2898b) <:range:580168050121113623>

.
**Notes**
⬥ <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714> set the autofire rate to 1.
⬥ <:elitesirenicbody:643846908305211413> has <:eq4:712073088589627505> for consistency.
⬥ <:sirenicbody:643846948570267648> switch has <:ch4:712073087864012840> to prevent stuns for almost the entire kill.
⬥ <:kwuarmsticks:565726489341984779> and <:spiritsticks:565726489136463894> increase damage to help ensure the rotation ends consistently at 13.2-15.6 seconds.
⬥ Toss the <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> 1-2 ticks before clicking on Kalphite King, otherwise Melee phase might null your click.
⬥ <:as4:712074245202772009> is better than <:caroming4:791281588792590336> on <:bolg:994189289623662702>.

### Rotation
.tag:rot1
**Pre-build:**
<:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> → Enter portal
Enter instance + drop <:dummy:656844963522281473> → <:imbueshadows:1478061866193518744> → <:wenarrows:971025697046925362> <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> → <:piercingshot:1478061863723073636> → <:rangedbasic:1478061858622537809> → <:piercingshot:1478061863723073636> → <:anticlearheaded:867678154071998464> → <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> → s<:grico:787904334812807238> (at 2 <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> stacks)

**In the fight:**
r<:grico:787904334812807238> + <:gloomfirebow:1446178203608809472> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> → <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> → <:deadshot:1478061860371828929> → <:gloomfirebow:1446178203608809472> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> → <:snapshot:1478061855732666439> → <:snapshot:1478061855732666439>

If you don't kill after the last <:snapshot:1478061855732666439>, finish with <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> <:piercingshot:1478061863723073636> and improvise.

If you get Melee phase spawn, <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> after <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> and using <:gloomfirebow:1446178203608809472> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> over the first <:snapshot:1478061855732666439> is an option to help ensure a 13.2 second kill.

**Post-fight building:**
Target kalphite marauder + <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> <:piercingshot:1478061863723073636> → <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> <:piercingshot:1478061863723073636>

These deathspores ensure you can both skip gaining adrenaline at crystal for <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> and avoid the <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> during the pre-build, saving both banking time and supplies.
