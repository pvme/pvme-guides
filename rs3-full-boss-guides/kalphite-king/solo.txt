## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
This guide assumes you are already familiar with the basic mechanics of Kalphite King, which can be found in <#1350536914289168427>

Being on a Kalphite slayer task <:slayer:797896049548066857> is recommended as you gain the benefits of the Slayer Helm <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> and Genocidal <:genocidal:689503091539705870>

This guide assumes no Green Shields happen, it's recommended to teleport out if one happens early into the fight
