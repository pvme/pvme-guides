__Arrow swaps note:__
⬥ Arrows shown before an ability are used on cast, and arrows shown after an ability are equipped same tick as cast.  This allows in getting both arrow effects on the same ability

### Prefight
.tag:pre
At War's Retreat:
Touch crystal → click <:dummy:656844963522281473> + s <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> → <:enhreplen:634350450887622656> + touch crystal

After entering the instance:
tc <:dummy:656844963522281473> r <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> + <:nat:535541258131865633> → <:wenarrow:971025697046925362> <:piercing:535541258538450944> → <:gricocaroming:867678153966878740> → <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> → <:wenarrow:971025697046925362> <:piercing:535541258538450944>
_Notes:
⬥ You will need to use the east crystal and stall meteor on the closest dummy
⬥ If you are not last inside the instance, wait until everyone is in to release your stall. Otherwise, drop your dummy and release your stall as soon as possible
⬥ It is possible to stall infinitely by using <:dive:1049378668197195808> and <:invokelordofbones:1176968330582700174> / <:darkness:1137809209782698024>
⬥ If time allows after your 2nd <:piercing:535541258538450944>, you may try to get more <:wenarrow:971025697046925362> stacks
⬥ Start with 0 <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> stacks and <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> active_
