> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Zemouregal & Vorkath is a boss encounter consisting of two bosses; Zemouregal and Vorkath. To access this encounter, the players must progress through the [Battle of Forinthry](https://runescape.wiki/w/Battle_of_Forinthry). Completion of the quest unlocks the normal mode and hardmode encounters.

.
**__Applicable Damage boosts for Vorkath__**
⬥ <:undeadslayer:641339922019516416> and <:undeadslayerperk:689502804720615441> work (<:dragonslayer:641339921814126594> and <:dragonslayerperk:689502927731163170> do **not** at time of writing)
⬥ <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792>boost and <:genocidal:689503091539705870> work while on a dragons cluster, blue dragons or undead cluster slayer task.
⬥  <:salveamulet:797899945730244648> works
⬥ Jas dragonbane arrows <:jasdragonbanearrow:971025697042755584> work

.
**Lifepoint scaling**
⬥ Vorkath: 750k <:constitution:689509250887712902> per player (double in Hardmode)
⬥ Zemouregal: 250k <:constitution:689509250887712902> per player (double in Hardmode)

**__Normal Mode specifics__**
⬥ Every place of power is protected by an undead giant, kill it to disrupt the beam.
⬥ Disrupting the places of power disables some of Zemouregal's abilities during the encounter, or starts the encounter.
    • Northern location: minion spawns.
    • Northwestern location: Zemouregal's Spikes.
    • Western location: Starts the encounter.

> **__The Fight__**
.tag:fight
**Phase 1 (Vorkath):**
4 autos → Dragonfire Breath → 4 autos → Zonal ice Breath (+ Bone Spikes and <:soulstrike:1137809142376058910>) *Repeat*

**Phase 1 (Zemouregal):**
7 autos (+ minions) → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → 1 auto (+ Bone Spikes) → <:soulstrike:1137809142376058910> *repeat*

**Phase 2 (Vorkath):
**4 autos → Dragonfire Breath → 4 autos → Zonal ice Breath (+ Bone Spikes and <:soulstrike:1137809142376058910>) → 4 autos → green poison pools *Repeat*

**Phase 2 (Zemouregal):**
7 autos (+ minions) → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → 1 auto (+ Bone Spikes) → <:soulstrike:1137809142376058910> *repeat*

**Phase 3 (Vorkath):**
2 autos (**not** repeated) →
Fly → 1 auto (+ Bone Spikes) → Dragonfire Breath → 4 autos → Zonal Ice Breath (+ Bone Spikes) → 4 autos → purple poison pools → 4 autos → Dragonfire Breath (+ Bone Spikes) → 4 autos → Zonal Ice Breath → 4 autos → purple poison pools (+ Bone Spikes) → 4 autos *Repeat*

**Phase 3 (Zemouregal):**

8 autos (+ minions) → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> #1 (+ Bone Spikes) → 6 autos (+ minions) → <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> → 1 auto → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> #2 → 1 auto (+ Bone Spikes) → <:bloodsiphon:1159434279311380532> (+ minions) → 4 autos → auto + <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> → 1 auto (+ Bone Spikes) → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> #3 → 2 autos (+minions) → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> *Repeat*

.
*Notes:
⬥ If Zemouregal kept a soul from P2: <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> #3 is skipped, going <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> → 3 autos → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> instead.
⬥ Minions and Bone Spikes are time-based and **will** desync with repeats, hence the brackets!*
