## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
When Nex spawns for the first time in an instance she will be praying <:DeflectMage:544195487926845462> for the majority of the kill, this is why Ranged only kills are the meta for fast consistent kills. After every kill you will teleport out of the instance and re-enter in order to force Nex to always be praying <:DeflectMage:544195487926845462>

*Note: it is strongly advised to complete the **Ritual of the Mahjarrat** quest in order to get access to the Stone of Jas <:jasboost:1006121672790773770> boost, which gives a +5% damage boost in GWD1 (including Nex). This can be done via Fairy ring code D.K.Q.*

.
