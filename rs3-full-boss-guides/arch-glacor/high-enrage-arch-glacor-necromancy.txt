## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

This guide is aimed at players looking to kill Arch-Glacor at **3500%-4000%** Enrage and assumes strong underlying knowledge of all information presented in <#1034830043114909766>.

This guide will also work at **0%-3500%**, however should be adjusted as required.

The Arch-Glacor is **not** affected by Slayer tasks in hard mode.

### Preset Notes

⬥ Depending on the enrage and personal preference, the following Auras can be used:
    • Aegis Aura <:aegis:1204128299241177148> (Blocks 10% of all incoming damage)
    • Vampyrism Aura <:vampaura:643505653079343144> (Gain life points equal to 5% damage dealt, capping at 50 life points per hit. This provides increased damage through Blood Reaver <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> attacks applying poison)
    • Mahjarrat Aura <:mahj:643148943856762890> (Increases damage dealt by 5%)
    • Supreme Invigorate Aura <:invigaura:643505652865695767> (10% additional adrenaline will be maintained after using an ultimate)

⬥ If using power armour, you can spirit shield flick to help manage auto attack damage, although this is not required.

⬥ <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> is generally considered the best familiar for all high-enrage strategies due to the strong healing over an extended fight.
    • Ensure <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> is used to keep Reaver alive.

.
⬥ Darkness <:darkness:1137809209782698024> should be active at all times, but please note that this can result in dodging attacks during <:res:535541258844635148>.

⬥ Penance Powder <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>, Kwuarm Incense Sticks <:kwuarmsticks:565726489341984779>, Lantadyme Incense Sticks <:lantasticks:565726489404899368>, and Spirit Weed Incense Sticks <:spiritsticks:565726489136463894> should be used before the fight.

.
### General Rotations
.tag:strategy
⬥ Rotations for Arch-Glacor are not strict, as per the random nature of the boss's mechanics. In general, aim to use <:bloat:1159433682403201044> and <:omniguard:1138809234922934282> <:spec:537340400273195028> as often as possible.

⬥ Living Death <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> rotations can be used where comfortable, such as during the Flurry mechanic.

⬥ Ensure that some Necrosis Stacks <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> or Residual Souls <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> are held for the Exposed Core or Glacyte Minions special attacks.

⬥ It is best to start the fight with <:nat:535541258131865633> in order to be sufficiently prepared for Exposed Core (arms) as the first mechanic.

.
### Clearing Exposed Core (Arms)
⬥ Before the Exposed Core mechanic, ensure that either Necrosis Stacks <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> or Residual Souls <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> are held to quickly kill the arm.
⬥ Ideally have your Conjures <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> alive and Ghost pre-commanded <:commandghost:1159434409913634816>.
⬥ Use <:limitless:641339233638023179> where required depending on adrenaline in order to use <:devo:513190158728953857> if the magic damage is scaling up.
⬥ If you need to use <:cade:535541306353778689> and your adren situation is dire (such as on back to back Exposed Core mechanics at high enrage where <:devo:513190158728953857> will be on cooldown), use <:divert:787904334377648130> and flick to <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> for 1 tick.

.

### Clearing Glacyte Minions

⬥ 3+ Residual Souls <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046>
    • <:debil:535541278264393729> Arch-Glacor → <:bloat:1159433682403201044> Bolstered Glacyte → <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642>

⬥ 0-2 Residual Souls <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046>
    • <:debil:535541278264393729> Arch-Glacor → <:bloat:1159433682403201044> Bolstered Glacyte → <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> → <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>

⬥ In either scenario, if the Glacyte Minions are still alive after the rotation, use:
    • (High HP) <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892> → <:spectralscythe2:1137809149690916945> → <:spectralscythe3:1137809152043925505>
    • (Low HP) <:anti:535541306475151390> → fillers to proc Death Mark

*Note: <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> flick on volley as required for healing.*
.
### Defensive Usage
⬥ At higher enrage, you will want to prioritize defensives as follows,
    • **Frost Cannon**: <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:DeflectMage:544195487926845462> <:reflect:535541258786177064> → <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> <:res:535541258844635148>
    • **Exposed Core** (arms): <:devo:513190158728953857> → <:divert:787904334377648130> → <:cade:535541306353778689> as needed
    • **Glacyte Minions**: <:debil:535541278264393729> (and, rarely, <:anti:535541306475151390>/<:freedom:535541258240786434> to block or clear stun)
    • **Flurry**: <:vitality:654618235097972737> / <:disrupt:535614336207552523> / <:res:535541258844635148> (unless cannon imminent)
    • **Pillars of Ice**: <:prep:535541258546970624> / <:voke:535541259465392143> for adrenaline
