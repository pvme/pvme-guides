## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

This guide is aimed at players looking to kill Arch-Glacor at **2500%-4000%** Enrage and assumes strong underlying knowledge of all information presented in <#1251054659750330518>.

This guide will also work from **0%**, however should be adjusted as required.

The Arch-Glacor is **not** affected by Slayer tasks in hard mode.

### General Rotations
.tag:strategy
⬥ Rotations for Arch-Glacor are not strict, as per the random nature of the boss's mechanics. In general, aim to upkeep <:bloat:1159433682403201044> & <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> as much as possible.

⬥ <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> , <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> and <:divert:787904334377648130> , are all high value abilities with medium-length cooldowns. Aim to **put these abilities on cooldown before the pillars of ice mechanic** blocks your line of sight. This keeps their cooldown ticking down, while you're using defensives to build adren during the mechanic.

⬥ Living Death <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> is best used during the flurry mechanic. This mechanic gives you a long, uninterrupted window in which you can deal damage to Arch-Glacor. It can situationally be used to reset <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> cooldown in **anticipation of an upcoming exposed core mechanic**. <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> shouldn't be strategically reserved for arms, as the time it takes to clear the mechanic doesn't largely change with or without the ult active.

⬥ Ensure that some Necrosis Stacks <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> (4+) and Residual Souls <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> (2+) are held for the Exposed Core.

⬥ It is best to start the fight with <:bloat:1159433682403201044> , as it is a low adren investment that has no cooldown, and has high use priority during the fight. There is a 2/5 chance you get a mechanic (minions/exposed core) that will nullify your first attack. Therefore it is important to use a low-investment cooldown/ability before knowing what the upcoming mechanic will be.
.
⬥ Both tank gear and TFN should be bound for swapping.

    • **TFN gear should be used in the following conditions**;
        ⬩ Exposed core mechanic is active.
        ⬩ Pillars of ice mechanic is active.
        ⬩ <:disrupt:535614336207552523> / <:devo:513190158728953857> / <:cade:535541306353778689> is active.
        ⬩ Equipped before casting <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> .
        ⬩ Arch glacor is not casting any auto attacks, and you are at or below your max HP in DPS gear (~11,100).
        ⬩ Do not spam swapping between TFN and tank gear between every ability/AG auto, as this will frequently fluctuate your max HP, causing your reaver to heal you above 11K HP, however swapping back to DPS gear will nullify the reaver's healing effect.

    • In all other situations, **tank gear** should always be worn in cases when you are taking damage.

⬥ It is important to note that **ranged autos during flurry**, will deal the most damage, as 3pc cryptbloom buff doesn't negate ranged damage. On average, the 3pc cryptbloom provides superior damage reduction over 4pc deathwarden.
.
### Exposed Core (Arms)
⬥ Before the Exposed Core mechanic, ensure that either Necrosis Stacks <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> or Residual Souls <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> are built.
⬥ Have your conjures <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> active and Ghost commanded <:commandghost:1159434409913634816>.
    • In some cases, it may be wise to equip <:excal:641337999170207763> excalibur to dismiss conjures, and re-conjure <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> to refresh their timer before the next mechanic, if you are anticipating arms coming soon in the spec order. It is essential that conjures have a long enough timer to be **active throughout the entire mechanic.**
⬥ Use <:limitless:641339233638023179> / <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> where required depending on adrenaline in order to use <:devo:513190158728953857> .
⬥ If you need to <:cade:535541306353778689> , use <:divert:787904334377648130> and flick to <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> for 1 tick to get to 100% adrenaline.
⬥ ~20s into exposed core, you can swap to tank gear to benefit from 3pc cryptbloom's magic damage reduction% passive.

.
**__Example Arms rotation:__**
⬥ Closely follow the rotation below in priority of cooldowns and stacks available.
    • If starting with low <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> soul stacks (1-2), use <:soulstrikeflank:1156683164593430579> over <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642>.
    • Use <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> & <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> where appropriate to build adren and stacks before dumping.
    • <:bloat:1159433682403201044> , <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> , and <:commandskeleton:1137809194423160883> should always be used first, as their damage is dealt over time.

        ⬩ <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:splitsoul:1137809168368148490> → (tc) <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> + <:bloat:1159433682403201044> → <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> → <:commandskeleton:1137809194423160883> → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> → <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> (6/8 <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> , use <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> →<:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> if at 12/12) → <:commandzombie:1137809191231295549> → <:commandphantom:1280109200432959528> → <:devo:513190158728953857> → <:omniguard:1138809234922934282> <:spec:537340400273195028>

*Note: Use <:devo:513190158728953857> when required.*

*Note: If flanking, stand central or on the south wall next to the boss.*

### Clearing Glacyte Minions
⬥ Use <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> → <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> once at 5 <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks. continue casting <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892> & <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> until at 5/5.
    • Always cast <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892> before <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> , as this starts its 15s cooldown which lets it be available for a second use before <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> \#3
    • Always target the bolstering glacyte. This causes perks such as crackling, and jas book damage to activate on it. With this strategy, bolstering glacyte at 4000% enrage should die shortly after the four weaker minions.
    • Cast <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> on the magic minion closest to the bolstering glacyte. This will cause vulnerability to apply to all five glacytes.

### Defensive & utility usage
⬥ At higher enrage, you will want to prioritise defensives as follows,
    • **Frost Cannon**: <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:veng:543478434953822208> + <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> + <:reflect:535541258786177064> → <:reprisal:513190159462694912> + <:vitality:654618235097972737> **(In TANK GEAR & max HP)**
        ⬩ **Always wear tank gear** for this mechanic.
        ⬩ Alternatively; <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:vitality:654618235097972737> / <:shielddome:537341954392850442> / <:disrupt:535614336207552523> / <:debil:535541278264393729> / <:reflect:535541258786177064> / <:res:535541258844635148> (hit 3, don't need to combo with defensives) / spam food + be overhealed with brew on first hit. Use of any one of these will ensure you survive the mechanic.
    • **Exposed Core** (arms): <:devo:513190158728953857> → <:divert:787904334377648130> → <:cade:535541306353778689> as needed
    • **Glacyte Minions**: <:debil:535541278264393729> (and, rarely, <:anti:535541306475151390>/<:freedom:535541258240786434> to block or clear stun)
    • **Flurry**: <:disrupt:535614336207552523> / <:reflect:535541258786177064> → <:divert:787904334377648130> (deals ~27k at 4000%) → <:debil:535541278264393729> if needed.
    • **Pillars of Ice**: <:lifetransfer:1137809128136388819> → <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:prep:535541258546970624> → <:commandskeleton:1137809194423160883>
        ⬩ <:commandskeleton:1137809194423160883> can deal damage even if the player doesn't have LOS, depending on the skeleton's position.
