## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
This is an advanced guide to hybrid solo AOD <:aod:580167371365548042> It is assumed you are familiar with the mechanics of the boss as well as having a **strong sense of timing for prayer flicking**. This guide covers strategies to maximize efficiency in this encounter. For a single kill for the CA, -link to solo necro- is recommended instead due to ease of access and gentler learning curve.

## __Strategy and Rotations__
.
### __Phase 1__
.tag:p1
⬥ Start with a <:berserk:535532854004678657> rotation and aim to get <:aod:580167371365548042> to 2500-2600k hp before reavers spawn
⬥ Use <:decimation:643848618477879328> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> to clear out the reavers <:bloodreaver:929773044296216666>, utilising <:bindingshot:535541306563231790> to bind the reavers and simultaneously apply <:blackstonearrow:785031580149743617>
    • Note that the **reavers must be close enough** for decimation <:decimation:643848618477879328> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> to reach them all, this is automatically timed if following the suggested rotation
⬥ When using defensives, keep in mind that Nex's melee autoattacks will reset defensive cooldowns at 5, 10, and 15 enrage stacks, visible on your debuff bar
⬥ To prepare to phase Nex into phase 2, hold damage slightly before hitting 2.1 million health <:constitution:689509250887712902> and overphase with <:overpower:535532879334080517>, pushing her somewhere into the 2060k-2080k range ideally
    • This is to ensure aod <:aod:580167371365548042> is phased into p3 on the amalg <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642>

### __Phase 2__
.tag:p2
⬥ Phase 2 will begin as Nex hits 2.1 million health <:constitution:689509250887712902> and will be shortly followed by amalgamation <:bloodamalg:878739232447938670> spawns, which will be cleared with Invoke Death <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> and threads <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933>
⬥ The **objective is to kill all 4 amalgamations simultaneously and lure all minions into the center** such that you can efficiently aoe them with decimation <:decimation:643848618477879328> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099>
⬥ Ideally phase into 1.8m with the second <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642>
⬥ Use a combination of <:gbarge:535532879250456578>, <:overpower:535532879334080517>, <:assault:535532855191928842>, <:chaosroar:1478062908767211640>, <:ezk:903244090953588787> , <:cane:535532878969438210> to push nex into the 1820-1850k hp range
⬥ **Nex should be centred during this phase** if you phased at the beginning of the elements special attack in phase 1, otherwise make sure to recenter her so that threads <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> will hit all amalgamations

### __Phase 3__
.tag:p3
After the <:gloomfirebow:1446178203608809472> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> spam sequence, optionally tweak <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> stacks with <:shadowtend:642713547142332416> / <:snipe:1478061857486143498> and finish the rest of the <:deathsswiftness:1478061862405931120> with a <:galeshot:1478061864943616122> + <:rapid:535541270521708566> sequence. <:aod:580167371365548042> should be at about 1.2m hp by the end of <:deathsswiftness:1478061862405931120>. The remaining hp should be dealt with using a <:berserk:535532854004678657> rotation.

.
Mechanics in phase 3 will happen in a semi-random order, with the primary restrictions being that ice cannot happen first and the same mechanic cannot happen back-to-back:

⬥ **Smoke Lines**: Simply place and walk out, can use <:devo:513190158728953857> to prevent taking extra damage in case of a stun

⬥ **Ice**: You go into a 'stasis' mode where bled channels will get cancelled and you are no longer able to attack the boss
    • Use <:freedom:535541258240786434> to use a few more damaging abilities before you are imprisoned, <:pulverise:535532879053062146> and <:saradominbow:1508822612049002627> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> can be very helpful for sustain if you just tanked a pool right before being iced
    • Disables defensives such as <:reflect:535541258786177064> / <:res:535541258844635148> but not <:shielddome:537341954392850442>
    • Use <:shielddome:537341954392850442> + <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> (if using <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>) and make sure to keep prayer flicking
    • Preparation <:prep:535541258546970624> isn't put on cooldown so <:res:535541258844635148> can be used before ice and <:prep:535541258546970624> once you are imprisoned
    • The timing for when ice ends is when the cooldown of your mobility abilities reach 4 seconds (they instantly become usable again right after ice ends)
    • If you phase into P4 while iced, use <:anti:535541306475151390> to avoid being frozen by glacies pillar while prayer flicking <:melee:1096130867279171706> <:magic:689504724159823906> against ice and boss autos.

.
⬥ **Pool**: Must have >14k(or 11.2k if <:hellhoundpouch:921410226475925514>) health <:constitution:689509250887712902> to take the pool
    • DPS gear and boosts from bonfire / desert pantheon aura <:pantheonaura:982264010126819380> + thermal spa <:thermalflask:689530593796423724> will put you >12.8k maximum health <:constitution:689509250887712902>
    • Fortitude <:fortitude:537341748180025365> may be flicked on for up to 1000 additional maximum health <:constitution:689509250887712902>
    • Save <:vitality:654618235097972737> for second pools so that you enter pillar phase with high hp
    • Be wary of an invisible autoattack as Nex finishes her initial animation of spawning the pool

⬥ Recover from pool damage using <:disrupt:535614336207552523> <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> + <:ezk:903244090953588787> <:spec:537340400273195028> / <:deadshot:1478061860371828929> / <:saradominbow:1508822612049002627> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099>

### Phase 4
.tag:p4
⬥ All mechanics are straightforward from here on. Cycle between <:deathsswiftness:1478061862405931120> and <:berserk:535532854004678657> to quickly finish the encounter.
⬥ Bombs can be placed at max range on a wall next to a pillar or as close to the pillar as possible to leave as much open space as possible
⬥ <:res:535541258844635148> / <:devo:513190158728953857> can be used to deal with the constant autoattacks from Nex
⬥ After all pillars have been defeated, you can reposition middle, only surging out to place bombs
    • Make sure to keep track of which bar is the instakill / bomb if doing this to prevent accidentally bombing mid
⬥ BSA <:blackstonearrow:785031580149743617> stacks will NOT carry over into phase 4, making <:wenarrows:971025697046925362> a strong option
