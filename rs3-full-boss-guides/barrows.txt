## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
The Barrows Brothers <:dharokhead:513222840162582541> are mainly camped by high-level players in an effort to obtain the Graverobber title. The brothers are relatively weak, with the exception of Linza.

## __Useful Unlocks__
⬥ Completing all [Hard Morytania achievements](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Morytania_Set_Tasks_-_Hard>):
    • Doubles rune drops.
    • Allows you to bank loot directly from the chest.

⬥ Completing all [Easy Combat Mastery Achievements](<https://runescape.wiki/w/War%27s_Blessing_1>):
    •  Portals will permanently appear in corner & central rooms of the underground crypt.
    •  Teleporting via these portals circumvents the need to to solve puzzles or open doors.

.
## __The Fight__
.tag:fight
### Suggested order for killing the brothers:
Linza → Ahrim → Verac → Akrisae → Torag → Karil → Guthan → Dharok

.
### General Strategy
⬥ If any brother is in the tunnels instead of their crypt you can use <:barrowsamulet:1097517253056024647> on the loot chest for the same function.
    • This is ideal as killing a brother while doing tunnels interrupts movement.
⬥ Tunnel doors are 2 tiles wide, click to open the door directly in front of you to avoid wasting a tick walking to the other door.
⬥ It is recommended to be on an Undead slayer task to benefit from the slayer helmet <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> and Guardhouse boosts.
⬥ You can pre-vuln by using <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> on the ground after clicking the sarcophagus.
⬥ Bringing a spade and using it to dig in order to enter the mounds is quicker.
⬥ Good movement can improve the efficiency of your runs, below are some examples starting at Dharok's crypt:
    • [Guthan last](<https://youtu.be/f4UiE6w-0zs>)
    • [Akrisae last](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LnnjpAuaeLE>)
    • [Karil last](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vUq-P5gYWgk>)
    • [Torag last](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=igtJtWTDS90>)
    • [Diagonal Surge](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XXSx2wDbvcQ>) inside tunnels.
⬥ Using High Contrast Mode lets you see which door is clickable when in the tunnels.
    • Right-click the World Map to toggle it on.

⬥ Start with <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> and <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> stacks.
    • If <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks are needed, you can <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> + <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> in tunnels.
