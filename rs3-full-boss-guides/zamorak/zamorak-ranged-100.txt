## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

100% Zamorak can either be farmed or killed 10 times to build up bad luck mitigation. Ranged with <:bolg:994189289623662702> is currently the meta for fast solo low enrage kills. Rotations presented in the guide should achieve consistent sub 3:40 solos.

This guide assumes knowledge of zamorak and BOLG rotations. Visit <#1251104929599455263>, <#1251104943566491710>, <#1252196383159484519> to learn more.

## __General information__
.tag:general
⬥ The pad order for the fight is: **2 → 1 → 4 → 6 → 5 → 3**
⬥ When killing witch cancel <:rapid:535541270521708566> early if possible (typically you can activate <:grico:787904334812807238> when witch has <10k health remaining)
⬥ Press <:realmmovement:1073955377537876089> to exit infernus the same tick as you activate your last ability (typically <:grico:787904334812807238>). This will let you exit infernus and tc + ability on <:zamorak:994914159969976440> without losing ticks
⬥ When <:zamorak:994914159969976440> transitions from green to grey hp he becomes immune that tick (for example if 1st hit of <:dbow:643848618553507843> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> transitions <:zamorak:994914159969976440> from green to grey the remaining damage and entire 2nd hit will be nulled). Avoid high damaging abilities when <:zamorak:994914159969976440> is close to phasing to grey hp
⬥ Drink <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> when you need adrenaline during the 1st minute of the kill
⬥ Aim to keep your HP low throughout the fight to benefit from <:berserkersfury:697808774106185768> and 6th edict. Best way to keep hp low is to tank some autos with <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> while under <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099>

## __Phase 7__

### General information
⬥ With correct positioning and <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> placement it's possible to reach all runes from your <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> except the back one (refer to image below). Red arrow indicates the tile where you should place your <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121>
⬥ The rotation for runes assumes no back runes. If you get back rune <:surge:535533810004262912> towards it and improvise
⬥ Before 1st rune is shown you should target and kill demon. Once it's dead throw <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> on zamorak and start lowering hp of north-eastern rune until 1st rune is shown
⬥ After demon is killed use <:disrupt:535614336207552523> + <:Veng:543478434953822208> which will be used to block the melee hit
⬥ When large red bomb is about to land equip your <:RoD:513190159462825984> to tank it
⬥ It's very important to stay at very low hp during entire P7 (typically ~2k). This means you shouldn't use <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> at all. Having low hp will not only increase the damage from 6th edict and <:berserkersfury:697808774106185768> but also make it safer to survive since bleed from <:RoD:513190159462825984> will be lower

⬥ <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> placement (red tile):
