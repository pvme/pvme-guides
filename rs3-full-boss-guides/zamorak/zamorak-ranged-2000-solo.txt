## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

2000% Zamorak can be farmed solo with <:range:580168050121113623> while skipping most specs that would normally slow down other styles such as <:magic:689504724159823906> and <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>. The rotations and spec order in this guide are designed to work at HP cap (1740%+), but can still function at slightly lower enrages with some adaptation. Higher enrages than 2000% will run into issues with incoming damage and Channelers HP scaling resulting in higher red HP to deal with.

⬥ Players can expect to get consistent kills below 7:15 when following the rotations in this guide, with room to optimise well below 7:00 with clean movement and good decision making.

⬥ This guide assumes pre-existing knowledge of Zamorak's mechanics, how to deal with them, and how his auto attacks work. Visit <#1251104929599455263> or <#1251104943566491710> for in-depth information on these mechanics.

## __General Information__ ##
.tag:info
.
⬥ Improvisation is required around <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> deathspore procs, <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> BoLG stacks, adrenaline, and incoming damage between phasing into **grey HP** and **red hp** during the next pad. However, **green hp** rotations should work consistently to phase Zamorak at the desired times.


⬥ All <:wenarrow:971025697046925362> uses are assumed to be at 15 stacks, building between pads alongside <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642>

⬥ Abilities denoted by <:wenarrow:971025697046925362> <:grico:787904334812807238> <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> represent wensporing (swapping from <:wenarrow:971025697046925362> to <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> after casting an ability on the same tick to receive both arrow effects)

⬥ Take note of the arrow types used during each section of the fight. Messing up ammo swaps can result in an inability to phase at the desired times.

.
⬥ <:enhreplen:634350450887622656> is used in the preset to be able to <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> + <:enhreplen:634350450887622656> → <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> on phase 4 but <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> works fine as well with tighter input timing. There is minimal difference between the two options.

⬥ <:zekkil:903244090953588787> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> is optional but <:abyssalscourge:947871842469834832> is highly recommended to be able to quickly put up two bleeds with minimal time investment.

⬥ <:animahelmsliske:643120082398085158> is used to avoid a hit chance penalty for using <:melee:1096130867279171706> and <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> abilities during the fight.

⬥ <:incend:535541258429661215> on a <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> proc will deal more damage and make clearing **red HP** easier, any <:incend:535541258429661215> → <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> that appears in this guide can be adjusted into <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> → <:incend:535541258429661215> → <:piercing:535541258538450944>/<:mds:535541259033378827> if available at 5/6 <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> stacks respectively.
    • This is a slight optimization but you should not intentionally slow down otherwise just to get the <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> proc.
    • The largest time saves will come from good movement between pads and good rotational improvisation to clear **red HP** as quickly as possible.

## __Special Attack Order__
.tag:specs
.
⬥ Pad order is assumed to be **2 → 4 → 1 → 3 → 6 → 5**
    • (Optional) **2 → 5 → 1 → 3 → 6 → 4** may also be used for less incoming damage during p1-6 but with more required movement and having to deal with traps

⬥ **Red HP** mechanics are always skipped in this guide, all listed mechanics occur in **green HP**

⬥ Order:
    • **P1:** Flames of Zamorak → Infernal Tomb + Phase (Tomb and Phase occurs at the same time)

    • **P2:** Adrenaline Cage → Phase

    • **P3:** Chaos Blast → Phase

    • **P4:** Rune of Destruction → Adrenaline Cage → Phase

    • **P5:** Chaos Blast → Phase

    • **P6:** Rune of Destruction → Flames of Zamorak (skippable) → Phase
