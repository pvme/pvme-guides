> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
Many of melee's damage handicaps are lifted at <:zamorak:994914159969976440> making the fight fun and interesting to play out. Lifted hitcaps and massive adrenaline refund allows <:havocgop:994920849117282365> + <:eofspec:746403211908481184> <:dragonclaw:779048041088024606> and <:chaosroar:994644356671737966> <:overpower:535532879334080517> to deal their full damage. In addition, since <:zamorak:994914159969976440> is not poisonable, glove swapping is not necessary either. This strategy estimates 5:55-6:05 kill times at 7-8kph.

.
