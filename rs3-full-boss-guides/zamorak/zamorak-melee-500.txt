## __Hellhound Strategy__
.tag:hhintro
Many of melee's damage handicaps are lifted at <:zamorak:994914159969976440> making the fight fun and interesting to play out. Lifted hitcaps and massive adrenaline refund allows <:havocgop:994920849117282365> + <:eofspec:746403211908481184> <:dragonclaw:779048041088024606> and <:chaosroar:994644356671737966> <:overpower:535532879334080517> to deal their full damage. In addition, since <:zamorak:994914159969976440> is not poisonable, glove swapping <:glovesofpassage:556588694862037012> <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> is not necessary either. A hellhound <:hellhoundpouch:921410226475925514> is a much safer familiar choice than a ripper demon <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714>. Aim to have on average **5:55-6:05** kill times at **7-8kph**.
