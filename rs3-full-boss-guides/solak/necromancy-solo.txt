> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
This rotation seeks to deal high damage throughout the encounter to skip most boss mechanics. The primary checkpoints for a successful execution are comprised of:

    • Root skipping Phase 1
    • Entering eruptions around 1:40 boss timer
    • Completing all 4 Anima Eruptions in <8 Global Cool Downs
    • Phasing into Phase 3 before Anima Rain ends
    • One cycling Erethdor on the second Sigil after capping Solak

The rotation as written has an element of RNG from critical strikes, Impatient perk procs and general boss mechanics, but should provide kill times around 5:00 on average.

This is an **advanced guide** which assumes a strong underlying knowledge of all Solak mechanics.

> **__The Fight/Strategy__**
.tag:strategy
⬥ This rotation is built around skipping/ignoring most mechanics in favor of dealing more damage.
⬥ Root skips are expected at about ~95% success rate, they are killed moving clockwise from north-east to north-west
⬥ This method utilizes free adren-cost  <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> , <:conjureskeleton:1159434516658651147> , <:conjurezombie:1137809200219684924>  multiple times via target dropping on <:roots:848803062684581929> ,  eruptions and <:dummy:656844963522281473>
⬥ A <:flank4:712073088296157185>  switch is brought mostly to speed up clearing eruptions, but is also used whenever getting to a 4 or 5 stack <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642>  will take too long during Blighted Core and Phase 3
