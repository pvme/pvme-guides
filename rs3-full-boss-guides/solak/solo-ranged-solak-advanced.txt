> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
With bik arrows <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> and the <:bolg:994189289623662702> ranged has the potential for extremely high damage output at Solak, as it allows for heavy <:poisonicon:944649693500154006> utilisation. However, due to the nature of crits, no two kills will ever be the same. As such, this channel will discuss rotations in broad strokes.
This is an advanced <:range:580168050121113623> guide to Grandmaster role (sub 5:15) solo Solak, assuming strong underlying knowledge of all Solak mechanics.

⬥ Excess adrenaline can be used for more <:ecb:615618531937222657><:eofspec:746403211908481184>, <:dbow:643848618553507843><:eofspec:746403211908481184> , or <:bolg:994189289623662702><:spec:537340400273195028>

.

⬥ All abilities on roots/arms/legs/core/eruptions/Erethor are done with <:fularrow:971025696958853180> unless stated otherwise. All abilities on Solak under 400 bik stacks <:evolvingtoxin:1016651068882550885> are done with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> unless stated otherwise.
⬥ At 400 stacks, use <:fularrow:971025696958853180> and swap for <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> every 20 seconds and before going to kill elf to upkeep <:evolvingtoxin:1016651068882550885>. All <:sgb:626466665848242186><:eofspec:746403211908481184> are done using <:fularrow:971025696958853180>

> **__The Fight__**
.tag:fight
**__Pre Build on Dummy:__**
Use <:grico:787904334812807238> and other basics for 5 stacks of <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> and 2-6 bolg stacks <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> but keep <:grico:787904334812807238> almost off cd before starting.

.
__**Phase 1:**__

__Rootlings:__
Improvise with basics, <:snap:535534127131394088> and <:bolg:994189289623662702><:spec:537340400273195028>, always doing a <:piercing:535541258538450944> on Solak after to keep/build up <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> stacks.
On last two rootlings build up <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> (<:grico:787904334812807238>/<:piercing:535541258538450944>) and <:incend:535541258429661215> either last root or arm.

__Arms:__
( <:incend:535541258429661215> →) <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> then improvise tresholds with <:ecb:615618531937222657><:eofspec:746403211908481184> on, always doing one <:grico:787904334812807238> with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> before going to legs.

__Legs:__
Same as arms, <:grico:787904334812807238><:bikarrow:971025697235689513> before killing last leg and doing a <:bolg:994189289623662702><:spec:537340400273195028> with 3 stacks in between.

__**Phase 2:**__
Do three defensives while waiting for Solak to be attackable, then use a damaging ability with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> (preferable <:grico:787904334812807238>).

__Eruptions:__
Always use the <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> to get an extra <:snap:535534127131394088>, <:bolg:994189289623662702><:spec:537340400273195028> or <:snipe:535541258425204770> hit. Use <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> on all eruptions. Use <:piercing:535541258538450944> with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> on Solak after two south eruptions.
