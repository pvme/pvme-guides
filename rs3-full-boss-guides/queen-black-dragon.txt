> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
The Queen Black Dragon resides in the lowest level of the Grotworm Lair which can be found just west of Port Sarim. 60 <:Summoning:583430259064569856> is required to be able to enter the Arena via the Summoning Portal. Before attempting the fight it is _highly_ recommended to have level 80 in your preferred combat stat (generally <:range:580168050121113623> or <:Magic:689504724159823906>) and access to Super Antifire potions.

*Note: Completion of the Song of the Depths quest is recommended as well to reduce the amount of damage received.*

> __**Rotation (Courtesy of <@!300195714267480064>)**__
.tag:rotation
*Note: Rotation assumes camping <:RubyBakri:565726489413287956>*

__**Phase 1**__
At 1.2s <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:spec:537340400273195028> → (tc) <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:snap:535534127131394088> → <:corruptshot:535541306294796299> (if not phased use <:mds:535541259033378827>) → tag pillar

__**Phase 2**__
<:rapid:535541270521708566> (cancel at ~5k HP until phase end) → <:mds:535541259033378827> + <:surge:535533810004262912> to pillar off GCD and tag pillar

__**Phase 3**__
s<:Tuskas:513201065513058306> → r<:Tuskas:513201065513058306> + <:piercing:535541258538450944> → <:surge:535533810004262912> east off GCD + <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> + activate <:dragonslayer:641339921814126594> → tag pillar

__**Phase 3 Notes**__
⬥ <:Tuskas:513201065513058306> and <:piercing:535541258538450944> are used only as filler abilities and also due to cooldowns

__**Phase 4**__
s<:corruptshot:535541306294796299> → r<:corruptshot:535541306294796299> + <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:snap:535534127131394088> → (finish kill with basics) → <:bd:535532854281764884> to pillar

If less than 100% adren at the end of kill, use <:anti:535541306475151390> and/or <:freedom:535541258240786434>
