> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
In order to fight Rasial you must complete [Alpha vs Omega](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Alpha_vs_Omega>) quest. Rasial deals Necromancy and typeless damage, and can currently only be killed using Necromancy.

⬥ **Important:** Do not move too far from Rasial (>5 tiles) or <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> cannot bounce to you.

**Rotations last updated: 17 Sep 2023**

.tag:reqs
The rotations may not work as well as intended if any of the following are missing:
⬥ Undead damage boosts <:salveamulet:797899945730244648> <:undeadslayer:641339922019516416> <:undeadslayerperk:689502804720615441>.
⬥ <:igneouskalzuk:902209626479685734>/<:igneouskalmor:1139200072438005851> for <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728>.
⬥ Blood Reaver <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> autofire set to 3-5.
    • Spam eat <:blueblubber:689530593742291033> under <:splitsoul:1137809168368148490> if not using <:reaverscroll:1022562484378419284>.
⬥ The Lunar spellbook is used for <:veng:543478434953822208>.
⬥ All of the Necromancy talent tree unlocked.
    • Lesser bone shield <:lesserboneshield:1137809125590450276> active.
