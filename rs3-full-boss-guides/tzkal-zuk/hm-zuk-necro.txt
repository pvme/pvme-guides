## __Introduction and preface__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

This guide is targeted towards players with a strong basic understanding of the encounter and are aiming for the <@&931064588655525909> and <@&931064626370715718> roles, and not for players attempting hardmode Zuk for the first time. The rotations used are examples and can be adapted depending on Adrenaline, Residual Soul stacks <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> and Necrosis Stacks <:necrosis:1139452791878856805>. The lures generally require moving depending on which monsters are blocked prior to using <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933>.

.
Notes:
⬥ With Necromancy, 2-cycling Zuk <:tzkalzuk:931175752651653121> and 1-cycling Har'Aken <:haraken:513212948781596682> is fairly consistent with a good rotation.
⬥ Players should focus on wave clear and try to follow this guide as best as possible. The guide contains an outline of the necromancy rotations and lures.
⬥ You should upkeep <:darkness:1137809209782698024> and <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> + <:commandghost:1159434409913634816> at all times for a reduction in damage taken, healing and increased damage dealt.
⬥ <:greaterboneshield:1137809118283976814> and <:zemouregalsnexus:1179716261681307728> should be used as defensives will be vital during the encounter, such as to use <:reflect:535541258786177064>/<:devo:513190158728953857> while tanking waves to be able to use <:threadsoffate:1137809172335951933> on all monsters, or to block <:tzkalzuk:931175752651653121>'s attacks during the pillar phase.
⬥ <:mahj:643148943856762890> and <:berserkersfury:697808774106185768> together are likely a stronger alternative to using <:invigaura:643505652865695767> and <:furyofthesmall:697808773917573233> but it is less forgiving on hp and adrenaline. This guide assumes use of <:invigaura:643505652865695767> and <:furyofthesmall:697808773917573233> - rotations will need to be adapted if using <:mahj:643148943856762890>.

### Preset Notes

⬥ Hand wrap of the First Necromancer <:firstnecrohandwrap:1142416978112417812> should only be worn when summoning conjures and when fighting Zuk <:tzkalzuk:931175752651653121> as he isn't poisonable.

⬥ Mobile <:mob:689501908628799488> perk is a must have on gear.
    • Alternatively, use Shadow's Grace <:shadowsgrace:895999229737185280>.

⬥ You may prefer to use a Mage, Ranged or Melee Obsidian Helm <:obsidianmagehelm:1100353893063462912>/<:obsidianrangerhelm:1100353910130102312>/<:obsidianwarriorhelm:1100353943277686924> for damage reduction rather than a Necromancy helmet <:firstnecrocrown:1206671120623870013> on many of the waves. Although it is offstyle, it can be useful for the damage reduction (especially if charged in the Fight Cauldron beforehand). It will provide 9% damage reduction from all sources, or 19% reduction if Tokkul is donated to the coffer at the Fight Cauldron.

⬥ This guide assumes that Kal'gerion Demon Scrolls <:kalgscroll:841409588954923049> are used, which can be set to autofire and will auto-renew as the buff expires. If you are struggling with food or damage taken, a <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> with <:reaverscroll:1022562484378419284> (low autofire) or <:vampaura:643505653079343144> will help, however this may affect the rotations below. It is possible to obtain a sub 15minute kill with a blood reaver.
