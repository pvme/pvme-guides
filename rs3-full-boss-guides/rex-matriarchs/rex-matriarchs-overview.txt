## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
⬥ The Rex Matriarchs are four dinosaur bosses found on Anachronia
⬥ Each Matriarch has its own attack style, weakness and mechanics
⬥ While the Matriarchs share a boss instance, only 3/4 share an arena
    • Osseous' arena is accessed via teleportation device near the entrance, only after completion of the [Osseous Rex quest](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Osseous_Rex_quest>)

.
