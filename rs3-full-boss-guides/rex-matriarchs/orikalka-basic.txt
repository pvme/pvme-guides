## __Fight Mechanics__
.tag:fightMechanics
⬥ Stay aware of nearby Matriarchs when moving, as only 3/4 Matriarchs share the main arena
⬥ You should attack with Magic <:magic:689504724159823906>
    • Fire spells <:firesurge:543465116092334083> give a +27% hit chance boost
    • Any other style will only deal insignificant damage

.
### __Attack Rotation__
3x <:melee:1096130867279171706> → **Frost Storm** → <:melee:1096130867279171706> → <:magic:689504724159823906> → **Pyroclastic Shock** →
3x <:melee:1096130867279171706> → **Pyroclastic Shock** → <:magic:689504724159823906> → 2x <:melee:1096130867279171706> → **Pyroclastic Shock** →
2x <:melee:1096130867279171706> → <:magic:689504724159823906> → **Pyroclastic Shock** → 3x <:melee:1096130867279171706> → **Pyroclastic Shock** → <:magic:689504724159823906> →
*Repeat*

### __Auto Attacks__ ([Melee example](<https://img.pvme.io/images/n77pITX1Ix.mp4>) / [Magic example](<https://img.pvme.io/images/ua9uchPcaZ.mp4>))
**Mechanic:** Orikalka uses Melee <:melee:1096130867279171706> and Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> auto attacks.

**Solution:**
⬥ If lower Defence <:defence:689509250979987525> pray against Melee <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> / <:protectfrommelee:1052551452788269127>
    • Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> once you are comfortable surviving the mechanics

.
### __Frost Storm / Frostbite__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/icvqcL0erz.mp4>)
**Mechanic:** Orikalka spins in place, dealing up to 4500 Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> damage and applying *Frostbite*.
While *Frostbite* is active, your global cooldown is doubled for 30 seconds. This makes your abilities feel much slower and lowers your damage.

**Solution:**
⬥ If Frost Storm deals too much damage, use <:deflectmage:544195487926845462> / <:protectfrommagic:1052551456835768351> or <:res:535541258844635148>
⬥ If you get *Frostbite*, either:
    • Keep fighting with abilities which don't care about the global cooldown (e.g., channels <:asphyx:535533833072672778> / <:gconc:1478063111104757760> or damage-over-time abilities <:combust:1478063107908702482>)
    • Clear it using the fissures from **Pyroclastic Shock** (see below)

.
### __Pyroclastic Shock / Fissures__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/fxCDXAXY4V.mp4>)
**Mechanic:** Orikalka slams its tail, creating 3 fissures on the ground. Standing on a fissure deals rapid Melee <:melee:1096130867279171706> damage, with the final hit dealing up to 6000 damage.

**Solution:**
⬥ Avoid the fissures unless you are attempting to clear *Frostbite* (see above solution under **Frost Storm**)
