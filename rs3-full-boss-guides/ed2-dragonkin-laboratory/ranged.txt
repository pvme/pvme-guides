**__Introduction__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

The second Elite Dungeon, the Dragonkin Laboratory, is a relatively straightforward encounter. The encounter is useful for anyone wanting to learn how to deal with large mobs as well as bosses and combines aspects of single target and AoE damage.

The three bosses of the Laboratory drop the Greater Flurry, Greater Fury and Greater Barge codices in the order that they appear.

Each solo run of ED2 is worth <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Bosses!E546$ in uniques.

.

> __**Verak Lith**__
.tag:veraklith
.img:https://i.imgur.com/CSMjoJH.jpg
.
The second encounter of the Laboratory. Verak Lith's attack rotation is predictable, but deals consistent moderately high damage. Therefore, it is recommended to learn the mechanics properly to avoid any unnecessary deaths. Additionally, he drops the <:gfury:535532879334080527> <:greatercodex:643169137098752001>

He always melees the player when in melee distance and always uses mage otherwise. The auto attacks hit quite hard with the mage ones being desynced visually, so it is important to not mess up too many prayer flicks here.

> **__Black Stone Dragon__**
.tag:blackstonedragon
.img:https://i.imgur.com/DDRMznI.jpg
.
The final boss of the Dragonkin Laboratory. She is the main source of the dungeon's primary unique: Draconic Energies <:draconicenergy:643162228991983659>, which are used to make the Mage T92 Elite Tectonic set <:elitetectbody:552955120707698699> and also drops the <:gbarge:535532879250456578> <:greatercodex:643169137098752001>

This fight consists of simple mechanics but can be very punishing if mistakes are made. It is also one of the few bosses to auto attack in all three combat styles, with melee hitting especially hard, so it is possible to die just from auto attacks if the player can't out heal with <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>, is low on food, and/or can't prayer flick the fight.
