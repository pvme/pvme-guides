## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Sliske's general in the Heart of Gielinor, Gregorovic is largely considered to be one of the harder GWD2 bosses for learners. This is generally due to his high accuracy and hard hits.

.

## __Tips and Positioning__
.tag:tips
⬥ The recommended method of killing Gregorovic involves attacking at a distance (at least 2 squares away).
    *• Note: For melee you can use halberd-range weapons, such as Laniakea's Spear <:lanispear:839903893177106454>, Dragon Rider Lance <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> or Noxious scythe <:noxscythe:513190159341322240>*
⬥ You should stand in the north-west corner of the arena with <:IritSticks:690987265371144202> **Irit Incense Sticks** at overloaded potency to negate the poisoning spirits and effectively ignore the 'Summon Spirits' mechanic
