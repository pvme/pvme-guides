> **__Prefight__**
.tag:prefight
Ideally melee roles at AoD should teleport out of the instance as soon as they pick up their loot. They should bank at War's Retreat, use the altar if necessary to reset their berserk cooldown, use the adrenaline crystals to get to 100% adrenaline, DBA <:DBA:603979368850653216> spec, and then enter the instance as fast as possible so as to not hold up the rest of the team.

Once inside:
Wait 1gcd after base suns → tc <:nat:535541258131865633> → <:HydrixBakri:550834403136503822> <:gricocaroming:867678153966878740>
If >80 adren <:lengmh:883134308146098227><:spec:537340400273195028>
If not, continue to build to 100 adren

**__Movement__**
It is incredibly important to not lose ticks while travelling between pillars. To do this follow the guide below.
t0 - Cast ability (e.g. <:deci:535532879325822986>)
t1 - <:deci:535532879325822986> lands
t3 - Cast next ability (e.g. <:cleave:535532878616985610>) + <:bdmobile:867678153668820992> + <:surgemobile:867678153798975498>
t4 - <:cleave:535532878616985610> lands (Pillar ideally dies here)
t5 - <:surgemobile:867678153798975498>
t6 - Ability on next pillar
