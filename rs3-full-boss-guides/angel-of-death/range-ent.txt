.tag:pre
At War's Retreat:
Touch crystal → click <:dummy:656844963522281473> + s <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> → <:enhreplen:634350450887622656> + touch crystal

After entering the instance:
tc <:dummy:656844963522281473> r <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> + <:nat:535541258131865633> → <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:gconc:869285393223254107> → <:dbreath:535533833391702017> → (3.0) <:ecb:615618531937222657><:eofspec:746403211908481184> → s <:bolg:994189289623662702><:spec:537340400273195028>

_Notes:
⬥ You will need to use the east crystal and stall meteor on the closest dummy
⬥ If you are not last inside the instance, wait until everyone is in to release your stall. Otherwise, drop your dummy and release your stall as soon as possible
⬥ It is possible to stall infinitely by using <:dive:1049378668197195808> and <:invokelordofbones:1176968330582700174>/<:darkness:1137809209782698024>_
