> __**Chinner**__
.tag:chin
**__Role Responsibilities__**
.tag:chinrr
The chinner is responsible for clearing minions in p3 with mechanised chinchompas as well as applying the statius warhammer debuff in p1 at the very beginning of the fight. They are also repsonsible for backup basing should the base die after minions have been killed. Should base die after minions are killed, it is the chinner's job to take over.

**__Rotations__**
There are 3 rotations available to a chinner. The 2/4 rotation uses their <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> on p2 and, depending on team speed, p4 as well. Deathspore involves using deathspore arrows to get a free <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> at the beginning of p3 before chincending. Deci-cend rotation foregoes chins in favour of decimation EoF. Out of these rotations, deci-cend is the most complex but gives the most damage if done correctly.

**Please note that the rotations using BoLG <:bolg:994189289623662702> are subject to change post-eof cooldown fix, which is to be released with an unkown ETA**

⬥ **Pillars**
Pillars and Zaros phase have no set rot for rangers, due to adren variation between kills caused by <:incend:535541258429661215> buff and hydrix bolts as well as pillar kill speed varying hugely from team to team or even kill to kill. You should aim to be in <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> for 1st and 2nd pillars, re <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> at third pillar for uptime on 3rd to 4th pillar, and finally re-<:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> on <:aod:580167371365548042> during Zaros phase. When travelling between pillars, minimising tick loss is different to other roles due to wanting to keep your ecb spec, so you can't use bd to do this. To work around this, you're going to need to double surge. To do this, start spam clicking towards the next pillar before doing your ability, then the same tick you do the ability, surge and then surge again. By doing this will get you within range of the next pillar. If you aren't able to attack from that new position, make sure to stall an ability on the pillar for once you do run to it.

.tag:rangeent
**__Role Responsibilities__**
.tag:rangeentrr
**Range minions tanks** are responsible for killing their amalgamations in p2 and subsequently luring the minions under <:aod:580167371365548042> for the chinner to kill in p3.

**Free range** is reponsible for helping umbra and fumus minion tanks kill their amalgamations during phase 2. Should the base die before minions are dead, it is the free range's job to take over until the chinner has finished killing minions.

⬥ **Pillars**
Pillars and Zaros phase have no set rot for rangers, due to adren variation between kills caused by <:incend:535541258429661215> buff and hydrix bolts as well as pillar kill speed varying hugely from team to team or even kill to kill. When travelling between pillars, minimising tick loss is different to other roles due to wanting to keep your ecb spec, so you can't use bd to do this. To work around this, you're going to need to double surge. To do this, start spam clicking towards the next pillar before doing your ability, then the same tick you do the ability, surge and then surge again. By doing this will get you within range of the next pillar. If you aren't able to attack from that new position, make sure to stall an ability on the pillar for once you do run to it. Below is basic 'guide' rot for you to base your pillar and zaros rotations off of.
