### __Phase 5 (1-Cycle strategy)__
*From here on, roles are typically not correlated with base/DPS. Basic responsibilities are outlined as follows:*

**South Charge:**
⬥ Charge the two inner fragments, switching sides at 50% charge if aiming to 1-cycle
⬥ <:magmatempest:902209626509025290> as often as possible on the following spot to keep aggro on Salawas
    • Attacking them before they reach the top of the stairs may cause them to become un-aggroable
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/pS7lzYU3nL.png

.
**Far East/West Charge:**
⬥ Charge the far fragments to 100%, then quickly <:dive:1049378668197195808> + <:surge:535533810004262912> to charge the inner statue that was left at 50% if aiming to 1-cycle

**Green 1/Green 2:**
⬥ Alternate blocking lines from Amascut by standing directly in front of her with a defensive <:reflect:535541258786177064> <:debil:535541278264393729> active
⬥ Charge the same fragment as South Charge while not blocking, making sure to keep the tether active as much as possible
    • Use <:dive:1049378668197195808> / <:escape:535541258832052231> / <:prismofsalvation:892342109431029761> to maintain full tether uptime

**Rotation:** <:tsunami:535533809995874304> ~9s from Tumeken being clicked → <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:berserk:535532854004678657> → <:gbarge:535532879250456578> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977> → clear Salawas w/<:cane:535532878969438210> and <:quake:535532879506309150> → target <:amascuthead:1400943309576470589> + improvise using <:overpower:535532879334080517> / <:ezk:903244090953588787> <:spec:537340400273195028> / <:varanussmercy:1448697873603498045> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977> once available → <:dragonscimitar:1048592044731400252> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099>  if platforming + end phase with enough adren to build to ~120 adren
Notes:
⬥ Second <:gflurry:535532879283879977> only needed for platformer role on phase 6, everyone else have more lenient timing
⬥ Provided rotation is generally overkill if multiple players are doing it, you may comfortably phase using <:gsunshine:994644352871714836> + <:fsoa:869284271595069451> also

.
**Clicking Tumeken (Anyone):**
⬥ Timing the click on the same tick that a new set of lines appear from Amascut will cause one jump to be skipped for a total of 3
⬥ If <2 charge ticks have been lost during the phase, the timing will line up near-perfectly with the final charge completion during a 1-cycle
    • Lines appear exactly 9s (15t) in between each set
