## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

This guide assumes you are already familiar with the basic mechanics of Kerapac which can be seen here <#1020559705204076614> and own BIS gear in Necromancy.

It is recommended to have a Nodon dragonkin slayer task <:slayer:797896049548066857> as the genocidal boost does work with Necromancy. You may also use the Premier Artefact <:premierartefact:918517285176623104> to have a 10% chance to not decrease the slayer task count with each kill.

### Phase 4 Notes
⬥ The speed of this phase relies on <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> bouncing between echoes, manipulated via "Skull Juggling":
    • Cast <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> at max distance (6 tiles) away from the initial target.
    • Wait an odd number of global cooldown cycles.
    • <:dive:1049378668197195808> towards your next target during the last global cooldown cycle.
⬥ The third echo must be killed with a fast hitting ability ( <:necroauto:1137809137401602109> / <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> / <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> ) otherwise the first juggled skull will null on <:kerapac:875443920673337344>
