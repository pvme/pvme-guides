## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

This guide assumes you are already familiar with the basic mechanics of Kerapac which can be seen here <#1020559705204076614> and own BIS gear in Ranged.

It is highly recommended to have a Nodon dragonkin slayer task <:slayer:797896049548066857> as the helmet boost does work. You may also use the Premier Artefact <:premierartefact:918517285176623104> to have a 10% chance to not decrease the slayer task count with each kill.

This strategy aims to keep HP below max from P1 to P3 to continually heal with <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:vampaura:643505653079343144>, thus maximising hitsplats with <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive to maximise <:poisonicon:944649693500154006> damage which has excellent synergy with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513>

## __Phase 4__
.tag:p4
⬥ Keep high hp by spamming food and <:reaverscroll:1022562484378419284> throughout P4.
⬥ After killing south echo Kerapac is tagged with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513> basic to extend the bik arrow duration.
⬥ Camp <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> for the 1st clone. Afterwards swap to <:DeflectMage:544195487926845462> and only <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> flick if you're careful.
