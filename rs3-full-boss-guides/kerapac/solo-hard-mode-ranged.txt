## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

This guide assumes you are already familiar with the basic mechanics of Kerapac which can be seen here <#1020559705204076614> and own BIS gear in Ranged.

It is highly recommended to have a Nodon dragonkin slayer task <:slayer:797896049548066857> as the helmet boost does work. You may also use the Premier Artefact <:premierartefact:918517285176623104> to have a 10% chance to not decrease the slayer task count with each kill.

This strategy aims to keep HP below max from P1 to P3 to continually heal with <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:vampaura:643505653079343144>, thus maximising hitsplats with <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive to maximise <:poisonicon:944649693500154006> damage which has excellent synergy with <:bikarrow:971025697235689513>

### Overview
⬥ Use <:KwuarmSticks:565726489341984779> + <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> with <:reaverscroll:1022562484378419284> + autofire set to 0.
⬥ Rotations assume slayer task and <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> on anachronia stand.
⬥ Do not <:timewarp:870328868815396914> and <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> on the same tick.
    • Only <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> after seeing the <:timewarp:870328868815396914> icon on buff bar (at least 1t apart).
⬥ Manually spam <:reaverscroll:1022562484378419284> whenever you need health.
⬥ Have <:darkness:1137809209782698024> active for a smoother P4.
⬥ For <:fleetingboots:789813993480388640> <:rapid:535541270521708566> equip <:edracoboots:1179717602151514123> back immediately after casting <:rapid:535541270521708566>.
⬥ <:edracogloves:1179717613769728100> / <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> means equip <:edracogloves:1179717613769728100> for a tick and switch back to  <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> immediately.
    • Flicking <:edracogloves:1179717613769728100> / <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> manipulates charging timer to benefit from <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> and <:fleetingboots:789813993480388640> whilst still getting increased <:dracolichinfusion:1285348459876057138> duration.
⬥ Drink 1 <:zamorakbrew:841409588632092694> before the fight and sip throughout to keep your hp below max.
⬥ If you get jumps <:dive:1049378668197195808> SW for <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> placement during P3. If you jump skip lure Kerapac SW before <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> P3.
⬥ stall <:incend:535541258429661215> at <:war:1005552206302416986> and bring the stall into instance whilst using <:dive:1049378668197195808> / <:invokelordofbones:1176968330582700174> / <:darkness:1137809209782698024>.
