**__Introduction__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

The third installment in the Elite Dungeon series, the Shadow Reef hosts Kranon, the Ambassador <:Ambassador:583430542809235458> as its final boss, which drops pieces for the T92 <:ecb:615618531937222657>. The usage of Greater Concentrated Blast <:gconc:869285393223254107> and Greater Chain <:gchaincaroming:867678153882861568> abilities are assumed.

.
Each solo run of ED3 is worth <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Bosses!E568$ in Eldritch pieces alone.

During group encounters each player can roll an Eldritch piece individually.
Solo - (180/10,000) Duo - (90/10,000) Trio - (60/10,000)

.

> __**Kranon, the Ambassador**__
.tag:ambassador
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/9uU19GX.jpg

At the bottom of the abyssal section awaits Kranon, the Ambassador, the final boss of the Elite Dungeon 3. This boss has a moderate learning curve compared to most solo bosses and has a variety of mechanics ranging from DPS checks, area denial, self-healing, and the usage multiple combat styles.

The boss primarily attacks with <:range:580168050121113623>, with a <:Magic:689504724159823906> after every 5 autos. The mage hit is replaced by a small but instant <:melee:1096130867279171706> swipe if the player is in melee distance.

⬥ Try to maximize the potential healing from <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> Spinners and the Seiryu Heal at 400k to cut down on food usage throughout the fight.

⬥ You can use <:SBS:543875425055670275> <:disrupt:535614336207552523> throughout the fight.

⬥ Flanking <:flank4:712073088296157185> can be utilized throughout the fight by stepping directly underneath the Ambi.
    • This is less applicable if using <:soa:869284271595069451>

⬥ Drop purple smokes at the edges of your <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> and run to the other edge after (example of position below).
