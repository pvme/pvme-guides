> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
The Kalphite King (KK) is a powerful kalphite found within the Exiled Kalphite Hive just north of Menaphos. KK can use all three attack styles, but he will only use one style at a time with a set attack rotation for each style. At any time throughout the fight, KK can randomly change attack style, starting a new attack rotation.

KK is a significant step up in difficulty in comparison to other mid-tier bosses, requiring close tracking of auto attacks and specs, and the use of specific abilites at certain times to dodge one-hit mechanics.

Being on a Kalphite slayer task is recommended while fighting Kalphite King, as you gain the benefits of the Slayer Helm <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792>, Genocidal <:genocidal:689503091539705870> perk, and TMW <:tmwbody:536966366272552960> set effect.

> __**Player Stun Timings**__
.tag:playerstun
.
Stunning KK at the right time is essential, allowing you skip certain mechanics that either waste time (charge & dig) or result in a death/failed kill (Green).

.
**__Range Phase: Skips Green__**
2 Autos → Stun → 2 Autos → **Your Stun** → 2 Autos → Dig → Repeat

.
**__Melee Phase: Skips Charge__**
2 Autos → Push → 2 Autos → **Your Stun** → 2 Autos → Green → Repeat

.
**__Mage Phase: Skips Dig__**
4 Autos → **Your Stun** → 2 Autos → Charge → Repeat

.

> __**Strategies and Tips**__
.tag:strats
**__General__**
⬥ Anticipate <:anti:535541306475151390> before and Berserk <:zerk:535532854004678657> at the start of every kill.
⬥ Never stun outside of proper stun timings.
⬥ Stay close to the tank during digs but not in the surface radius.
⬥ Run with the boss during charges.
⬥ Anticipate <:anti:535541306475151390> or Freedom <:freedom:535541258240786434> at the start of Range <:range:580168050121113623> and Magic <:Magic:689504724159823906> phases, use the other defensive when your first runs out.
⬥ Don’t stand directly between KK and a wall during melee or mage phase or you will be hit up to 5k.

.
**__Melee Form__** <:Attack:689509250472476758>
⬥ Use Freedom <:freedom:535541258240786434> on melee phase only after you receive both bleeds. If you <:freedom:535541258240786434> a Dismember <:dismember:535532879376023572> before you get Slaughtered <:slaughter:535532879237873666> be prepared to take hefty damage, especially if you get pushed.

.
**__Ranged Form__** <:range:580168050121113623>
⬥ The amount of times you get hit by the standard attack while the Incendiary Shot icon <:incend:535541258429661215> is on your buff bar is the amount of times you will be hit by the Incendiary Shot <:incend:535541258429661215>.
⬥ If you get a stun with the Incendiary Shot <:incend:535541258429661215> after green, don’t freedom. Either wait until the stun clears and GBarge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> to KK when he surfaces or GBarge <:gbarge:535532879250456578> to minions to clear and then Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> back to KK.

.
**__Magic Form__** <:Magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ Use Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857> during the first 4 autos of this phase so you can continue to attack the boss without moving.
⬥ Anticipate <:anti:535541306475151390> during charge.
⬥ Resonance <:res:535541258844635148> the blue ball.
    • Usually happens the attack after charge if you forget how to count.

.
> __**What you must avoid**__
.tag:nop
**__General__**
⬥ Never use Smoke Tendrils <:Smoke_Tendrils:536257336130404372>, Unload <:unload:535541258664411169>, or Frenzy <:frenzy:535532879279554581>.
⬥ Attack minions at a time where the Kalphite King is still attackable.
⬥ Never stun outside of proper stun timings. If you do you could cause:
    • Early greens.
    • Early charges.
    • Early digs.
    • Skipped attacks.
    • A sped-up attack rotation.
⬥ Never stun during a Green. If you do you could cause:
    • Multiple greens.
    • Early digs.
    • Multiple digs.
⬥ Never Destroy <:destroy:535532879330148352> for longer than 1 GCD or stun immediately after a dig or charge. If you do you could cause:
    • Early greens.
    • Multiple greens.
    • Early digs.
    • Infinite digs.

.
**__Ranged Form__** <:range:580168050121113623>
⬥ Never use Berserk <:zerk:535532854004678657> during the middle of a range phase rotation. Wait for the cycle to reset after dig.

.
**__Magic Form__** <:Magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ Never use Berserk <:zerk:535532854004678657> during mage phase unless the boss spawns in form.
