> **__Strategy and Tips__**
.tag:strategy
⬥ This strategy relies heavily on having <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> active and keeping your <:hellhoundpouch:921410226475925514> alive by using <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> or <:hellhoundscroll:922417969731088435>.
    • *Note: These have to be used manually and cannot be auto-cast.*

⬥ You will still need to deal with the mechanics correctly, as due to the nature of the guide, you will not be skipping many, if any.

⬥ You are free to use <:chaosroar:994644356671737966> before <:cane:535532878969438210> or <:dragonclaw:779048041088024606> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>.
    • Be sure that your barged ability DoT has ended before doing so, as it will consume the <:chaosroar:994644356671737966> buff.

⬥ Abyssal Scourge is extremely important due to it's passive effect *(Abyssal Parasite)* that stacks up to 50 times, dealing increasing bleed damage with each stack. This will contribute heavily to your <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:VampScrim:513201294262009874> healing.

⬥ Using <:tmwbody:536966366272552960> (10-11kph) armour will allow you to easily out-heal Raksha's attacks due to it's passive effect; resulting in potentially no-food kills as shown in the example kill below.

⬥ Using <:vestmentsofhavoctop:994189295592161291> (13-18kph) armour will allow you to kill Raksha faster due to the increased adrenaline gain and of course the tier 112 damage. The downsides to this is taking a lot more damage which will need to be offset by eating food or occassionally pray flicking.
