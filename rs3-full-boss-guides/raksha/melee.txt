### Overview
⬥ There are 2 setups available:
    • Normal prayers with <:zealots:926021352626982942> - More consistent but harder to execute and survive.
    • Curses - Less consistent but easier to execute and survive.
⬥ **Do not** use <:darkness:1137809209782698024> as it will mess up rotation due to <:divert:787904334377648130> splashing.
⬥ Use <:veng:543478434953822208> throughout the fight.

## __Rotation__
.tag:rotations
Use this consistent rotation for standard farming of the boss. If done properly, expect kills around 1:15-1:25.

.
