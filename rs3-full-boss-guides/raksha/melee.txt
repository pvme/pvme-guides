## __Rotation__
.tag:rotations
Use this consistent rotation for standard farming of the boss. If done properly, expect kills around 1:40 / 27kph

⬥ All bleeds are applied using <:mwspear:694566917456789554>
⬥ <:Veng:543478434953822208> is used opportunistically, although not explicitly stated.
⬥ Having <:zemouregalsnexus:1179716261681307728> equipped for 9 seconds increases the level of your <:greaterboneshield:1137809118283976814> which increases the adrenaline you receive from <:divert:787904334377648130>

.
### Pre-kill
<:DBA:603979368850653216> <:spec:537340400273195028> → get 120% adrenaline.

*Notes:*
*⬥ After first <:surge:535533810004262912> you use both <:prep:535541258546970624> and <:surge:535533810004262912> at the same time to get target cycle on the tick from global cooldown right after <:divert:787904334377648130>*
*⬥ Sometimes in P2 after <:lengmh:883134308146098227> <:spec:537340400273195028> you might be able to skip a basic or two and go straight for bleeds. This depends on how many stacks of <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> you had in order to save adrenaline.*
