## __Introduction__
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

Raksha is a dinosaur dubbed the "Shadow Colossus" which is located in Anachronia. Raksha can be fought either solo or duo, with linear hp scaling for duo mode. The drop rates for duo are also exactly the same as solo.

Each Raksha kill is worth <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:gpkill_raksha$, with <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:gpkill_raksha_commons$ of that coming from commons.

This guide assumes you are already familiar with Raksha's mechanics, phasing, and attack rotations. If not, please read <#1020559920669663304>.
