## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
The [Fight Kiln](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Fight_Kiln>) is a wave-based encounter which requires completion of [The Elder Kiln](<https://runescape.wiki/w/The_Elder_Kiln>) quest and a one-time sacrifice of a fire cape. There are 37 waves which become increasingly difficult as the fight progresses.

⬥ Completing the fight rewards you with a cape for your most used style <:kilnmage:1153768065427853354> / <:KilnMelee:602582045553590274> / <:kilnnecro:1153772314215858247> / <:KilnRanged:602582045541269567>
    • Each cape can be used to craft its respective igneous variant <:igneouskalmej:1153772938252787793> / <:igneouskalket:902209626404192316> / <:igneouskalmor:1139200072438005851> / <:igneouskalxil:902209626404171786>
        ⬩ These are combined into the <:igneouskalzuk:902209626479685734>

.
### General Wave Breakdown
⬥ 1-9 are mostly weak to <:Attack:689509250472476758>
⬥ 11-19 are mostly weak to <:range:580168050121113623>
⬥ 21-29 are mostly weak to <:Magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ 31 is weak to <:Attack:689509250472476758>
⬥ 32 is weak to <:Magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ 33 is weak to <:range:580168050121113623>

## __Preset Suggestions__
.tag:presets

⬥ Gear and inventory can get switched around based on what you have and how experienced you are
    • As long as you have a pickaxe in your toolbelt, you will be able to kill Dills using your Melee weapon
⬥ Obsidian armour reduces damage taken in the Fight Kiln and saves inventory space if not bringing switches
    • Tri-bridding is the best way to deal with the Fight Kiln. At the minimum, you want a 2H weapon of every style
⬥ <:tokkulzo:975012160759550042> provides a 10% damage boost against the mobs but requires tokkul to upkeep
    • Bring a <:rov:513201065877831680> if the passive version is not unlocked

## __The Fight__
.tag:thefight
### Waves 1-9
.tag:waves1-10
There will be mostly rangers with an increasing number of magers and meleers so use Melee and switch to kill the others
⬥ **Wave 2**: Invulnerability crystal spawn
⬥ **Wave 4**: Restoration crystal spawn
⬥ **Wave 5**: Dill spawn
⬥ **Wave 9**: All rangers
    • Use <:zerk:535532854004678657> on the last ranger to have it for Jad

.
### Wave 10
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/cibB2kx.jpg
⬥ **Jad** spawns **southwest**
⬥ 1 ranger also spawns southeast

.
### Wave 11-19
.tag:waves11-20
There will mostly be magers with an increasing number of meleers and rangers so use Ranged and switch to kill the others
⬥ **Wave 14**: Invulnerability crystal spawn
⬥ **Wave 16**: Restoration crystal spawn
⬥ **Wave 19**: Dill spawn (switch to Melee for Dill)
    • Go northeast, kill the Dill then go southwest and <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> killing all the magers, use remainder on Jad

.
### Wave 20
⬥ **Jad** spawns **southwest**
⬥ 1 mager also spawns southeast

.
### Waves 21-29
.tag:waves21-30
There will mostly be meleers with an increasing number of magers and rangers so use Magic and switch to kill the others
⬥ **Wave 26**: Invulnerability crystal spawn
⬥ **Wave 28**: Restoration crystal spawn
⬥ **Wave 28**: 6 Dills spawn with a mager (kill mager then swap to Melee)
⬥ **Wave 29**: <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> and kill meleers, use remainder on Jad

.
### Wave 30
⬥ **Jad** spawns **southwest**
⬥ 3 meleers spawn in other corners

.
### Waves 31-33
.tag:waves31-36
⬥ **Wave 31**: Use Melee
⬥ **Wave 32**: Use Magic
⬥ **Wave 33**: Use Ranged

.
### Wave 34
⬥ **Jad** spawns **southeast**
⬥ 1 meleer spawns northwest, and one ranger southwest

.
### Wave 35
⬥ **Jad** spawns **northeast**
⬥ 1 meleer spawns northwest, 1 ranger southwest, and 1 mager southeast

.
### Wave 36
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/hLsQnEw.png
⬥ **Double Jads** spawn in **both southern corners**
⬥ Use invulnerability crystals here

.
### Wave 37 (Har-Aken)
.tag:wave37haraken
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/n4B2Z2KLAw.png
⬥ Spawns tentacles that deal Magic or Ranged hits. Build/heal on these when he is not on the surface
⬥ He never spawns on northern side of the platform
⬥ Spawns after ~72 seconds and is up for ~30 seconds
    • Use <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> / <:zerk:535532854004678657> / <:livingdeath:1159434908486357072> / <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> as he surfaces
    • Apply <:Vuln:537349530551582720> / <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858>
    • Spam <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787> if <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714> with <:ripperscroll:703581275155464203>

.
## __Tips and Tricks__
.tag:tips
### General
⬥ You can use restoration crystals in emergencies and to extend <:onsl:513190159085207555> on Har-Aken if needed
    • Make sure you have <:signoflife:1005552211847295076> up just in case because the hits start to add up very quickly
⬥ Use <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> for the majority of the fight and <:deflectmage:544195487926845462> / <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> / <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> as needed, don't forget <:devo:513190158728953857>
    • On wave 19 there are a lot of magers so using <:devo:513190158728953857> can help
⬥ Always use <:deflectmage:544195487926845462> / <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> / <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> when fighting Jads
⬥ Invulnerability crystals do not protect you from Dill special attacks, instead use restoration crystals

.
### Speed
⬥ Make sure to take advantage of the combat triangle whenever possible
⬥ Use <:tokkulzo:975012160759550042> if you have excess tokkul as that will be your best in slot ring if you are able to sustain it
⬥ It is helpful to use <:LantaSticks:565726489404899368>, <:KwuarmSticks:565726489341984779>, <:SpiritSticks:565726489136463894> for increased potion duration, weapon poison damage, and scrolls used per hour

.
