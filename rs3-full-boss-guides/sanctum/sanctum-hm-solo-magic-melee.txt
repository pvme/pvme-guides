## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
⬥ This is an advanced guide and assumes a deep understanding of the **boss's rotations and mechanics** for the **Hard Mode** version of the Sanctum of Rebirth, found in <#1267830757213212766>.

⬥ Only in Hard Mode is the [Shard of Genesis Essence](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Shard_of_Genesis_Essence>) <:shardofgenesisessence:1266418218264694954> dropped, which upgrades Tier 95 weapons to Tier 100.

⬥ 4TAA is used throughout this guide. <:icebarrage:537340400185245701> + ability is assumed to use with the 2h.

⬥ This guide is a compilation of transcribed Sanctum of Rebirth boss kills. In them, improvisation was heavily utilized. Improvisation when attempting to perform these rotations should be expected.
