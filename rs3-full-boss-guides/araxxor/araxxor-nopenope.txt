### Preset Notes

⬥ It is recommended to have the *NopeNopeNope* <:nopenopenope:1006123123994808370> perk from Player Owned Farms active.

⬥ Upkeep the <:kalgscroll:841409588954923049> buff from your familiar. Can do so by setting the familiar scroll autofire to every 30s, which means the Kalg demon will automatically use the special buff, giving the player 60 seconds of an extra +5% crit buff.

⬥ Araxxor takes a few seconds to clear vulns on phase 1-3, making vuln bombs <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> usable on all phases. Araxxi, however, doesn't clear vulns as quickly in phase 4, making it important to quickly get the encounter over with.

⬥ Preferred the Ranged variant of Araxxor because it's autos only decrease the anti-poison timer rather than overload timer.

    *• Can do this by going into an instanced encounter and equipping any melee weapon (e.g. <:nymorablade:643161612819365888>) before Araxxor spawns.*

⬥ The perks for the armor are <:biting4:712073087809617931>, <:invig4:712073087859949570> + <:mob:689501908628799488> for the <:firstnecrorobetop:1206664820682657822>, <:crack4:712073087662686249> + <:relentless5:712244800920748092>, <:enhdev4:712073087507628035> for the <:firstnecrorobebottom:1206664822654115911>. The perks for the weapons are <:as4:712074245202772009> for the <:omniguard:1138809234922934282> and <:p6:712073088769982475> for the <:soulboundlantern:1138809230573441064>.

⬥ If no <:mob:689501908628799488> perk on any armor or equipment, then switch <:deathward:697808774068699286> or <:fontoflife:698225967679930408> relic for <:shadowsgrace:895999229737185280> relic.

### Top Path (Take this path for P3 skip)
.tag:top
⬥ **Phase 1**

    • This phase is timegated so it can be used to set yourself up for phase 2 while you deal damage.
    • You will need to improvise a lot of your set up based on stacks gained and damage done.
    • It is very important to not use <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> + <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642> in Phase 1 as you will not have the stacks for the skip.
        ⬩ Due to this you have a lack of damage aside from the start + <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892> which is also good for <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks.
    • Can delay <:invokelordofbones:1176968330582700174> → <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> → <:lifetransfer:1137809128136388819> until about 0:20.
    • Eat some <:blueblubber:689530593742291033> or take a <:brew:565726489400573962> if needed, or <:excal:641337999170207763> to replenish some hp.
    • Try to have near or at 100% adrenaline in order to use <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> and have enough adrenaline for <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>.

Set up:
<:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:omniguard:1138809234922934282> <:spec:537340400273195028> → <:necroauto:1137809137401602109> → <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → build 12 <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> stacks + 5 <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks

At 12 <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> stacks + 5 <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks, <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> → filler → <:commandghost:1159434409913634816>

.
