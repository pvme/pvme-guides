__**Notes**__
⬥ It is recommended to have the *NopeNopeNope* perk from Player Owned Farms active.
⬥ Use a Kalgerion Demon <:kalgpouch:921410226484301925> + <:kalgscroll:841409588954923049> for your familiar.
⬥ Araxxor takes a few seconds to clear vulns on phase 1-3, making vuln bombs <:vulnbomb:655341074235129858> usable on all phases. Use them at the start of each rotation and after the third ability.

> __**Advanced Guide**__
.tag:top
**__Top Path__**
⬥ **Phase 1**
    • This phase is timegated so it can be used to set yourself up for phase 2 while you deal damage.
    • You will need to improvise a lot of your set up based on stacks gained and damage done.
    • It is very important to not use <:fingerofdeath:1137809115066937414> + <:volleyofsouls:1137809182364540989> in Phase 1 as you will not have the stacks for the skip.
        ⬩ Due to this you have a lack of damage aside from the start + <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892> which is also good for <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks.

<:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:omniguard:1138809234922934282> <:spec:537340400273195028> → <:necroauto:1137809137401602109> → <:deathguard90:1138809243143766118> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> → <:anti:535541306475151390> → <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → build 12 <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> stacks + 5 <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> stacks

.
⬥ **Phase 2 & 3**
    • The ideal method is to bring Araxxor directly into phase 3, through the usage of a Powerburst of Acceleration <:accel:756236265472524418>. It can be used any time after 0:57.

<:kalgscroll:841409588954923049> + <:surge:535533810004262912> + <:dive:1049378668197195808> → <:surge:535533810004262912> + <:accel:756236265472524418> + <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> → <:surge:535533810004262912> +  <:dive:1049378668197195808> → <:dive:1049378668197195808> + <:surge:535533810004262912> → <:dive:1049378668197195808> → (tc) + <:deathskulls:1137809106640572583> → <:fingerofdeath:1137809115066937414> → <:volleyofsouls:1137809182364540989> → <:fingerofdeath:1137809115066937414> if needed

.
⬥ *Notes:*
    • *The first <:surge:535533810004262912> <:bd:535532854281764884> should be executed when web burn reads 99%.*
    • *Ensure to trigger player movement on every tick along with your mobility abilities.*
        ⬩ *Clicking on an enlarged minimap may be helpful in facilitating movement and <:bd:535532854281764884> placements across the path.*
.
**__Middle Path__**
.tag:mid
⬥ **Phase 1**
    • No special setup. Just dps araxxor down to <5k hp.

.
⬥ **Phase 2**
    • Just dps araxxor down to <5k hp.
    • When you go to the top of the ramp, place a <:dummy:656844963522281473> + (tc) + dismiss dummy → press any basic ability keykind → <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> → <:conjurezombie:1137809200219684924> → <:commandghost:1137809196864262154> → <:conjureskeleton:1137809204112011274>

.
⬥ **Phase 3**

<:invokedeath:1137809121983336548> + <:surge:535533810004262912> + run to the edge of the arena near Phase 4 → (tc) + <:deathskulls:1137809106640572583> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → <:touchofdeath:1137809175980810380> →  <:fingerofdeath:1137809115066937414> → <:soulsap:1137809140476031057> → <:volleyofsouls:1137809182364540989>

**__Bottom Path__**
.tag:bot

.
