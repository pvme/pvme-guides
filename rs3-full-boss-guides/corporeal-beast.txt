## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
The Corporeal Beast is a boss unlocked from completing the [Summer's End](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Summer's_End>) quest. Summoning familiars cannot be used at this boss, however a legendary pet can be used here.

## __General__
.tag:general
Any weapon that isn't a **Spear** will deal half damage, so **Melee** is the recommended style that should be used at this boss.

⬥ It Is recommended to teleport out <:warsretreatteleport:1032540079589163049> after every kill to reset Abilities, Health and Prayer.
