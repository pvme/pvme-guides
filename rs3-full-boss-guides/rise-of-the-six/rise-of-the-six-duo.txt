## __Useful Unlocks__
.tag:unlocks
⬥ Double <:surge:535533810004262912> and <:bd:535532854281764884> / <:dive:1049378668197195808> unlocked is crucial for high kph. They are important in exiting tunnels efficiently.
    • Equipment with the <:mob:689501908628799488> should also be used for this purpose.
⬥ Equipment with <:undeadslayerperk:689502804720615441> and <:undeadslayer:641339922019516416> for a flat 7% and 15% (for 10 seconds) respectively.
    • Both the perk and sigil work against the brothers.
    • Take note that <:salveamulet:797899945730244648> and <:ghosthunterhelm:855865893808767016> do now work against the brothers.
    • You can now get an Undead slayer task and <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> will work against the brothers.

## __The Fight/Strategy__
.tag:info
⬥ <:persistentrage:739965637056659567> is a very crucial part of RotS, this relic allows you to start every kill at full adrenaline without <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> and <:dummy:656844963522281473>
⬥ Ahrim takes 50% reduced damage from Necromancy <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> whilst flying. You have 2 ticks to do damage before all your damage is reduced.
⬥ You can take Extreme Necromancy potions and Berserk Blood Essence <:berserkerbloodess:1132205017546358794> which will make 2 global cooldown kills more consistent as this will boost your Necromancy <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> level to 160.
⬥ At RotS the meta is to AoE the brothers down as fast as possible. Most of the meta and strategy revolve around stacking and luring the brothers so that they can be killed at the same time.
