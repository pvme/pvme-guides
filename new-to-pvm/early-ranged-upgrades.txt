## __Frequently asked questions__
**⬥ Some Dual-Wield weapons have more damage bonus than others. Are they better?**
    • No. When using abilities, weapons of the same tier deal the same amount of damage. Weapon speed and damage bonus together make it deal identical ability damage in the end.
    • You can safely mix different types of main hand and off-hand weapons.
**⬥ Why do I prefer crossbows to bows?**
    • At this stage in the game, crossbows are stronger than bows because of the enchanted effects on bolts.
    • This reverses when arrows have better effects in the later game.
**⬥ When should I change ammo?**
    • Damage is capped at the tier of the bow or the tier of the arrow - whichever is lowest. As you upgrade your bow/crossbow, you should also upgrade your ammo.
**⬥ Can I just train slayer?**
    • You certainly can if you want some variety. It is, however, slower to train at lower combat levels.
**⬥ Why are weapons upgrades more important than armour upgrades? / Why is power armour recommended over tank armour?**
    • Frankly put: You gain more from hitting harder than from getting hit less. A higher tier weapon lets you hit harder by increasing hit chance and damage bonus. Power armour increases damage bonus too. This lets you hit harder and kill more monsters, which means more xp gained per hour.
