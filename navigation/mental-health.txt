**Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.**

Other aspects of our life such as addictions, grief, stress, relationships, and identity impact our mental health greatly and it is important to take care of these, or seek help when needed.

Many people struggle with these problems and are far too afraid to seek help. So please if you ever feel like you ever need someone, or anyone to talk to below are some resources to help you.

__**Our Commitments**__
.tag:disc
Our Encyclopedia is dedicated to helping our readers realize their full potential in PvM, never at the cost of one's mental health or sanity. The nature of the game favours grinds, and the level of social bond our server and Discord itself provides makes it an easy distraction for the duties we are bound to in real life.

Our commitment to providing the tools for your well-being and benefit aren't limited to providing health resources through this channel. It is possible to request a self-mute, self-kick, or self-ban if you believe this can help with concentration with real life matters. The action taken is **not** a punishment and can be reverted at any time on request.

Happy gaming 'Scapers, best wishes from the PvM Encyclopedia team!
