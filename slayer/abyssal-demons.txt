> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Abyssal demons offer high experience rates while being completely afk. Being that they don't deal much damage, they're a great option for lower level players.

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 85
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 278
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~2600 <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~2400 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>, ~2100 <:melee:1096130867279171706><:range:580168050121113623>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~723k <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~667k <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>, ~584k <:melee:1096130867279171706><:range:580168050121113623>

> **__Desirable drops__**
.tag:drops
⬥ Abyssal Demons drop the Abyssal whip <:abyssalwhip:860891494805536769>, wand <:abyssalwand:841409588590805043> and orb <:abyssalorb:860891495041073152> which are required for the Slayer Log. They also drop alchables and hard clues at a decent rate.
⬥ If killed in the Wilderness, they can also drop <:revenantdropenhancer:1006481325076787240> which can be helpful for Ultimate Slayer hunters.

> **__Location and How to Get there__**
.tag:location
.
The best spot for abyssal demons is located at the level 18 Wilderness obelisk (Graveyard of Shadows). The fastest way to reach the location is using the portable obelisk or an obelisk shard.

⬥ Place cannon <:cannon:869283142350041098> on yellow tile as shown below (2E of stump NE of obelisk) and stand on yellow X (2N and 2W of stump SE of obelisk).

⬥ If your character gets walked off the marked tile, step back on it, and click the nearest demon.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/MtWr7YkgwP.png

> **__Method - AFK (All styles)__**
.tag:method1
Due to their relatively low HP and defences, abyssal demons can be AFK'd with all styles. Demon slayer circlet and boots <:demonslayercirclet:1004325697503907910> <:demonslayerboots:1004325691510231041> can be taken on all styles if owned for a 8% damage boost (4% each piece).

Before loading your cannon and starting the session, check that:
⬥ Elder overload <:elderovl:841419289831800882> and weapon poison +++ <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active.
