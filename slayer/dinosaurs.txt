> **__Dinosaurs__**
.
**__Introduction__**
Mid-level players will kill dinosaurs for the Dinosaur tooth and Dinosaur rib bone - both of which are required to full upgrade the Anachronia base camp. End-game players will kill these for slayer logs. It is recommended to kill Venemous Dinosaurs if your <:slayer:797896049548066857> level is high enough as they are the best in every conceivable way.
.
> **__Venomous Dinosaurs__ (Courtesy of <@212008790944514048>, <@191844571024457728> and <@645045935587721216>)**

.
**__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Venomous dinosaurs are the go-to for dinosaur tasks as they have the highest kills per hour of any dinosaur.

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill : 1378 + 454.7 <:Constitution:689509250887712902>

⬥ Optimal kills per hour: 480
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour : ~640k

**__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:locationandhowtogetthere
Venomous Dinosaurs are located on the northwestern part of Anachronia. They can be accessed by 3 methods, arranged fastest to slowest:
⬥ **[1]** Anachronia Teleport to a Totem of the Abyss placed on the north totem pedestal (red path)
⬥ **[2]** Lodestone teleport to Anachronia (keyboard shortcut "Alt + D"), run to the Orthen teleportation device, teleport to Outpost (keyboard shortcut "O"), and run west (blue path)
⬥ **[3]** Lodestone teleport to Anachronia, and run north (yellow path)


.

> **__Melee__**
.tag:melee
⬥ Up to 450 KPH

Venomous Dinosaurs hit with melee auto-attacks. To prevent taking too much damage, <:enhdev4:712073087507628035> is helpful, as you will be praying <:DeflectMelee:544195488447201300> . If using the Corbicula Rex <:corbicula:690136117273821280> perk from the Ranch out of Time, excess adrenaline can be used in the form of the <:zgs:626465964325601290> <:spec:537340400273195028> or <:dragonhalberd:841409589261238282> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> if actively slaying, rotating between these greatly increases the damage dealt.

.
