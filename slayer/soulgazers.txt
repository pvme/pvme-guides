**__Soulgazers__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Soulgazers <:soulgazer:1004107315521257572> can be killed in the Wilderness, the stalker dungeon in Zamorak's hideout, and the Sunken Pyramid. The Sunken Pyramid is easier to loot in and has no cannonball cost, but the Wilderness spawns can drop <:revenantdropenhancer:1006481325076787240>

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 99
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1950.4
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~400 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~780k <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> (AFK)

> **__Location and How to Get there__**
.tag:location
⬥ Wilderness (level 27 Wilderness)
    • Place and load the <:cannon:869283142350041098> in the open area a few tiles east of the lava pool
    • Northeast of the level 18 obelisk, accessible via <:portableobelisk:1137735861937655900>
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/qGhOmREHX2.png
⬥ Sunken Pyramid (south-eastern room, 5 spawns)
    • In the small room, position yourself six tiles south of the northern statue (northeastern corner of the brazier's shadow)
    • You can teleport there with a teleport to house tablet, or with a Slayer Codex <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>

.
> **__Method: Necromancy__**
.tag:method1
Soulgazers can be effectively AFK'd in the Sunken Pyramid and the Wilderness using Necromancy due to its high damage output and sustain from <:commandghost:1159434409913634816> and <:vampaura:643505653079343144>

⬥ Elite soulgazers should be focused when they spawn as they have KO potential when ignored
⬥ Pray <:deflectmage:544195487926845462> and <:sorrow:1137941003895046284>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:ancientshard:656426717505650708> and <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882>, <:weppoison:689525476158472288> and <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581> are active at all times, drinking a <:superprayerrenewal:547603714681339915> dose whenever you drink a weapon poison
    • Soulgazer's in the Wilderness are permanently aggressive, so <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581> is **not** required
