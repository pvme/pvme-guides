> __**Abyssal Creatures Courtesy of <@159421704270118912> and <@145884917627224065>**__
**__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
⬥ Abyssal Creatures consist of abyssal demons (85 <:slayer:797896049548066857>) and creatures introduced in the Senntisten Asylum, consisting of:
    • Abyssal Savage (95 <:slayer:797896049548066857>)
    • Abyssal Beast (105 <:slayer:797896049548066857>)
    • Abyssal Lord (115 <:slayer:797896049548066857>)

⬥ All abyssal creatures have teleporting mechanics and can mostly be AFK'd, although the gear requirement to AFK abyssal lords <:abyssallord:1004107327126904842> is quite high.

⬥ This guide is primarily based off the video guide by <@159421704270118912>, with setups for Abyssal Beasts <:abyssalbeast:1004107328964014100>/Savages <:abyssalsavage:1004107330650124462> based off <@145884917627224065>'s preset which can be found in the Clue Chasers discord.

⬥ When you can afford to lose some accuracy and do not require <:animatedead:856635090453135382>, <:demonslayercirclet:1004325697503907910> and <:demonslayerboots:1004325691510231041> and be worn for a 8% damage boost.

⬥ All creatures are classed as demons which means <:demonslayerperk:689502842653900818> and <:jasdemonbanearrow:971025697055334491> work against them.

.
> **__Desirable Drops__**
⬥ Abyssal demons drop the Abyssal whip <:abyssalwhip:860891494805536769>, wand <:abyssalwand:841409588590805043> and orb <:abyssalorb:860891495041073152> which are required for the Slayer Log. They also drop alchables and hard clues decent rate.

⬥ All 3 abyssal creatures found in the Senntisten Asylum drop Abyssal Flesh <:abyssalflesh:947871842520170576>, required to craft Abyssal armour spikes <:abyssalarmourspikesalloy:947871842541129768>
    • Abyssal Beasts drop the Jaws of the Abyss <:jawsoftheabyss:947871842595639306>, a T85 melee power helmet.
    • Abyssal Lords drop the Abyssal Scourge <:abyssalscourge:947871842469834832>, a T92 melee main-hand weapon.
        - Both of these are required for the slayer log.

.
> **__Location and How to Get There__**
⬥ Viable spots to kill Abyssal Demons <:abyssaldemon:1004107332478840873> includes:
    • Morytania Slayer Tower (via <:dgcape:976732922545586246>/Ring of Slaying)
        - You need to cross a plank that requires 71 <:agility:1039864819760758844> if doing them on the top floor (best AFK KPH spot)
        - You can pick up a slayer contract from Markus for some additional XP or GP
    • The Wilderness (Graveyard of Shadows)
        - You will benefit from <:infernalpuzzlebox:994189299681607800>
    • Kuradel's dungeon (only on task).
        - They can be killed extremely quickly here if using the Kuradel's Favour sigil.

⬥ Abyssal Savages, Beasts and Lords are located in the Senntisten Asylum. Teleport using the Pontifex Shadow Ring <:pontifexshadowring:870326852361789501> and run north and then east past the Ful front to access it (image below).
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/CkxGGdg.png
.
⬥ The Mask of the Abyss <:maskoftheabyss:1004325563068071936>/Helm of Warping can be used to teleport to all areas containing Abyssal creatures.

> **__Abyssal Savages/Beasts__**
.tag:beasts
Abyssal beasts are relatively low-hitting abyssal creatures that can be done as an abyssal demons task before acquiring the gear or level needed for abyssal lords. They provide a good profit and a good elite clue drop rate.

On the other hand, Abyssal savages serve as a good pre-105 slayer alternative, offering high combat exp and <:abyssalflesh:947871842520170576> drop rates.

.

> **__Abyssal Lords__**
.tag:lords
Abyssal lords are by far the best abyssal creatures task for both exp and gp. Due to their high damage and defence, Magic with <:animatedead:856635090453135382>, a T90+ staff, and T90 tank armour <:achtoprimevalhelm:641346213316395019> / <:cryptbloomhelm:892819107123195956> should be used to do this task.

**Refer to Omid's video guide for other viable gear combinations.**
