> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
⬥ Abyssal Creatures consist of Abyssal demons (85 <:slayer:797896049548066857>) and creatures introduced in the Senntisten Asylum, consisting of:
    • Abyssal Savage (95 <:slayer:797896049548066857>)
    • Abyssal Beast (105 <:slayer:797896049548066857>)
    • Abyssal Lord (115 <:slayer:797896049548066857>)

⬥ All Abyssal creatures have teleporting mechanics and can mostly be AFK'd, although the gear requirement to AFK Abyssal lords <:abyssallord:1004107327126904842> is quite high.

⬥ This guide is primarily based off the video guide by <@159421704270118912>, with setups for Abyssal Beasts <:abyssalbeast:1004107328964014100>/Savages <:abyssalsavage:1004107330650124462> based off <@145884917627224065>'s preset which can be found in the Clue Chasers discord.

⬥ If you can afford to lose some accuracy and do not require <:animatedead:856635090453135382>, <:demonslayercirclet:1004325697503907910> and <:demonslayerboots:1004325691510231041> and be worn for a 8% damage boost.

⬥ All Abyssal creatures are classified as demons which means <:demonslayerperk:689502842653900818> and <:jasdemonbanearrow:971025697055334491> work against them.

.
> **__Desirable Drops__**
.tag:drops
⬥ Abyssal Demons drop the Abyssal whip <:abyssalwhip:860891494805536769>, wand <:abyssalwand:841409588590805043> and orb <:abyssalorb:860891495041073152> which are required for the Slayer Log. They also drop alchables and hard clues at a decent rate.

⬥ All 3 Abyssal creatures found in the Senntisten Asylum drop Abyssal Flesh <:abyssalflesh:947871842520170576>, required to craft Abyssal armour spikes <:abyssalarmourspikesalloy:947871842541129768>
    • Abyssal Beasts drop the Jaws of the Abyss <:jawsoftheabyss:947871842595639306>, a T85 melee power helmet.
    • Abyssal Lords drop the Abyssal Scourge <:abyssalscourge:947871842469834832>, a T92 melee main-hand weapon.
        ⬩ Both of these are required for the slayer log.

.
> **__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:location
⬥ Viable spots to kill Abyssal Demons <:abyssaldemon:1004107332478840873> include:
**    • The Wilderness (Graveyard of Shadows)
        ⬩ You will benefit from <:infernalpuzzlebox:994189299681607800>
        ⬩ This is the location used for this guide, as it offers the highest KPH.**
    • Morytania Slayer Tower (via <:dgcape:976732922545586246>/Ring of Slaying)
        ⬩ You need to cross a plank that requires 71 <:agility:1039864819760758844> if doing them on the top floor (best AFK KPH spot)
        ⬩ You can pick up a slayer contract from Markus for some additional XP or GP.
    • Kuradal's dungeon (only on task).
        ⬩ They can be killed extremely quickly here if using the Kuradal's Favour sigil.

⬥ Abyssal Savages, Beasts and Lords are located in the Senntisten Asylum. Teleport using the Pontifex Shadow Ring <:pontifexshadowring:870326852361789501> and run north and then east past the Ful front to access it (image below).
.

.
⬥ The Mask of the Abyss <:maskoftheabyss:1004325563068071936>/Helm of Warping can be used to teleport to all areas containing Abyssal creatures.

**__Location and how to get there__**
The best spot for abyssal demons is located at the level 18 Wilderness obelisk (Graveyard of Shadows). The fastest way to reach the location is using the portable obelisk or an obelisk shard.

.
**__Where to stand__**
⬥ Place cannon <:cannon:869283142350041098> on red X as shown below (2E of stump NE of obelisk) and stand on yellow X (2N and 2W of stump SE of obelisk).

⬥ If your character gets walked off the marked tile, step back on it, and click the nearest demon.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/aUm1f6G5JI.png

.
**__Method - AFK (All styles)__ (Courtesy of <@868880315194691605>)**
Due to their relatively low HP and defences, abyssal demons can be AFK'd with all styles. Demon slayer circlet and boots <:demonslayercirclet:1004325697503907910> <:demonslayerboots:1004325691510231041> can be taken on all styles if owned for a 8% damage boost (4% each piece).

Before loading your cannon and starting the session, check that:
⬥ You are using leech strength/leech magic strength/leech range strength prayers if using Amulet of Zealots <:zealots:926021352626982942>
    • Also pray leech ranged if using <:range:580168050121113623>
⬥ You are using soulsplit <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>
⬥ Elder overload <:elderovl:841419289831800882> and weapon poison +++ <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active.
⬥ You are autocasting <:incitefear:856635090783567902> if using <:magic:689504724159823906>

> **__Abyssal Savages/Beasts__**
.tag:beasts
Abyssal Beasts are relatively low-hitting Abyssal creatures that can be done as an Abyssal demon task before acquiring the gear or level needed for Abyssal Lords. They provide a good profit and elite clue drop rate.

Abyssal Savages serve as a good pre-105 slayer alternative, offering high combat exp and <:abyssalflesh:947871842520170576> drop rates.

.
**__Stats (Savages/Beasts)__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 95/105
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill: 868/2170 + 286.4/716.1 <:constitution:689509250887712902>
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 423.2/1448.4
⬥ Optimal kills per hour - ~1680/640 (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP hour - ~711k/926k (AFK)

> **__Abyssal Lords__**
.tag:lords
Abyssal Lords are by far the best Abyssal creatures task for both exp and gp. Due to their high damage and defence, Magic with <:animatedead:856635090453135382>, a T90+ staff, and T90 tank armour <:achtoprimevalhelm:641346213316395019> / <:cryptbloomhelm:892819107123195956> should be used to do this task.

**Refer to Omid's video guide for other viable gear combinations.**

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 115
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill: 9785 + 3229 <:constitution:689509250887712902>
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 8235
⬥ Optimal kills per hour - ~280 (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP hour - ~2.30M (AFK)
