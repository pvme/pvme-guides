*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
[Revenants](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Revenants>) are a popular type of Wilderness monster which may be found in the [Forinthry Dungeon](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Forinthry_Dungeon>). They're mainly killed for the [Chaos Elemental collection log](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Boss_collection_log#Chaos_Elemental>), [Wilderness collection log](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_collection_log#Wilderness>), and the [Revenant Spirit](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Revenant_Spirit>) which is used to unlock the Revenant pet as well as its skins; this drop gets increasingly rarer the more skins you have.

⬥ After the Wilderness update killing revenants became substantially safer, as players can now opt-out of PvP.
⬥ Using a [Forinthry bracelet](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Forinthry_bracelet#Effects>) <:forinthrybracelet:1023152061350678549> will make revenants **NOT** be aggressive.
    • This effect will persist for an hour and only count down while in the Forinthry Dungeon.
    • Even if it was used years ago, the effect may still be active.

### __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ Revenants are located in the [Forinthry Dungeon](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Forinthry_Dungeon>).
    • The quickest way to get there is via the <:wildernesssword:1009419838764109834> Forinthry Dungeon teleport.
    • Alternatively you may use the <:wildernesslode:1032552297231822878> and run west to the level 17 Wilderness entrance.

## __Method: Active Magic__
.tag:method1
The active Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> method consists of killing revenants using [Legacy Combat Mode](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Legacy_Mode#Combat>) one at a time following specific routes, this can be replicated using Melee <:melee:1096130867279171706> with an augmented [Revenant bane 2h sword](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Revenant_bane_2h_sword>) <:bane2h:859483155210567741>

⬥ The knight and dragon are the fastest spawning revenants; use routes that prioritise killing them as soon as they respawn, as they have the highest drop rates for uniques.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/hAJb1MB50g.png
⬥ <:cannon:869283142350041098> is positioned to kill the southern revenant goblins and imp, occasionally being able to kill the pyrefiend, and lowering the dark beast and demon such that they can be consistently one-shot.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/mjtXJs8Zua.png
⬥ **Red** and **green**: Main routes to take when killing revenants between knight and dragon spawns.
    • When going from knight to dragon, kill 3-5 revenants. When going from dragon to knight, kill 2-3 revenants.
⬥ **Blue** and **yellow**: Potential detour routes for red and green routes respectively depending on revenant movement.

⬥ Ensure Legacy Combat Mode is active.
⬥ Ensure <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active at all times.
⬥ Using <:bloodblitz:535616247807868938>
    • The healing from the spell alone should negate the need for <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>
    • Pray <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Using <:crumbleundead:1351696446646390824>
    • Use <:crumbleundead:1351696446646390824> Crumble Undead with Soul Split <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>, Holy Scarab <:holyscarab:929773040273862656> and any overload with prayer restore to restore prayer

## __Method: Low Effort Magic__
.tag:method2
The low effort Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> method consists of killing revenants using [Legacy Combat Mode](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Legacy_Mode#Combat>) to AFK until they are no longer aggressive. When this happens you will follow a specific route killing revenants along the way in order to reset their aggro.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/LlbDHRTBgQ.png
⬥ <:cannon:869283142350041098> will solo kill the dragon.
    • You AFK kill the dark beast, ork, demon, cyclops, pyrefiend, and 2 goblins.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/5K8rvWazPb.png
⬥ **Blue** → **Green** → **Yellow** → **Blue (back to start)**
⬥ Every ~10 minutes follow the route shown above: take the outer loop to the dragon → knight → dragon → start.
    • **Make sure to reach the marked northwesternmost tile in order to properly reset aggro.**

⬥ Ensure Legacy Combat Mode is active.
⬥ Pray <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active at all times.
⬥ Using <:bloodblitz:535616247807868938>
    • The healing from the spell alone should negate the need for <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>
    • Pray <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Using <:crumbleundead:1351696446646390824>
    • Use <:crumbleundead:1351696446646390824> Crumble Undead with Soul Split <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>, Holy Scarab <:holyscarab:929773040273862656> and any overload with prayer restore to restore prayer
