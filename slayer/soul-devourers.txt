**__Soul Devourers__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
There are 6 types of soul devourers, ranging from 105 to 117 Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857>. The Magister may also be killed for this task, requiring 115 Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857>. The mobs each require a different level of attention, and the profits differ. This guide will focus on the recommended mobs to kill for this task.

⬥ The Magister will give 8000 Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> experience per kill and benefit from the use of Tuska's Wrath <:Tuskas:513201065513058306>, but the slayer helmet <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> effect does not work on him.

.
> **__Desirable drops__**
.tag:drops
⬥ Key to the Crossing <:keytothecrossing:1048592046409138217>
    • Dropped at a rate of 1/75 on task, which can be increased to 1/60 with the use of Sophanem slayer dungeon drop enhancers.
    • Required for the Slayer collection log.
⬥ Vital Spark <:vitalspark:656426807893032963>
    • Can be doubled via the use of vital spark drop enhancers.
    • Required for the Slayer collection log.
⬥ Soul devourers each have their own drop tables, containing good alchables and other resources, making them a consistent moneymaker.
    • The Sophanem Slayer Dungeon contains chests which will [automatically gather drops](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Sophanem_Slayer_Dungeon#Chest>).

.
> **__Location and How to Get there__**
.tag:location
⬥ Sophanem Slayer Dungeon
    • You can teleport there with Sophanem Slayer Dungeon teleport tablets.
    • Alternatively, you can teleport to the Menaphos lodestone <:menaphoslode:1032552289589796904>, take the bridge to Sophanem, and then run south to the dungeon entrance.
⬥ Sunken Pyramid (south-eastern room, 5 spawns)
    • In the small room, position yourself anywhere near the center of all the spawns.
        ⬩ For Imperial warrior akh's stand in the northern part of the room and target southern spawns.
    • You can teleport there with a teleport to house tablet, or with a Slayer Codex <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>

> **__Salawa Akh__**
.tag:salawa
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/midZL8f.png

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 105
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 504
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~1100 <:melee:1096130867279171706> (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~554k <:melee:1096130867279171706> (AFK)

.
**__Method - AFK Melee__**
The lowest level soul devourer, they do weak melee attacks and have no special mechanics, making them easy to AFK. A scrimshaw of vampyrism <:vampscrim:513201294262009874> is recommended; however, due to their weak attacks, they can be killed with Soul Split <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> active.

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:turmoil:583429936606347280> or <:malevolence:513190159416557573>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> or <:restore:642708657825644554>
⬥ Ensure <:holyaggroverload:1049230112849133589> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active

> **__Feline Akh__**
.tag:feline
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/oy7rulc.png

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 107
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 536.2
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~1100 <:melee:1096130867279171706> (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~590k <:melee:1096130867279171706> (AFK)

.
**__Method - AFK Melee__**
Feline akh's have harder hitting attacks than Salawas, and a special attack which deals high magic damage and makes nearby feline akh's aggressive towards you. This occurs when the mob reaches 2500 life points, and hits when the bar above their head fully charges. Despite this, they can very easily be AFK'd in the same method as Salawas, and are often killed for their common elite clue drops <:eliteclue:1123586941057769552>. Due to their harder hitting attacks, the usage of a vampyrism scrimshaw is recommended, as the player should be praying <:DeflectMelee:544195488447201300>.

⬥ Pray <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> and <:turmoil:583429936606347280> or <:malevolence:513190159416557573>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> or <:restore:642708657825644554>
⬥ Ensure <:holyaggroverload:1049230112849133589> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active

> **__Crocodile Akh__**
.tag:crocodile
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/xgLCv4o.png

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 111
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 761
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~1000 <:melee:1096130867279171706> (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~761k <:melee:1096130867279171706> (AFK)

.
**__Method - AFK Melee__**
Due to their low life points their dangerous special attack can be ignored. After 5 auto-attacks, they strike the floor to deal a large 3000 hit through deflect prayers. With a good setup they can be AFK'd. At 105 Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> it is possible to use Wilder Pies to boost, to kill these; however, at 111 Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> it is recommended to move on to the Imperial akhs via the usage of a Wilder Pie.

⬥ Pray <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> and <:turmoil:583429936606347280> or <:malevolence:513190159416557573>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> or <:restore:642708657825644554>
⬥ Ensure <:holyaggroverload:1049230112849133589> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active

> **__Imperial Warrior Akh__**
.tag:imperial
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/DrVy98i.png

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 117
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1780.6
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~800 <:melee:1096130867279171706>, ~600 <:magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~1.4m <:melee:1096130867279171706>, ~1.07m <:magic:689504724159823906>

.
**__Method - Active Melee__**
Differing to the other akh’s, these are best killed in the Sunken Pyramid as it provides better opportunities for AoE, and can be made to contain only Imperial warrior akh’s. Melee <:melee:1096130867279171706> is strong as an active method of killing them.

⬥ They use abilities and build adrenaline, starting with Backhand <:backhand:535532854302605333>, continuing with Smash <:smash:535532879820619786>, and Slaughter <:slaughter:535532879237873666>, so standing still is important.
    • It is strongly recommended to use Anticipate <:anti:535541306475151390> often.
    • Use Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> and laceration boots <:laceration:602581988599398400> to constantly deal AoE damage.

**__Method - Legacy Magic__**
Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> with an Inquisitor's staff <:inquisitorstaff:694566917553520680> at the Sunken Pyramid is the best method to kill these whilst AFK. This is done in Legacy Combat Mode, so there is no way to avoid stuns. Standing in northern part of the room, target the southern Imperial warrior akh’s to consistently AoE.

⬥ Vampyrism aura <:vamp:643505653079343144> is recommended as they can hit hard.
⬥ As the Inquisitor staff <:inquisitorstaff:694566917553520680> is tier 80, Blood Burst <:bloodburst:841419289714098246> can be used as the main spell due to it scaling up to tier 79, and it being significantly cheaper than Blood Barrage <:bloodbarrage:537338981747261446>.
