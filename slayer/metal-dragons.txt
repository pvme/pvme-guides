## __Mithril Dragons__
.tag:mithrildragons
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/5clGyMHybb.png
Mithril Dragons are often killed for their drops of Ancient Pages and Chewed bones/Dragon Full helm. You can obtain both all of these items in your PSD, making it a desirable AFK method and location for players hunting the Dragon Full Helm or the [Barbarian Scavenger](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Barbarian_Scavenger>) achievement.

.
### __Stats__
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 1
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 564.4
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~700-730 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~412k <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>

.
### __Location and Where to Stand__
.tag:mdlocs
.
Due to bad respawn timers in habitat locations, it is highly recommended to kill Mithril Dragons in a large room in your PSD.
There is no precise positioning required as you may need to move for chewed bones to be in area loot range. Due to this, setting up an alt1 loot beam notifier is recommended if hunting for the Dragon Full Helm.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/kbHDJj9td7.png

.
### __Method - AFK Necromancy__
.tag:mdnecro
⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:sorrow:1137941003895046284>/<:ruination:1137940996391452772>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:demonhornnecklace:975765831248130079> and <:bonecrusher:975284344526602240>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532> and <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581> are active

## __Method: Ranged (AFK)__
.tag:rdranged
You should kill all rune dragons around the statues when you initially arrive the spot to reset their spawn points. **For the most part, these 2 dragons should be the only ones targeting you at any time.**

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:desolation:513190159018098713> / <:anguish:583429936665198592>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> **OR** <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> + <:holyscarab:929773040273862656>
⬥ The pillar often blocks area loot, so you may wish to use <:telekeneticgrab:866551637970255872> to loot without moving.
⬥ Set an Alt1 alert for Elite spawns if fully AFK as Elites often require manual intervention to prevent KO.
