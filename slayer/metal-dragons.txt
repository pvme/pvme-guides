**__Desirable Drops__**
.tag:desirabledrops
⬥ All metal dragons drop bones ranging from regular dragon bones to reinforced dragon bones.
⬥ All metal dragons also drop a guaranteed plated salvage corresponding to their type.
⬥ <:mithrildragon:1080862292016644176> Mithril and <:adamantdragon:1080862288006873138> Adamant dragons drop Dragon Full Helm - 1/8192* and 1/16384 respectively
    • Required for the Slayer log, may alternatively be obtained from the Abomination
    • *includes the drop chance from Chewed bones
⬥ <:adamantdragon:1080862288006873138> Adamant and <:runedragon:1004107341374963852> Rune dragons drop Forcae's Journal pages - 1/20
    • 20 are required for MQC <:masterquestcape:1091050888828813412>
⬥ <:runedragon:1004107341374963852> Rune dragons drop Kethsi Ring <:kethsiring:937093437881192498> - 1/1024, 1/512 from elites
    • Required for the Slayer log
⬥ <:runedragon:1004107341374963852> Rune dragons drop Kethsi outfit scroll - 1/200 **only from elites, 100% from Barry the destroyer**
    • Required for the Slayer log (only 1 outfit required)
⬥ <:runedragon:1004107341374963852> Rune dragons drop T85 boot upgrades
    • Required for the Slayer log, may alternatively be obtained from Elite Dungeons using Lucky Charms <:ed1luckycharm:841409588561707009> <:ed2luckycharm:841409588694876222> <:ed3luckycharm:841409588938276874>

> **__Mithril Dragons__**
.tag:mithrildragons
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/5clGyMHybb.png
Mithril Dragons are often killed for their drops of Ancient Pages and Chewed bones/Dragon Full helm. You can obtain both all of these items in your PSD, making it a desirable AFK method and location for players hunting the Dragon Full Helm or the [Barbarian Scavenger](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Barbarian_Scavenger>) achievement.
.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 1
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 564.4
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~700-730 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~412k <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>
.
**__Location and Where to Stand__**
.tag:mdlocs
.
Due to bad respawn timers in habitat locations, it is highly recommended to kill Mithril Dragons in a large room in your PSD.
There is no precise positioning required as you may need to move for chewed bones to be in area loot range. Due to this, setting up an alt1 loot beam notifier is recommended if hunting for the Dragon Full Helm.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/kbHDJj9td7.png

> **__Method: Ranged (AFK)__**
.tag:rdranged
Your main targets are the 2 southern spawns in the picture above. Kill all rune dragons around the statues when you initially arrive the spot to reset their spawn points. **For the most part, these 2 dragons should be the only ones targeting you at any time.**

Rune dragon aggression is permanent as long as you're in the area. This is makes it easy to trap stray dragons behind the pillar if the <:cannon:869283142350041098> hits them.

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:desolation:513190159018098713>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> or <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> and <:holyscarab:929773040273862656>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882> is active
⬥ <:telekeneticgrab:866551637970255872> can be utilised to loot without moving from the safespot

⬥ **Set an Alt1 alert for Elite spawns** if fully AFK as Elites require manual intervention most of the time. Use powerful abilities such as <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> or <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> along with <:dragonslayer:641339921814126594> and <:dommine:662249620579155968> on any Elite Rune Dragons that spawns. Use <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> if caught out on low adren.

⬥ With <:sgb:626466665848242186> this set-up obtains around 185 KPH off-task.
    • Note that kph varies as sometimes the west spawn will not be hit by <:cannon:869283142350041098> and walks out of range.
        ⬩ When coming back to pot, attack the west spawn if the <:cannon:869283142350041098> cannot hit it to reset its spawn.
    • kph figures are close to 200 KPH with <:bolg:994189289623662702>
        ⬩ Link an example video in <#1020853673317908500> if you wish to provide accurate KPH figures with <:bolg:994189289623662702>
