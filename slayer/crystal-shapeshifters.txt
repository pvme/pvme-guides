*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
[Crystal Shapeshifters](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Crystal_Shapeshifter>) <:crystalshapeshifter:1004107281153150986> are a slayer monster found on [Tarddiad](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Tarddiad>) and are accessible via the World Gate (looks like the number 1), they can be assigned by Morvran or Laniakea after the completion of [The Light Within](<https://runescape.wiki/w/The_Light_Within>) quest. Crystal Shapeshifters will change their attacking style to what your armour is **weakest** against (if this is equal, attack styles will be random).

.

⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~490 <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~500 <:melee:1096130867279171706>, ~570 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>

### __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ If killing Crystal Shapeshifters in Tarddiad use option 2 on <:sixthagecircuit:937093290245894154> to select the World Gate.
    • Make sure to attune the World Gate to Tarddiad.
⬥ If killing Crystal Shapeshifters in the Sunken Pyramid use a <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>
    • Relocating your house to Menaphos and using a teleport to house tablet also works.

.
## __Method: Tarddiad__
.tag:method1
⬥ Make sure you **toggle on aggression** by speaking to Angof on Tarddiad (north, near the camp fire).
    • This makes the Crystal Shapeshifters permanently aggressive and eliminates the need for <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581>

## __Method: Sunken Pyramid__
.tag:method2
⬥ Make sure you **toggle on aggression** by speaking to Angof on Tarddiad (north, near the camp fire).
    • This makes the Crystal Shapeshifters permanently aggressive and eliminates the need for <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581>
⬥ Place 5 Crystal Shapeshifters in the small room of the Sunken Pyramid.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/67qNuIGcUI.png
⬥ Position yourself in the southeast corner of the small room.

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:affliction:513190158468907008> / <:malevolence:513190159416557573> / <:ruination:1137940996391452772>
