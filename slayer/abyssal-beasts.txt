# Abyssal Beasts
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Abyssal beasts are relatively low-hitting Abyssal creatures that can be done as an Abyssal demon task before acquiring the gear or level needed for Abyssal lords. They provide a good profit and elite clue drop rate.

.
### Stats
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 105
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1448.4
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~640 <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~750 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~927k <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~1086k <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>

.
## __Desirable drops__
.tag:drops
⬥ Abyssal Beasts drop the Jaws of the Abyss <:jawsoftheabyss:947871842595639306>, a T85 melee power helmet.
    • As well as Abyssal Flesh <:abyssalflesh:947871842520170576>, which is required to craft Abyssal armour spikes <:abyssalarmourspike:1004107347259555981>.
⬥ If killed in the Wilderness, they can also drop <:revenantdropenhancer:1006481325076787240> which can be helpful for Ultimate Slayer hunters.

## __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
.
They can be fought in the Senntisten Asylum, the Wilderness, or the Player-owned Slayer Dungeon (POSD).

.
⬥ Senntisten Asylum:
    • If using an Oldak Coil, place it 3 tiles North of player position. The yellow tile marked is for Dwarf Multicannon only.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/3bJyG0olTS.png
.
⬥ Level 35 Wilderness:
    • Go just north of the level 35 Wilderness obelisk. Then position yourself two tiles north of the skeleton. A beast might get stuck initially, this won't happen when it respawns.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/NdWbnpS0jN.png
⬥ Player Owned Slayer Dungeon:
    • In the small room, position yourself six tiles south of the northern statue (northeastern corner of the brazier's shadow).
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/V8fzRErOIf.png

.

⬥ Elder overload <:elderovl:841419289831800882> and weapon poison +++ <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active.

For all methods, pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and t95/t99 curse for the style you are using.
