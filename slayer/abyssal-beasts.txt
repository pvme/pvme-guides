## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
[Abyssal beasts](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Abyssal_beast>) <:abyssalbeast:1004107328964014100> are a higher level alternative for Abyssal Slayer creature assignments. They are mainly killed for Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> tasks, the [Senntisten collection log](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_collection_log#Senntisten>), combat <:combat:797896050370281523> / <:invention:689509250946695292> item experience, profit, and <:eliteclue:1123586941057769552>

⬥ Abyssal beasts can be assigned as a Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> assignment by Mandrith, Laniakea, Morvran, Kuradal, Duradel, Sumona, and The Raptor.
    • Abyssal beasts specific tasks may be assigned by Mandrith.
    • Demon cluster tasks also work, they may be assigned by Laniakea, and The Raptor.
        ⬩ Laniakea assigns almost twice as many.
⬥ The level 35 Wilderness location yields the highest kph.

.

## __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
Abyssal beasts can be fought in the Senntisten Asylum, the Wilderness, or the Sunken Pyramid.

**Senntisten Asylum**
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/ZRpoxozL1P.png
⬥ If killing Abyssal beasts at the Senntisten Asylum, use a <:pontifexshadowring:870326852361789501>
    • Go to the second room of the Senntisten Asylum and position yourself 5 tiles east of the west drain.
    • Place <:cannon:869283142350041098> 4 tiles east of you.
        ⬩ If using an <:oldakcoil:798285651387613194> place it 3 tiles north of you.

.
**Level 35 Wilderness**
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/3ccvUzRWdX.png
⬥ If killing Abyssal beasts at the level 35 Wilderness location, use a <:portableobelisk:1137735861937655900>
    • Go just north of the level 35 Wilderness obelisk and position yourself two tiles north of the skeleton.
        ⬩ A beast might get stuck initially but this won't happen when it respawns.
    • Place <:kineticcyclone:798285651405438997> 1 tile north of you.
    • This location yields the highest kph.

.
**Sunken Pyramid**
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/t6Us9dgqjv.png
⬥ If killing Abyssal beasts in the Sunken Pyramid, use a <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>
    • Relocating your house to Menaphos and using a teleport to house tablet also works.
    • Place 5 Abyssal beasts in the small room, and position yourself six tiles south of the northern statue (northeastern corner of the brazier's shadow).

.
