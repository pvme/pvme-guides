# Nightmare Creatures
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
[Nightmare creatures](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Nightmare>) <:nightmare:1004107308516782111> can be assigned as a Slayer assignment after purchasing the toggle for 50 Slayer points, this requires completion of the [Children of Mah](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Children_of_Mah>) quest. They are mainly killed for Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> tasks, the [Other Worlds collection log](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_collection_log#Other_Worlds>), combat <:combat:797896050370281523> / <:invention:689509250946695292> item experience, and profit.

⬥ Nightmare creatures can be assigned as a Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> assignment by Laniakea, Morvran, and Kuradal.

.
### Stats
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 80
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1540
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~901 <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~800 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> (All AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~1.39m <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~1.2m <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> (All AFK)

.
### __Desirable drops__
.tag:drops
⬥ [Nightmare gauntlets](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Nightmare_gauntlets>) <:nightmaregauntlets:513190159441723392>
    • Required for the Slayer collection log.
    • 1/2,000 on-task and 1/10,000 off-task.

*Note: if you change the monetary value for custom loot to 5k this will allow you to loot everything relevant (No sharks or Muspah spines). This is achieved by going to Settings > Gameplay > Item Drops > Loot System > Monetary Value 5,000.*

### __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ Nightmare Creatures are located at the Ritual site on Freneskae, accessible through the [World Gate](<https://runescape.wiki/w/World_Gate>) after completion of the Children of Mah quest.
    • Bring a Sixth-Age circuit <:sixthagecircuit:937093290245894154> for a quick teleport.

.
## __Method: AFK Magic__ <:magic:689504724159823906>
.tag:method1
Killing Nightmare creatures with Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> will yield the highest kph but require expensive abilities and gear for optimal results. If wanting to camp Nightmares, this is the preferred setup.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/PuyjMXH9uG.png
⬥ Positioning can greatly impact kph.

.
Before loading your <:cannon:869283142350041098> and starting the session, check the following:

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Autocast <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258> and upkeep <:animatedead:856635090453135382>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:demonhornnecklace:975765831248130079>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active at all times.
⬥ Ensure that <:scriptureofwen:883134307902816297> is active.

## __Method: AFK Necromancy__ <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>
.tag:method2
Necromancy <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> has good sustain with <:conjurearmy:1166094935066423348> / <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> and <:bloodsiphon:1159434279311380532>, as well as strong aoe damage potential with <:conjurezombie:1137809200219684924> (poison), <:soulstrike:1137809142376058910> and <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892>. 4-piece T90 Deathdealer Robes <:dealerhood80:1142783479810117702> for chance to proc <:invokedeath:1137809121983336548>

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/9AEsPuLs2E.png
⬥ You may be walked backwards one square if you attack a Nightmare that is running towards you.

.
Before loading your <:cannon:869283142350041098> and starting the session, check the following:

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:sorrow:1137941003895046284> or <:ruination:1137940996391452772>
⬥ Upkeep <:darkness:1137809209782698024>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> or <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
    • May alternatively use <:demonhornnecklace:975765831248130079>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active at all times.
⬥ Ensure that <:scriptureofwen:883134307902816297> is active.
