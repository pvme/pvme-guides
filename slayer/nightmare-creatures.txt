__**Nightmare Creatures**__
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
.
Unlockable for 50 slayer points after the completion of the Children of Mah quest, Nightmare Creatures are powerful slayer creatures, requiring 80 <:slayer:797896049548066857> to kill. They offer good XP rates, good GP rates, AFK-ability and a high valued unique.

⬥ Nightmares have two forms of attacks, a weaker melee auto-attack, and a 2-hit ranged special attack which deals a lot of damage (~4000 total).
    • It is generally encouraged to use <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> on all methods as the ranged attack has a higher KO potential.

.
**__Stats__**
.
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill : 1540
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~720 AFK (on task)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour : ~1.10M AFK

.
**__Desirable Drops__**
.tag:desirabledrops
.
⬥ Nightmares have a strong common drop table, offering a large amount of alchables, fletching supplies, and <:coins:698816156961603654>, alongside being a very good source of <:soulrune:536252660333019136> <:bloodrune:536252658970001409>

.
⬥ For the slayer log, Nightmares also drop the Nightmare Gauntlets <:NMG:513190159441723392>, a set of powerful range gloves that are best-in-slot in some scenarios. These are dropped at a rate of 1/2,000 on-task and 1/10,000 off-task.

If you change the monetary value for custom loot to 5k this will allow you to loot everything relevant (No sharks or Muspah spines). This is achieved by going to Settings > Gameplay > Item Drops > Loot System > Monetary Value 5,000.

This makes Nightmare Creatures worth <:coins:698816156961603654> $data_pvme:Slayer!D18$ per kill.

> **__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:locationandhowtogetthere
Nightmare Creatures are located at the Ritual site on Freneskae, accessible through the World Gate after completion of the quest Children of Mah. Bring a Sixth-Age circuit <:sixthagecircuit:937093290245894154> for an easy teleport.

.
> **__General strategy__**
.tag:general
⬥ <:magic:689504724159823906> and <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> are the recommended styles to kill Nightmares with. Place your <:cannon:869283142350041098> at the spot shown in the image below, and stand next to the large pillar with <:DeflectRange:544195488317046812> and auto-retaliate on.
.
⬥ Position yourself as shown below. Place the <:cannon:869283142350041098> on the yellow square, and stand on the green square after loading it with cannonballs.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/9AEsPuLs2E.png
.
***Note:** The player may have a chance to be walked backwards one square if they attack a Nightmare that is running towards them, in this case simply click back onto the highlighted square.*

.
> **__AFK Magic__**
.tag:afkmagic
.
⬥ Magic can be used via Legacy mode, where the healing offered from Blood spells <:bloodbarrage:537338981747261446> is extremely strong, or using revo++.
    • Revo++ is mainly used to save runes as camping <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258> saves a lot of runes compared to using legacy.

⬥ **If using Legacy:** If using <:inquisitorstaff:694566917553520680>, the spell Blood Burst <:bloodburst:841419289714098246> can be used for slightly less damage (T79 vs T80) whilst saving a large amount of blood runes, which one could do to improve profits.
    • Do not use <:bloodburst:841419289714098246> with other staves such as <:fsoa:869284271595069451>

⬥ **If using revo++:** Bring your best magic tank armour and use <:animatedead:856635090453135382> since you are lacking blood spell healing.
    • <:vampaura:643505653079343144> is the **only viable aura** if using revo++.

⬥ On task, you should achieve around 720kph, with off-task rates being around 680 to 690kph for both revo++ and legacy.

> **__AFK Necro__**
.tag:afknecro
.
Thanks to Necromancies sustain with <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> and <:bloodsiphon:1159434279311380532>, as well as its strong aoe damage potential with <:soulstrike:1137809142376058910> and <:spectralscythe:1137809145706319892>, it can effectively AFK Nightmare Creatures. Around 660-720kph can be achieved on task with this method while being safe and AFK with low input costs.

⬥ Pray <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> and <:sorrow:1137941003895046284>/<:ruination:1137940996391452772>
⬥ Upkeep <:darkness:1137809209782698024>, <:elderovl:841419289831800882>, <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581>, <:superprayerrenewal:547603714681339915> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> at all times
⬥ Use <:cannon:869283142350041098> to help aggroing the Nightmare Creatures
