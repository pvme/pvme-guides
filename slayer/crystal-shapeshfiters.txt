> __**Introduction**__
.tag:introduction
Crystal Shapeshifters <:crystalshapeshifter:1004107281153150986> are <:slayer:797896049548066857> monsters found on Tarddiad, accessible through the World Gate. They can be assigned by Morvran or Laniakea after the completion of 'The Light Within', and requires level 80 <:slayer:797896049548066857>. They are best killed in the small room of a Player-Owned Dungeon with either <:Magic:689504724159823906> or <:melee:1096130867279171706>.

A set of Crystal Armour (preferably attuned) <:attunedcrystalhelm:1097515663490285648> should be used for doing this task.
⬥ It is possible to kill them AoE without <:attunedcrystalhelm:1097515663490285648>, although only with <:magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ <:melee:1096130867279171706> should only be used if using 3 pieces of crystal armour **and** in a PoD.

<:crystalshapeshifter:1004107281153150986> will **change their attacking style to what you're weakest in defending against.** Generally, you want all of them to attack using <:melee:1096130867279171706>, as it makes them easily clustered for fast AoE kills. To do this, equip 2 pieces of <:range:580168050121113623> armour (1 if using <:attunedcrystalhelm:1097515663490285648>) so that your defensive bonuses against <:melee:1096130867279171706> are the most negative. You can check this in the **Hero > Loadout** tab.

> **__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:location
**Tarddiad**
Teleport to the World Gate using the <:sixthagecircuit:937093290245894154>. Ensure the gate is attuned to Tarddiad and enter.

**Player Owned Dungeon**
Teleport using the <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875> or a house teleport attuned to the Menaphos house portal and travelling via Portmaster Kags.

For all Tarddiad methods, you'll want <:crystalshapeshifter:1004107281153150986> aggression toggled **'ON'**. **Visit Angof on Tarddiad to do so.** This makes them permmanently aggressive and eliminates the need for <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581>
