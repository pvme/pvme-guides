# Greater demon berserkers and ash lords
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
.
Greater demon variants found southwest of Elite Dungeon 2 and within the Zamorakian undercity elite dungeon are significantly stronger than standard greater demons.

⬥ The two wilderness variants of enhanced greater demons attack using melee (berserker) and magic (ash lord). The wilderness population is weaker than those found in the Zamorakian undercity and are poisonable <:poisonicon:944649693500154006>. Along with access to <:infernalpuzzlebox:994189299681607800> buffs, this makes them ideal for slayer training.

⬥ Since they don't have drop any uniques, a <:sacrificescrim:1023150750915899402> can be used for additional slayer XP. This makes greater demon berserkers and ash lords some of the best AFK slayer exp in the game.
    • This also means there's no reason to do them off-task.

.

### Location and How to Get There
.tag:location
Use the dragonkin laboratory teleport or a portal attuned to the Elite dungeon via War's Retreat. Exit via the southwest to enter the wilderness.

.
## __General strategy__
.tag:general
Enhanced greater demon variants possess high damage and cannot be recklessly AoE'd unlike many other slayer mobs. Hence, it would be best to safespot them or at most tank 2 of them at once.

⬥ Players who do not possess end game equipment may utilise a safespot west of the dungeon as shown below:
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/QNQKfw6CFG.png
⬥ More information on <:cannon:869283142350041098> placement and demonstration: https://youtu.be/mSMcfhR4OKM?t=270
.
⬥ Players who are well-geared and can afford to tank their attacks may run south and position themselves on a small penninsula as shown below, using a <:cannon:869283142350041098> to aggro the demons.
    • This spot allows you to lure 5 demons in total (3 ash lords + 2 berserkers), and limits the number of demons attacking you to at most 2 at once (1 Ash lord + 1 Berserker) as other demons will be stuck behind.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/nC2nSMEQNe.png
.
## __Method: AFK Magic/Ranged__
.tag:afk
With high end gear, both <:range:580168050121113623> and <:magic:689504724159823906> can be used to AFK the demons, at around the same rates.

⬥ When you need to repair the <:cannon:869283142350041098>, quickly <:surge:535533810004262912> out, click, and then <:dive:1049378668197195808> back to the suggested spot.
    • Check that the demons did not walk in a way which gets them stuck.

***Note: ** During the initial lure, the demons may get stuck in a conformation that makes them unable to approach you, to resolve this, kill the initial wave and then walk back to the suggested spot.*
