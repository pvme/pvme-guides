## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
[Capsarius](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Capsarius>) are the healers of the Order of Ascension. They are mainly killed for Ascension members Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> tasks, the [Feldip Hills collection log](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_collection_log#Feldip_Hills>), and Ascension Keystones in the Sunken Pyramid.

⬥ This guide will focus on killing Capsarius in the Sunken Pyramid, as this location yields the highest kills per hour.
    • Capsarius have weak attacks, don't heal each other, and have low health which makes them very easy to farm.
⬥ Ascension members can be assigned as a Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> assignment by Duradel, Kuradal, Morvran, and Laniakea.

.

.

    • Only use this location while collecting souls.
