## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Celestial Dragons <:celestialdragon:1027212539441975296> are generally only killed for the Ultimate Slayer title. Otherwise, the XP and GP/hr are mediocre at best.

.

⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 1
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 976.6

### __Location and How to Get There__
.tag:location
.
<:celestialdragon:1027212539441975296> can be found in the [Dragontooth Island resource dungeon](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Dragontooth_Island_resource_dungeon>) after the question [One of a Kind](<https://runescape.wiki/w/One_of_a_Kind>) is complete.
.
You can get there by:
⬥ Option 0 → 3 on the [Dungeoneering cape](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Dungeoneering_cape>) <:dgcape:976732922545586246> (option 0 → 3).
⬥ Using a [Max guild portal](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Max_guild_portal>) tuned to the Radiant Wisp colony.
⬥ Teleport to Dragontooth Island using the [Big Book o' Piracy](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Big_Book_o%27_Piracy>).
⬥ You can also go to Port Phasmatys (Ectophial teleport or charter a boat) and speak with the Ghost captain while wearing a ghostspeak amulet.

.
## __Method: AFK Ranged__ <:range:580168050121113623>
.tag:ranged
.
Place your <:cannon:869283142350041098> in the middle of the room and ensure <:autoretaliate:1084799717726228530> is active.
.
With this <:range:580168050121113623> method trips are only limited by your supplies (e.g. <:elderovl:841419289831800882> and cannonballs).
.
⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:anguish:583429936665198592> or <:desolation:513190159018098713>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:demonhornnecklace:975765831248130079> + <:ectoplasmator:1023152065431744542>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882>, <:superantifire:864492695997513748> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288>
