> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
.
Camel Warriors are one of the 4 Raptor Mobs, generally killed for the collection log. As such, they should **only be killed on task where they are 5x more likely to drop the <:camelstaff:917025698479210528>**

⬥ Camel warriors have one main mechanic - After taking some damage, they split into 3 mirages - Blood, Smoke and Shadow. Each has their own unique abilities, and attack with magic attacks.
    • Shadow mirages - Drains prayer points after charging the bar above their heads.
    • Blood mirages - Heal other mirages for ~900 HP per attack.
    • Smoke mirages - cast the Confuse, Weaken and Snare spells on the player, reducing hitchance and damage by 5%, and freezing you in place for 10s respectively.
        ⬩ After the mirages have been killed, the camel warrior re-appears with the HP it had before phasing, and can now be finished off.

.
⬥ **This mechanic can be bypassed by dealing a singular hit of more than 20,125 damage (before hitcaps apply).**
    • For example, if <:tuskas:513201065513058306> is used on task in <:berserk:513190158468907012> with 120 <:slayer:797896049548066857>, it will be capped at 15K, but in theory it will deal around 30K in damage, so the camel will not be phased. This will then allow the player to finish off the camel by dealing the remaining 5125 damage in 1 hit.
    • If the player fails to deal 5125 damage in the next hit, the camel will phase into mirages. This is why perks such as crackling and aftershock should be avoided when relying on methods that skip the mirage mechanic.

⬥ **All the methods suggested below focus on using tricks that bypass the mechanic entirely,** as it is very time consuming and detrimental to KPH to kill Camel warriors normally.

    • Raptor key part 3 (1 / 256)

> **__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:location
⬥ They can be found on the island east of Sophanem, which is accessed via stepping stones (no <:agility:1039864819760758844> level needed) near a large group of crocodiles. The area is affected by desert heat.
    • The quickest way to get there is via travelling south from Pharaoh's sceptre/Sceptre of the Gods option 2 followed by <:accel:756236265472524418>
    • Alternatively, is it also possible to reach there via a gate in the eastern wall of Sophanem that allows access from the city.

.

.tag:camelmelee

It also requires essentially best in slot melee gear to be as consistent as possible.

    • If you get a <:relentless5:712244800920748092> <:zerk:535532854004678657> and lucky crits, a 9 kill run is possible.

**__Strategy__**
Stalling <:tuskas:513201065513058306> on a dummy will incur a 15 second cooldown, but releasing it on a slayer target will deal the damage of an enhanced <:tuskas:513201065513058306>, allowing players to 1-shot a camel every 15 seconds with <:tuskas:513201065513058306> + a suitable EoF spec that consistently hits for more than 5125.

.
⬥ Buffs needed:
    • Elite sirenic / tectonic armour <:elitetectmask:556582803014418432> <:elitesirenicmask:643846921944956929>
    • Berserker aura variants for the respective style <:mani:643505153709965322> <:reckless:643505179378974748>
    • Corrupted slayer helmet on Anachronia helmet stand <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792>
    • Zuk cape for the respective style <:igneouskalmej:902209626408382494> <:igneouskalxil:902209626404171786>
    • Stalker's / Channeller's ring <:stalkerring:839903943601029120> <:channellerring:839903943404027914>
    • Ful arrows <:fularrow:971025696958853180> if using ranged and 12 <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258> stacks if using <:magic:689504724159823906>
    • Deathtouched bracelet <:dtb:513190159429271562> or glove slot providing equivalent bonuses.
    • Enhanced blast diffusion or fleeting boots <:detoboots:602581956072439828> <:fleetingboots:789813993480388640> <:shadowspike:1003947797491499098>
    • Essence of Finality (orn) <:eofor:745279787471470713> with a Guthix staff or Magic shieldbow <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:magicshieldbow:1114256524047568947>
    • Staff of Sliske/Noxious longbow or above <:sos:626466320132734976> <:noxbow:513190159425208342> or better with P6 <:p6:712073088769982475> and R3 <:ruthless3:712244800572883025>
    • If using <:bolg:994189289623662702>, <:p5:689504239025782825> will be sufficient and <:ruthless3:712244800572883025> is not needed.
    • Stack <:ruthless3:712244800572883025> on the Crocodiles before the camel warrior island and refresh it before it expires.
    • Scrimshaw of the Elements/Cruelty <:elementsscrim:841409588959510539> <:cruelty:513190159546712074> (superior version preferred if lacking any armour bonuses).
    • Berserker's Fury <:berserkersfury:697808774106185768> with **less than 100 HP.**

.
⬥ Steps to setup:
    • Use Enchanted Lyre option 7/Fremennik sea boots to teleport to Fremennik.
    • Enter the building as shown below, climb up the ladder and spam click the chest to lower HP. Stop once your HP is lower than 1k as it is possible to die from the chest.
    • Travel to the camel warrior location while eating the dwarven rock cake to lower your HP **below 100.**
    • Kill Crocodiles before hopping over to the camel island if stacking <:ruthless3:712244800572883025>. Make sure you get 12 <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258> stacks if using <:magic:689504724159823906>

    • Waiting until you can use a spec, using target cycle, and then using a spell to release <:tuskas:513201065513058306> + an <:eofspec:746403211908481184> to kill the camels.
