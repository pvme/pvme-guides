> **__Misc Slayer Guides__**
.
**__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
.
This guides cover the monsters that can be killed on a Creatures of Daemonheim cluster task. The creatures that can be killed on this task are: Frostdragon, Kal'Gerion demon, Seeker, Soulgazer, Edimmu, or monsters in Dungeoneering floors. Currently the guide contains guides for Kal'Gerion demons, Soulgazers, and Edimmus.

.

.tag:kalgs

**__Desirable drops__**
.tag:kalgsdesirable
⬥ Demon Slayer equipment <:demonslayerbody:1004325695520002068> - 3/2048 per piece
    • You will likely need to kill about a thousand demons or more to obtain each piece.
⬥ Adenaline Urn <:adrenurn:656426717413507074> - 1/1024
⬥ Kal'Gerion battle commendations - 1/512
    • You may need to kill more in order to obtain all 5, a requirement for the Trimmed Completionist cape <:comptrim:654618235223932958>

.tag:gazersdesirable

.img:https://i.imgur.com/aEfPuEA.png

⬥ Optimal KPH: Around 400 per hour, more if on-task.

⬥ Mainly single target gazers on one side of the dungeon while using an Oldak Coil <:oldakcoil:798285651387613194> to aggro the gazers on the other side (shown below), causing them to cluster.
    • Use <:mechchin:641669268722810881> to kill the clustered gazers. This is the only time you should use <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> other than for Elite Soulgazers.
    • You may occasionally need to manually top up cannon ammo if standing too far.
.
.img:https://i.imgur.com/HC2aQXK.png
.
⬥ Try to not have too many Soulgazers on you when killing the Elite. Finish off any remaining attacking Soulgazers before taking on Elite Soulgazers.

⬥ Use Dominion Mines <:dommine:662249620579155968> on Elite Soulgazers.

**__Desirable drops__**
.tag:edimmudesirable
⬥ Edimmu pet - 1/512 **only from elites**
    • Required for the Slayer log.
⬥ Blood Necklace shards <:bloodnecklaceshard:1015699014764728492>
    • Required for the Slayer log.

Tier 2 luck increases the odds of spawning an Elite Edimmu and Tier 3 <:luckpotion:656426717295935500> luck increases the drop rate of <:bloodnecklaceshard:1015699014764728492> and the pet.

.
**__Active Strategy__**
.tag:edimmuactive
.
**__Preset__**
.tag:edimmuactivepreset
⬥ <:salveamulet:797899945730244648> **Salve Amulet (e)**
⬥ Emergency food (Rocktails in example preset)
⬥ <:sirenicbody:643846948570267648> Augmented armour with **Undead Slayer** <:undeadslayerperk:689502804720615441>
.
.img:https://i.imgur.com/RtQnehA.png
.
⬥ Some food should be kept in the <:Yak:513195712201228289> in case you mess up Elite Edimmu spawns.

⬥ Lure Edimmu to the barrier and use <:mechchin:641669268722810881> to mow them down.
    • If an Elite Edimmu is present, don't target the Elite but instead chin it together with the regular Edimmu around it.

⬥ While waiting for Edimmu to respawn, aggro the Edimmu cross the chasm, out of reach of the cannon.
    • Use <:ricochet:535541259566186521> or <:aggression:909338509234569216>.

⬥ Move rare drop table drops, sharks, or unstackable drops (e.g. crystal seeds) into your <:Yak:513195712201228289>
    • Use Winter Storage to bank untradeable drops like Elite clue scrolls or <:bloodnecklaceshard:1015699014764728492>

⬥ The image below shows the position of the cannon (yellow box).
.
.img:https://i.imgur.com/v8LtTm9.png
.
⬥ Optimal KPH: Around 550 per hour.
