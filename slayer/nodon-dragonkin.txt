**__Nodon Dragonkin__**
.
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
> **__Introduction__**
.tag:introduction
There are 4 Nodon dragonkin variants that can be killed for slayer: Nodon engineers, artificers, hunters, and guards. The Nodon dragonkin task can be assigned by Morvran or Laniakea and requires level 92 <:slayer:797896049548066857>. Additionally, players can kill Kerapac <:kerapac:875443920673337344> in Normal Mode or Hard Mode, since the <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> buffs (but not <:Tuskas:513201065513058306>) apply to the boss.

⬥ Currently there are only methods detailed for Nodon guards and artificers. If you have a different method for Nodon hunters or engineers, please make a post in <#1020853673317908500>.


***Note**: Dragon cluster tasks from Laniakea apply <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> and <:Tuskas:513201065513058306> buffs to Slayer monsters at Nodon front but not <:kerapac:875443920673337344>. This also applies for <:dragonslayer:641339921814126594>, <:dragonslayerperk:689502927731163170> and Dragonbane weaponry.*
.

**__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:location
⬥ Option 1: Teleport to the Senntisten cathedral using the <:pontifexshadowring:870326852361789501> and run to your destination **(Fastest for Nodon Guards)**.
⬥ Option 2: Enter a War's Retreat/Max Guild <:kerapac:875443920673337344> portal and run to the location from there **(Fastest for Nodon Artificers)**.

.

⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882>, <:weppoison:689525476158472288> and <:animatedead:856635090453135382> are active

It is unknown if the method works with Ganodermic armour <:ganohelm:1003947807754960896> <:ganobody:1003947805611655208> <:ganolegs:1003947803673903204> <:ganoboots:1003947801836789830>. If you try the method with this gear, please provide a vod in <#1020853673317908500>.

**__Method: AFK Magic__**
.tag:artificer_magic_afk
.
⬥ By standing 1 tile behind the position used in the image, one can safespot the artificers. Although this is considerably less AFK as you cannot use <:autoretaliate:1084799717726228530>, it is useful if the player is undergeared.

⬥ Ensure you are doing the following:
    • Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:torment:583429936958930964> / <:affliction:513190158468907008>
    • Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> points with <:bonecrusher:975284344526602240> + <:demonhornnecklace:975765831248130079> or <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> + <:holyscarab:929773040273862656>
    • Autocast <:bloodbarrage:537338981747261446>
    • Ensure <:elderovlsalve:648976643687317532>, <:weppoison:689525476158472288> and <:animatedead:856635090453135382> are active

⬥ No other inputs are required except for potential looting. The method is viable both on- and off-task.
