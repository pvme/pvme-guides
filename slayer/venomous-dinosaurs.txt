**__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Venomous dinosaurs are the go-to for dinosaur tasks as they have the highest kills per hour of any dinosaur. Mid-level players will kill these for the Dinosaur tooth and Dinosaur rib bone - both of which are required to full upgrade the Anachronia base camp. End-game players will kill these for slayer logs.

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill : 1378 + 454.7 HP
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill : 1332
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: 480
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour : ~640k

**__Location and How to Get There__**
.tag:locationandhowtogetthere
Venomous Dinosaurs are located on the northwestern part of Anachronia. They can be accessed by 3 methods, arranged fastest to slowest:
⬥ **[1]** Anachronia Teleport to a Totem of the Abyss placed on the north totem pedestal (red path)
⬥ **[2]** Lodestone teleport to Anachronia (keyboard shortcut "Alt + D"), run to the Orthen teleportation device, teleport to Outpost (keyboard shortcut "O"), and run west (blue path)
⬥ **[3]** Lodestone teleport to Anachronia, and run north (yellow path)

> **__Melee__**
.tag:melee
⬥ Up to 450 KPH

Venomous Dinosaurs hit with melee auto-attacks. To prevent taking too much damage, the Enhanced Devoted <:enhdev4:712073087507628035> perk is helpful for survivability, as you will be praying Deflect Melee <:DeflectMelee:544195488447201300> . If using the Corbicula Rex perk from the Ranch out of Time, excess adrenaline can be used in the form of the <:zgs:626465964325601290> <:spec:537340400273195028> or <:dragonhalberd:841409589261238282> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>, these can be rotated between to greatly increase the damage dealt.

.

> **__Magic__**
.tag:magic
⬥ Up to 480 KPH

Thanks to the Venomous Dinossaurs' higher affinity for Magic <:Magic:689504724159823906> (and susceptibility to the Inquisitor Staff <:inquisitorstaff:694566917553520680> ), aswell as the extreme tankiness provided by both Animate Dead <:animatedead:856635090453135382> and Cryptbloom Armour <:cryptbloombody:892819107253194762> , it can achieve similar or slightly higher KPH than melee, while being both very safe and AFK.
