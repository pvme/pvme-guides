## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Profane Scabarites are weak to earth spells and have high defence. Using <:shadowburst:841419289461260329> with <:inquisitorstaff:694566917553520680> or <:shadowbarrage:567727956567261184> with <:fsoa:869284271595069451> will result in 100% accuracy whereas other spells will not.
### Stats
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 87
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 900
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~850 <:magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~765k <:magic:689504724159823906>

## __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ The scabarites are only found in the [Scabarite Cavern](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Scabarite_Cavern>) outside of Pollnivneach.
⬥ Each red arrow signifies 2 tiles of walking.
⬥ This location is in the south-western most part of the cavern.
⬥ Place cannon (not coil or cyclone) on blue square.
⬥ Stand in green square after loading cannon.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/VaR3USyqMW.png

.
## __Method: AFK (all styles)__
.tag:method1
Before loading your cannon and starting the session, check that:
⬥ Elder overload <:elderovl:841419289831800882> and weapon poison +++ <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active.
⬥ For all methods, set an Alt 1 alarm for 50% HP in case of bad RNG, and active <:excal:641337999170207763> when it does.

Magic <:magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ Pray <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008> and <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>
⬥ Autocast <:shadowburst:841419289461260329> or <:shadowbarrage:567727956567261184>
⬥ Ensure that <:animatedead:856635090453135382> is active if not in recommended gear.
