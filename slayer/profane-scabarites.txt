*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Profane Scabarites <:profanescabarite:1333926854666621019> are slayer monsters requiring 87 <:slayer:797896049548066857> to kill. They can be killed off-task, or with either a "Scabarites" or "Profane Scabarites" task for on-task bonuses.

.
### __Stats__
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level required: 87
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 900
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~850 <:magic:689504724159823906>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~765k <:magic:689504724159823906>

.
### __Desirable drops__
.tag:drops
⬥ The Spectral Lens <:spectrallens:1334309383831818342> which is the Necromancy slayer helm component.

## __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ Profane Scabarites are only found in the [Scabarite Cavern](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Scabarite_Cavern>) outside of Pollnivneach.

.
### __Getting to: Profane Scabarites__
⬥ Once inside the cavern, cross the bridge to south-western area.
    • Follow the image below to place and load your cannon.
    • Stand 2 tiles south-west of your cannon.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/VaR3USyqMW.png

.
## __Method: AFK Magic <:magic:689504724159823906>__
.tag:magicprofane

.
⬥ Set an Alt1 alarm for 50% HP in case of bad RNG, and active <:excal:641337999170207763> when it does.
⬥ Pray <:torment:583429936958930964> / <:affliction:513190158468907008> and <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>
⬥ Autocast <:shadowburst:841419289461260329> or <:shadowbarrage:567727956567261184>
