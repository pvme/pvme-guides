**__Spiritual Warriors__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Spiritual warriors have four different variants, each resembling their respective god within the God Wars Dungeon. They're mostly similar, except that Armadylean spiritual warriors **can't** be attacked with melee <:melee:1096130867279171706>

⬥ This guide will focus on killing spiritual warriors in the Sunken Pyramid, as this location yields the highest kills per hour
    • Other methods of killing spiritual warriors, such as in the God Wars Dungeon will not be discussed
⬥ They can be assigned as a slayer assignment <:slayer:797896049548066857> by Jacquelyn, The Raptor, Vannaka, Sumona, and Laniakea
    • They count as spiritual warriors, ghosts, and undead slayer tasks
        ⬩ Armadylean spiritual warriors also count as aviansie slayer tasks

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 68
    • level 99 Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> is required to access the Sunken Pyramid
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 231
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~1170 <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~1000 <:melee:1096130867279171706> (All AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~270k <:magic:689504724159823906>, ~231k <:melee:1096130867279171706> (All AFK)

⬥ Sealed clue scroll (hard) <:hardclue:1123586945273049109>

**__Location and How to Get there__**
.tag:location
The best place to kill spiritual warriors is in the Sunken Pyramid due to how spread out they are in other locations. You can teleport there with a teleport to house tablet, or with a Slayer Codex <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>

.
> **__Method: Magic and Melee__**
.tag:method
⬥ Spiritual warriors can be AFK'ed for extended periods with minimal inputs required
    • It is possible to AFK them with only one input every 10 minutes
⬥ Sacrifice <:sacrifice:513201065907322880> is used to help sustain your life points
⬥ They're aggressive to you if you're not wearing any armour for their respective faction; however, using aggression potions <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581> will net you a higher kph and requires less inputs
⬥ If you're using melee <:melee:1096130867279171706> you can't have Armadylean spiritual warriors

.
**__Where to stand__**
⬥ With your camera facing north, center yourself between the northern statue and the two green pots to the east
