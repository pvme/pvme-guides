> **__Introduction__**
.tag:introduction
Vileblooms are a combination task of all plant monsters on Anachronia, they are bad for both GP and XP, and should ideally be skipped every time. The main reason to do them is to obtain their soul for <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>

All methods in this guide are doable off-task. The kill per hour numbers listed are all based on off-task testing.

Due to the limited popularity of the task, the methods below are not fully optimised. Please make a post in <#1020853673317908500> if you manage to improve them.

.
**__Desirable Drops__**
.tag:desirabledrops
⬥ <:detoboots:602581956072439828> Blast diffusion boots - 1/2000 to 1/4000 on task
    • 4x rarer off-task
⬥ Three different totem pieces - 1/300
⬥ Dinosaur rib bone - 1/1000
⬥ Dinosaur tooth - 1/800

.

> __**Devil's snare**__
.tag:devilssnare
.
Besides gathering its soul, devil snares <:devilssnare:1003947791707557958> should generally never be done unless you are clearing a task and are under-leveled to do the other varieties.

.
**__Stats__**
.tag:devilssnare_stats
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill: 1759 (580.4 <:Constitution:689509250887712902>)
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1760
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: 180
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~317K

.
**__Method: Magic (AFK)__**
.tag:devilssnare_magicafk
.
Ensure you have the following active before switching on the <:cannon:869283142350041098>:
⬥ Soulsplit <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and Torment <:torment:583429936958930964> / Affliction <:Affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Autocasting Exsanguinate <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258>
⬥ Penance aura <:penanceaura:643505653062565907>
    • You may need Penance powder <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> if using Crypt <:cryptbloomhelm:892819107123195956>

<:animatedead:856635090453135382> is **not** required but is helpful when cannoning. No monitoring is required for the method and a legendary pet can help pick up all loot if not using <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>. If using <:aggressionscrim:1022562486588821555>, do not use <:elderovl:841419289831800882> as it DECREASES kills per hour due to the mechanics of <:aggressionscrim:1022562486588821555>

> __**Luminous snaggler**__
.tag:luminoussnaggler
.
Luminous snagglers are usually not done except for souls <:ushabti:1097515642749468752>

.
**__Stats__**
.tag:luminoussnaggler_stats
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill: 1719 (567.2 <:Constitution:689509250887712902>)
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1536
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: 160
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~246K

.
**__Method: AFK Magic__**
.tag:luminoussnaggler_magicafk
.
Stand on the tile shown below and ensure the following active before turning on <:aggressionscrim:1022562486588821555>:
⬥ Elder overload <:elderovl:841419289831800882>
⬥ Weapon poison +++ <:weppoison:689525476158472288>
⬥ Soulsplit <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and Torment <:torment:583429936958930964> / Affliction <:Affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Autocasting Exsanguinate <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258>
⬥ Penance aura <:penanceaura:643505653062565907> **or** Penance powder <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
    • Use <:vampaura:643505653079343144> if struggling to upkeep HP.
⬥ Animate Dead <:animatedead:856635090453135382>

No extra monitoring is required for the method except upkeeping buffs, a legendary pet can pick up all loot if not using <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>

.

> __**Liverworts**__
.tag:liverworts
.
Generally regarded as the highest <:slayer:797896049548066857> exp Vile bloom, players should still avoid doing these unless gathering souls for <:ushabti:1097515642749468752> as they are slow and prevent players from quickly moving onto better tasks.

.
**__Stats__**
⬥ <:combat:797896050370281523> XP per kill: 2970 (980.1 <:Constitution:689509250887712902>)
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 3338
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: 140
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~467k

.
**__Method: AFK Magic__**
.tag:liverworts_magicafk
.
Stand on the tile shown below and ensure the following are active before turning on <:aggressionscrim:1022562486588821555>:
⬥ Elder overload <:elderovl:841419289831800882>
⬥ Weapon poison +++ <:weppoison:689525476158472288>
⬥ Soulsplit <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and Affliction <:Affliction:513190158468907008>
    • Method might not work with Torment <:torment:583429936958930964>
⬥ Autocasting Exsanguinate <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258>
⬥ Animate Dead <:animatedead:856635090453135382>
⬥ Penance powder <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
⬥ Vampyrism aura <:vampaura:643505653079343144>
⬥ <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> summoned.
    • <:reaverscroll:1022562484378419284> not necessarily unless struggling to upkeep HP.

The gear shown below barely suffices for the method and players **should be wary of cutting any corners.** The method was tested off-task, players looking to do this with lower gear should check for themselves if it is possible with the additional damage on task.
