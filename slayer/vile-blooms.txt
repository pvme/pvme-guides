# Vile blooms
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
[Vile blooms](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Vile_blooms>) are a combination task of all plant monsters on Anachronia. The main reason to do them is for the [Lost Lands and Dungeons](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_collection_log#Lost_Lands_and_Dungeons>) collection log, [Skeka's hypnowand](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Skeka%27s_hypnowand>) pieces, or to obtain their soul for <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>

⬥ Vile blooms can be assigned as a Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857> assignment by Laniakea.
⬥ Due to the limited popularity of this task, the methods below are not fully optimised. Please make a post in <#1020853673317908500> if you manage to improve them.

## __Method: Devil's snare__
.tag:devilssnare
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/UJ2KNvT.png

.
### Stats
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 90
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1,760
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: ~180 <:magic:689504724159823906> (AFK)
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: ~317k <:magic:689504724159823906> (AFK)

.
### __AFK Magic__ <:magic:689504724159823906>
No monitoring is required for this method, and a legendary pet can help pick up all loot if not using <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/QchiZn77AH.png
⬥ From the position of the eastern-most <:devilssnare:1003947791707557958>, stand 4 west and 1 north. Set up <:cannon:869283142350041098> as shown in the image above.

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008>
⬥ Autocast <:exsanguinate:856635090745950258> and upkeep <:animatedead:856635090453135382>
    • <:animatedead:856635090453135382> is **not** required but is helpful if cannoning.
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:penanceaura:643505653062565907>
    • May also need <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226> if using <:cryptbloomhelm:1334336169336373339>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882> and <:weppoison:689525476158472288> are active at all times.
⬥ If using <:aggressionscrim:1022562486588821555>, do **not** use <:elderovl:841419289831800882> as it **DECREASES** kills per hour due to the mechanics of <:aggressionscrim:1022562486588821555>

### __AFK Magic__ <:magic:689504724159823906>
The gear shown below barely suffices for this method and players **should be wary of cutting any corners.** The method was tested off-task, players looking to do this with lower gear should check for themselves if it is possible with the additional damage on-task.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/5NliTI3STi.png
⬥ Locate the northern rubble in the oculi apoterrasaur area and position as shown above.

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:torment:583429936958930964> or <:affliction:513190158468907008>

⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
⬥ Use <:vampaura:643505653079343144>
⬥ Summon <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>
