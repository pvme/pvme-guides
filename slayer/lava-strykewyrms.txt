> **__Introduction__**
.tag:introduction
Lava Strykewyrms are one of the most profitable slayer tasks, while also providing decent Slayer XP. They have a guaranteed drop of Searing Ashes (worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C109$ <:coins:698816156961603654> each) and also drop the Wyrm Spike, Scalp and Heart.

.

**__Desirable Drops__**
.tag:desirabledrops
⬥ Lava Strykewyrms have a valuable loot table containing: Searing Ashes, Onyx Bolts (e), Rune salvage, coins and potions.
    • This makes them one of the better tasks to do for gp.

.
⬥ For the Slayer log, Lava Strykewyrms also drop:
    • Wyrm Spike, worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C98$  <:coins:698816156961603654>
    • Wyrm Heart, worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C99$ <:coins:698816156961603654>
    • Wyrm Scalp, worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C100$ <:coins:698816156961603654>
        ⬩ Dropped at a rate of 1/6168 on task (1/12,288 off task) from Lava Strykewyrms or 1/1,536 from WildyWyrms.

.
**__Location in the Wilderness__**
.tag:location
⬥ They are located South of the Lava Maze (level 34-41 wilderness).
    • The quickest way to get there is via the obelisk teleport to the level 35 obelisk.

> **__Method: Active Magic__**
.tag:methodmagic
After the Wilderness Update, killing Lava Strykewyrms has become substantially safer. Despite that, their drops are still highly valued because the uniques are quite rare and the Searing Ashes are in high demand as they are the main ingredient for Aggression Potions <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581>

This method is can be done with low effort, only requiring the player to click on each spawn and letting Revo++ kill them (~250 KPH), or with high effort, using both dual-wield and 2H weapons and full manual (~450 KPH).
