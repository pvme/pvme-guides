> **__Introduction__**
.tag:introduction
Lava Strykewyrms are one of the most profitable slayer tasks, while also providing decent Slayer XP. They have a guaranteed drop of Searing Ashes (worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C109$ <:coins:698816156961603654> each) and also drop the Wyrm Spike, Scalp and Heart.

⬥ After the Wilderness Update, killing Lava Strykewyrms has become substantially safer. Despite that, their drops are still highly valued mainly due to Searing Ashes being the main ingredient for Aggression Potions <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581>

.

**__Desirable Drops__**
.tag:desirabledrops
⬥ Lava Strykewyrms have a valuable loot table containing: Searing Ashes, Onyx Bolts (e), Rune salvage, coins and potions.
    • This makes them one of the better tasks to do for gp.

.
⬥ For the Slayer log, Lava Strykewyrms also drop:
    • Wyrm Spike <:wyrmspike:1023150743982723143> - worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C98$  <:coins:698816156961603654>
    • Wyrm Heart <:wyrmheart:1023150746721603625> - worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C99$ <:coins:698816156961603654>
    • Wyrm Scalp <:wyrmscalp:1023150749091381248> - worth $data_pvme:Slayer!C100$ <:coins:698816156961603654>
        ⬩ Dropped at a rate of 1/6168 on task (1/12,288 off task) from Lava Strykewyrms or 1/1,536 from WildyWyrms.

.
**__Location and how to get there__**
.tag:location
⬥ They are located South of the Lava Maze (level 34-41 wilderness).
    • The quickest way to get there is via the obelisk teleport to the level 35 obelisk.

> **__General strategy__**
Place your cannon <:cannon:869283142350041098> between the 2 small lava pits as shown below. You will alternate between killing the strykes northwest and southeast of this spot, most of which can be hit by the cannon.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/LTV5mCPKc0.png
.
> **__Method: Active Magic__**
.tag:methodmagic
This method is can be done with low effort, only requiring the player to click on each spawn and letting Revo++ kill them (~250 KPH), or with high effort, using both dual-wield and 2H weapons and full manual (~450 KPH).

⬥ If 4TAA, stamp on mounds with <:inquisitorstaff:694566917553520680> equipped and input the auto when you see your character do the stomp animation.

> __**Method: Active Ranged**__
.tag:ranged
If <:bolg:994189289623662702> is owned, <:range:580168050121113623> is the best style for killing lava strykewyrms due to its high damage, range and mobility.

⬥ Upkeep <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> at all times.

⬥ Proc priority for <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> if 3 stacks: <:tuskas:513201065513058306> > <:shadowtend:642713547142332416> > <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> > <:rapid:535541270521708566> > <:snipe:535541258425204770>
    • Don't let autos snipe <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> proc when using <:shadowtend:642713547142332416>
    • Stacked damage buffs of <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> <:infernalpuzzlebox:994189299681607800> <:fularrow:971025696958853180> <:reckless:643505179378974748> <:scriptureofful:902209626412560395> allow you to easily hit hitcap with <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> even outside of <:deathsswift:994921434633744455>

⬥ <:grico:787904334812807238> <:mds:535541259033378827> <:piercing:535541258538450944> are your main basics, use them to balance stacks and keep an eye on your threshold cooldowns.

.
⬥ Common ability sequences for kills include (adjust order based on <:perfectequilibrium:1006119102814887957> stack count):
1. <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:bolg:994189289623662702> <:spec:537340400273195028> or <:shadowtend:642713547142332416> → <:mds:535541259033378827> if needed
2. <:piercing:535541258538450944> → <:snap:535534127131394088> → <:grico:787904334812807238>
3. <:rapid:535541270521708566> → <:grico:787904334812807238>/<:mds:535541259033378827>
4. <:grico:787904334812807238> → <:tuskas:513201065513058306> (helpful for building adren)
