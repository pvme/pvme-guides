**__Tormented Demons__**
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Tormented demons are a demon slayer monster, can be slain with no slayer level requirement. To get a task for them requires the ability to have them assigned bought from a slayer master for 50 slayer points.

⬥ Besides the default tormented demons task, you can also kill them for a Demons cluster task.

> **__AFK Strategy__**
.tag:afkstrategy
Tormented Demons can be effectively AFK'd in the PoSD using necromancy with a blood reaver familiar and weapon poison.
This method achieves ~240kph off-task, so on-task rates may be higher.
