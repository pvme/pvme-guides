# Tormented Demons
*Note: a **Table of Contents** can be found in the pins.*

.
## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Tormented demons are classified as demons, and can be slain with no Slayer level <:slayer:797896049548066857> requirement. They can be assigned as a task after completing [While Guthix Sleeps](<https://runescape.wiki/w/While_Guthix_Sleeps>) and purchasing the ability to have them assigned, for 50 Slayer points <:slayer:797896049548066857>

⬥ Besides the default Tormented demon task, you can also kill them for Demons cluster tasks

.
### Stats

## __Location and How to Get there__
.tag:location
⬥ Sunken Pyramid (either northern room, 4 spawns)
    • In either large room, position yourself anywhere near the center of all the spawns
    • You can teleport there with a teleport to house tablet, or with a Slayer Codex <:slayercodex:1097517249499254875>

.
## __Method: Necromancy__
.tag:method1
Four Tormented Demon souls should be placed into a large room in the Sunken Pyramid, use Necromancy <:necromancy:1148995625896120460> with a blood reaver familiar and weapon poison.

⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and <:sorrow:1137941003895046284>
⬥ Sustain <:prayer:1005450651347730533> with <:demonhornnecklace:975765831248130079> + <:ectoplasmator:1023152065431744542> and <:powderofpenance:928221126360969226>
⬥ Ensure <:elderovl:841419289831800882>, <:weppoison:689525476158472288> and <:aggressionpotion:925794592199147581> are active at all times
