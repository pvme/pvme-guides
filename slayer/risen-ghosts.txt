## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
Risen Ghosts are generally only killed for the Ultimate Slayer title. Otherwise, the XP and GP/hr are mediocre at best.

.
### Stats
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> level: 85
⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per kill: 1000
⬥ Optimal kills per hour: 800 <:magic:689504724159823906> 720 <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>
⬥ Optimal <:slayer:797896049548066857> XP per hour: 800k <:magic:689504724159823906> 720k <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>

.

### __Location and Positioning__
.tag:location
.
Risen Ghosts can be found in the [Crypt](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Crypt_(Wilderness)>) North of Fort Forinthry after the question [Unwelcome Guests](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Unwelcome_Guests>) is complete.

For both methods, place an <:oldakcoil:798285651387613194> in the tile shown and stand underneath it.

.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/MIlLxZRIhW.png

.
