> **__Recommended Perk Setups & Gizmo Layouts__**

.
__**Introduction and Notes**__
.tag:intro
⬥ This channel covers perk setups, where to put them on your gear, and how to obtain them. For explanations of the perks used, see <#689236234912137266>.
⬥ This channel will first list all setups, then perk gizmo combos.
    • Insert components *in the order listed in the combo*.

⬥ For augments where there are multiple gizmos, gizmos are seperated by **+** symbol.
    • e.g. Precise 6 Aftershock 1 + Aftershock 4 Equilbrium 2 → one gizmo with P6AS1, and a second gizmo with AS4E2.

⬥ **X** represents a secondary perk to combo with the first perk *in a single gizmo*.
    • e.g. Biting 4 X → Biting 4 comboed with a valid perk (like Biting 4 Mobile).

⬥ If you get Aftershock, you will need it on all your weapons for the style.
