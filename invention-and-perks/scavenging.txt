> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Scavenging <:scavenging:1109429520995463288> is an <:invention:689509250946695292> perk which provides players a chance to get **uncommon** or **rare** components *as a drop*, whenever the player kills an NPC.

⬥ Each rank provides a 1% chance per rank for uncommon components, with a 1% chance that they will be rare components instead.
    • Scavenging 4 <:scavenging4:1005934140669046824> on level 20 equipment provides a **4.4%** chance per kill, of activation, with a **1%** chance that the components will be **rare** insetad of **uncommon**

⬥ More information about it here: <https://runescape.wiki/w/Scavenging>

.
⬥ Killing monsters en masse for <:scavenging:1109429520995463288> procs is called **scav farming**, and is a low effort/AFK way to obtain a variety of components.

.
> **__Obtaining Scavenging 4__**
.tag:drops
⬥ With ancient invention unlocked, scavenging 4 should be obtained by putting 9 <:preciouscomp:583434604724224008> into an <:ancientarmourgizmo:697405383769194516>. Make sure you use an <:extremeinventionpotion:1032540072169439242>

> **__Useful unlocks__**
.tag:unlocks
.
Items that improve the rates and profit from scav farming include:

⬥ Scripture of Bik <:scriptureofbik:892342109791735848>
    • Only if you want clues.
    • Ensure <:bonecrusher:975284344526602240> is **toggled off** for <:scriptureofbik:892342109791735848> to trigger during combat

⬥ Full set of Superior Seasinger's armour <:seasingershood:556583076050763776>
    • 1/2000 chance to receive ports resource crates.

⬥ Death note relic <:deathnote:1023150755093426216>
    • Allows for automatic looting of noted bones in conjunction with the bonecrusher, when Scripture of Bik isn't being used.

⬥ Caroming 4 <:caroming4:791281588792590336>
    • Alllows for more KPH and scav procs.

.
> **__Method - Dedicated scav farming__**
.tag:skeletons
The most effective method to farm scavenging procs is to AoE skeletons in the Lumbridge Catacombs.

⬥ Activate <:scriptureofful:902209626412560395>
⬥ Activate incense sticks <:spiritsticks:565726489136463894> <:lantasticks:565726489404899368>
⬥ Summon <:smokedevil:1109432590865281126>
    • Stock with <:dustcloud:1109432587086200953>
    • Ensure the autofire rate is set to 1.
⬥ Upkeep <:holyaggroverload:1049230112849133589>
⬥ Ensure <:autoretaliate:1084799717726228530> is on.

.
__**Location and how to get there**__
The catacombs are southeast of the Lumbridge lodestone <:lumbridgelode:1032552337035767839>. Go to the bottom floor and walk south-east then south, to where you will find the Topaz statuette.
