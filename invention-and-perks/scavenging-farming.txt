> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Scavenging <:scavenging:1109429520995463288> is an <:invention:689509250946695292> perk which provides players a chance to get **uncommon** or **rare** components *as a drop*, whenever they kill an NPC.

⬥ Each rank provides a 1% chance per rank for uncommon components to drop from a kill, with a 1% chance that they will be rare components instead.
    • Scavenging 4 <:scavenging4:1005934140669046824> on level 20 equipment provides a **4.4%** chance per kill, of activation, with a **1%** chance that the components will be **rare** instead of **uncommon**.
⬥ Check the wiki for more information about [Scavenging](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Scavenging>) <:scavenging:1109429520995463288>
⬥ Killing monsters en masse for <:scavenging:1109429520995463288> procs is called **scav farming**, a low effort/AFK way to obtain a variety of components.

.
> **__Acquiring scav 4__**
.tag:getscav
⬥ With ancient invention unlocked, scavenging 4 <:scavenging4:1005934140669046824> should be obtained by placing 9 <:preciouscomp:583434604724224008> into an <:ancientarmourgizmo:697405383769194516>
    • Prepare a few gizmos as the chance for <:scavenging4:1005934140669046824> is `1/15.4 at 137 invention`.
    • Visit the wiki's [perk calculator](<https://tinyurl.com/bdfskkd9>) to calculate your chances at lower <:invention:689509250946695292> levels.
    • Make sure you use an <:extremeinventionpotion:1032540072169439242>

> __**Where to use scav**__
.tag:others
Besides dedicated farming via <:scavenging:1109429520995463288> (method below), it's a convenient perk to have for passive profit when engaging in any activity which requires killing large amounts of enemies. Consider bringing an armour piece with <:scavenging4:1005934140669046824> if doing:

⬥ <:slayer:797896049548066857> Slayer creatures - Check out <#1020024227115573369> for details on methods. Examples include:
    • Killing <:nightmare:1004107308516782111> - Popular AFK moneymaker, doable on and off-task.
    • Killing Vyres - Popular combat method for training <:prayer:1005450651347730533> and <:farming:513213693790519307>
    • Killing Dwogres - Popular combat method for AFK gp via dragon pickaxe drops <:dragonpick:1145061199118086215>
⬥ Champion scroll farming.
⬥ <:seiryu:583430543321071626> <:bsd:583430542326759435> <:ambassador:583430542809235458> <:zamorak:994914159969976440> Elite Dungeons - More effective for trash runs.
⬥ <:cluescroll:1037463867716018206> Gathering clues via combat.
⬥ <:tzkalzuk:931175752651653121> Wave 4 Zuk farming - Semi-afk moneymaker, refer to this [guide](<https://youtu.be/_1HKxuM9z7Q>)
⬥ Killing chickens - Lossless to kill with <:oldakcoil:798285651387613194> when doing some skilling activities.

> **__Method - Lumbridge Catacombs__**
.tag:corpsespiders
The most effective method to farm scavenging procs is to AoE corpse spiders in the Lumbridge Catacombs. There aren't any noteworthy drops besides the occasional spider egg, which could be ignored.

.
__**Location and positioning**__
The catacombs are southeast of the Lumbridge lodestone <:lumbridgelode:1032552337035767839>. Go to the bottom floor and walk south-east, to reach the third room.

⬥ Stand on the on red 'X' shown below, 3 north of the middle torch on the southern wall.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/6STOzO25ii.png

> **__Method - Chaos Dwarf Battlefield__**
.tag:dwogres
After completing [Forgiveness of a Chaos Dwarf](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Forgiveness_of_a_Chaos_Dwarf>), the Chaos Dwarf Battlefield is unlocked.

⬥ This location hosts an abundance of mobs which are profitable to kill.
    • Chaos dwogres, chaos dwarves and hand cannoneers can all drop the dragon pickaxe <:dragonpick:1145061199118086215>
        ⬩ Chaos dwarf hand cannoneers can drop the hand cannon <:handcannon:860891495037272094>

.
**__Location and positioning__**
The Chaos Dwarf Battlefield can be easily accessed by using a <:lotd:566453486913323042> to teleport to Keldagrim, and running south past the Giant Dwarf statue, then following the southwestern path to the cave entrace.

⬥ With your camera facing north, place your Oldak coil on the red X and stand on the yellow X.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/H3BGRVjIFX.png
