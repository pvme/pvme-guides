> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Helwyr, the Seren general residing in the Heart of Gielinor, requires 80 magic and 40 Seren killcount to fight. The notable drops for this boss are the Cywir Wand <:cywirwand:643161591571021824>, Cywir Orb, <:cywirorb:643161591210049556> and Crest of Seren <:crestseren:643161511937966122>. These items can be augmented and disassembled for Cywir components <:cywircomp:583430147538026496> which are required for Planted Feet <:pf:689501925770919981>.

⬥ This method will net less kills per hour but may be used if you do not wish to put high amounts of effort into a mid level boss.
⬥ It is important to pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> as this method relies on constant healing. It is also important to use the **Animate Dead** spell, as the method heavily relies on its usage.

.
⬥ Aura choice can vary, but will affect how afk kills can be. They are listed in order of effectiveness:
    • <:vamp:643505653079343144> Vampyrism Aura provides extra safety through its healing and allows more chances for poison damage. Due to the constant damage being taken while fighting Helwyr, this is by far the safest and most reliable aura choice to afk with.
    • <:penance:643505653062565907> Penance Aura allows for the most afk experience, but is less safe to use and doesn't benefit the usage of a Blood Reaver familiar.
    • <:dm:643505652571963417> Dark Magic provides extra damage, but you must pay more attention to both your HP and your prayer points. This should be an effective aura for fast kills, but is the least afk choice.
    • <:mahj:643148943856762890> Mahjarrat Aura provides extra damage, but does not give any extra poison procs. Still, it is a viable option as a secondary aura.
    • <:mani:643505153709965322> **Do __not__ use Maniacal** for afking, as it will get you killed very easily.

.
⬥ For fastest kill speeds, a Wand of the Praesul <:praeswand:643166769518739477> and Imperium Core <:Core:625304432581672961> should be used. A Seismic Wand <:seiswand:583429704837758997> and Singularity <:sing:513190159261630467>, or Elite Seasinger's Kiba <:eliteseasingerwand:581180345978716170> and Magikai <:eliteseasingerorb:581180345345638423> are slightly slower substitutes.

*Note: Assumes Max Reputation and $data_pvme:Bosses!B340$ kph.*
**__Explanation__**
⬥ This method will net less kills per hour but may be used if you do not wish to put high amounts of effort into a mid level boss.
⬥ It is important to pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> as this method relies on constant healing.

.
⬥ Aura choice can vary, but will affect how afk kills can be. They are listed in order of effectiveness:
    • <:vamp:643505653079343144> Vampyrism Aura provides extra safety through its healing and allows more chances for poison damage. Due to the constant damage being taken while fighting Helwyr, this is by far the safest and most reliable aura choice to afk with.
    • <:penance:643505653062565907> Penance Aura allows for the most afk experience, but is less safe to use and doesn't benefit the usage of a Blood Reaver familiar.
    • <:dm:643505652571963417> Dark Magic provides extra damage, but you must pay more attention to both your HP and your prayer points. This should be an effective aura for fast kills, but is the least afk choice.
    • <:mahj:643148943856762890> Mahjarrat Aura provides extra damage, but does not give any extra poison procs. Still, it is a viable option as a secondary aura.
    • <:berserker:643505116347105290> **Do __not__ use Berserker** for afking, as it will get you killed very easily.
