> __**Ability Themes**__
.tag:abilitythemes
.
Ranged abilities vary in their themes. Some are strong individually, some comboed and some provide powerful utility values. Currently, they can be interpreted into four different classes with three different damage types.

.
**__Classes: __**
⬥ **Power**: Deal high damage.
⬥ **Auxiliary**: Deal equal or less damage but enhance other abilities.
⬥ **Utility**: provide low or non-damaging utility buffs.
⬥ **Subpar**: as we know of lack any serious use. Generally avoid unless theorycrafted in advance.

.
**__Damage Types:__**
⬥ **Instant**: Deal damage immediately
    • *Examples:* <:piercing:535541258538450944> <:bindingshot:535541306563231790> <:snap:535534127131394088> <:needle:535541259108876293> <:dazing:535541307142307860> <:grico:787904334812807238>

⬥ **Channelled**: Deal channelled damage.
    • Channels lock the player into using the ability.
    • Can be cancelled early, losing the later hits of the ability.
    • *Examples:* <:snipe:535541258425204770> <:rapid:535541270521708566>

⬥ **DoT/bleed**: Deal damage that is spread over time.
    • Usually not boosted by <:deathsswift:994921434633744455>
    • Are boosted by <:reckless:643505179378974748> <:mahj:643148943856762890> <:Vuln:537349530551582720>
        ⬩ Full list here: **Damage over Time** - <https://runescape.wiki/w/Abilities#Damage_over_Time_abilities>
    • *Examples:* <:frag:535541273755385885> <:corruptshot:535541306294796299> <:deadshot:535541307666595870>
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/TrdFFkD.png

> __**Threshold Abilities**__
.tag:thresholds
.
Ranged thresholds are usually stronger than basics, but cost adrenaline to use. These abilities have 2 purposes:
⬥ Sacrifice adrenaline (-15%)
⬥ Deal higher damage

⬥ __**Power Thresholds:**__
    • *Examples:* <:snap:535534127131394088> <:rapid:535541270521708566> <:shadowtend:642713547142332416> <:tightbindingsflank:867678154096640010>
        ⬩ <:rapid:535541270521708566> *does snare however is more renowned for its damage.*

⬥ __**Utility Thresholds:**__
    • *Examples:* <:tight:535541275957657600>

⬥ __**Subpar Thresholds:**__
    • *Examples:* <:stw:535541259109138463>

__**Auxiliary/Utility Examples:**__
⬥ <:tight:535541275957657600> : Stuns target for 3.6s.

> __**Ultimate Abilities**__
.tag:ultimates
.
Ranged Ultimates deal maximum damage and or elevate other abilities. They typically have 1-3 purposes:
⬥ Sacrifice 100% adrenaline
⬥ Deal devastating damage
⬥ Provide a longstanding buff

⬥ __**Power Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386>

⬥ __**Auxiliary Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:deathsswift:994921434633744455>

⬥ __**Utility Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:incend:535541258429661215>

⬥ __**Subpar Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples: <:deadshot:535541307666595870> <:unload:535541258664411169>*

**__Death's Swiftness__**:
⬥ <:deathsswift:994921434633744455> is Ranged's strongest ultimate ability and is the core of Ranged dps.
⬥ Typically, ranged will cast <:deathsswift:994921434633744455> and then execute a series of abilities to maximise damage output during the uptime of <:deathsswift:994921434633744455>.
    • This is called a <:deathsswift:994921434633744455> **Death's Swiftness Rotation.**
    • The **Death's Swiftness Rotation** will be covered after introducing weapons.

> **__Weapons & Gear__**:
.tag:weapons&gear

⬥ In this section, we'll introduce the weapon used for the rest of the guide.
⬥ Ranged weapon(s) have several key descriptors which describe their capabilities.
*If you are familiar with this feel free to skip ahead.*

**__Sample Weapon Breakdown:__**
⬥ **Upgraded bone blowpipe:** <:upgradedboneblowpipe:925794591830056961>
    • **__Accuracy__**: This is assigned from the **Main Hand**'s accuracy value.
        ⬩ <:upgradedboneblowpipe:925794591830056961> has a t90 accuracy value of 2458.

    • **__ Ability Damage__**: Ability attack damage from the respective weapon.
        ⬩ This is damage dealt with **Abilities.**
        ⬩ <:upgradedboneblowpipe:925794591830056961> has tier 85 ability damage: ~1224.

    • **__Tier__**: Requirement to wield.
        ⬩ <:upgradedboneblowpipe:925794591830056961> requires level 87 ranged to wield.

    • **__Passive(s)__**:
        ⬩ <:upgradedboneblowpipe:925794591830056961> has a passive that causes poison hit splats to appear twice as often, but do half the damage. In theory and practice, this doesn't do anything - however, because it doesn't cause double procs from <:cinderbanes:513190158355660812> it actually becomes a **decrease** in damage once you unlock those.

> **__Vod Reviewing__**
.tag:review
.
Refining is considerably the most important practice of improving DPM. It exercises many healthy thought processes to rationalize where a boss encounter did not go as planned. Viewing a **recording or VoD** of your encounter is critical to identifying mistakes.

Below, is a video sample of the **rough rotation** alongside a list of mistakes. Try identifying the mistakes inside the video as an exercise.

.
> **__Identifying Mistakes:__**
.tag:identifying
.
**__The vod__**
https://youtu.be/-DDbnHqRJYU
.
**__The mistakes__**
⬥ Firstly, we use a **Subpar Threshold:** <:tight:535541275957657600> - as we will see later we can change the rotation to have another **Power Threshold**.
⬥ Secondly, we use <:snap:535534127131394088> before <:rapid:535541270521708566> - if we swap them we can use both twice
⬥ Thirdly, we can save ticks from abilities by using something called cancelling.
