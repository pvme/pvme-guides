> __**Ability Themes**__
.tag:abilitythemes
.
Melee abilities vary in their themes. Some are strong individually, some comboed and some provide powerful utility values. Currently, they can be interpreted into four different classes with three different damage types.

**__Classes: __**
⬥ **Power**: Deal high damage.
⬥ **Auxiliary**: Deal equal or less damage but enhance other abilities.
⬥ **Utility**: provide low or non-damaging utility buffs.
⬥ **Subpar**: as we know of lack any serious use. Generally avoid unless theorycrafted in advance.

**__Damage Types:__**
⬥ **Instant**: Deal damage immediately
    • *Examples:* <:cleave:535532878616985610> <:cane:535532878969438210> <:deci:535532879325822986> <:sever:535532879577612298> <:havoc:535532879300526080> <:gbarge:535532879250456578> <:punish:535532879439069184> <:slice:535532879594258432>

⬥ **Channeled**: Deal channeled damage.
    • Channels lock the player into using the ability.
    • After 1.8s or 3t, it can be **freely cancelled** by casting another ability.
    • *Examples:* <:fury:535532879510372352> <:gflurry:535532879283879977> <:flurry:864492981763702834> <:destroy:535532879330148352> <:assault:535532853979512842>

⬥ **Bleed/Burn**: Deal damage that is spread over time.
    • This is isn't boostable in <:Berserk:513190158468907012> <:zgs:626465964325601290><:spec:537340400273195028>
    • But is boosted by <:DBA:603979368850653216><:spec:537340400273195028> <:berserker:643505116347105290> <:mahj:643148943856762890> <:Vuln:537349530551582720>
        - Full list here: **Damage over Time** - <https://runescape.wiki/w/Abilities#Damage_over_Time_abilities>
    • *Examples:* <:dismember:535532879376023572> <:bloodtend:513190158431158274> <:slaughter:535532879237873666>

> __**Threshold Abilities**__
.tag:thresholds
.
Melee thresholds are stronger than basics however are more taxing to use. These abilities have 2 purposes:
⬥ Sacrifice adrenaline (-15%)
⬥ Deal higher damage

⬥ __**Power Thresholds:**__
    • *Examples:* <:assault:535532853979512842> <:destroy:535532879330148352> <:cane:535532878969438210> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> <:slaughter:535532879237873666>
        - <:destroy:535532879330148352> *does stun however is more renowned for its damage.*

⬥ __**Utility Thresholds:**__
    • *Examples:* <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> <:gflurry:535532879283879977> <:quake:535532879506309150>

⬥ __**Subpar Thresholds:**__
    • *Examples:* <:stomp:535532879552315412>

__**Auxiliary/Utility Examples:**__
⬥ <:gflurry:535532879283879977> : Reduces remaining cooldown of <:Berserk:513190158468907012>
⬥ <:quake:535532879506309150> : Lowers the enemy's defenses for 60s.
⬥ <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> : Stuns target for 3.6s.

> __**Ultimate Abilities**__
.tag:ultimates
.
Melee Ultimates deal maximum damage and or elevate other abilities. They typically have 1-3 purposes:
⬥ Sacrifice 100% adrenaline
⬥ Deal devastating damage
⬥ Provide a longstanding buff

⬥ __**Power Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633>

⬥ __**Auxiliary Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:Berserk:513190158468907012> <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439>

⬥ __**Utility Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:pulverise:535532879053062146>

⬥ __**Subpar Ultimates:**__
    • *Examples:* <:balancedstrike:535532854336290826> <:frenzy:535532879279554581> <:massacre:535532879384543253> <:overpower:535532879334080517>

**__Berserk__**:
⬥ <:Berserk:513190158468907012> is Melee's strongest ability and has been the core of Melee DPS since its release.
⬥ Typically, melee will cast <:Berserk:513190158468907012> and then optimize a series of abilities while <:Berserk:513190158468907012> is active for high burst.
    • This is called a <:Berserk:513190158468907012> **Berserk Rotation.**

> __**Refining each mistake**__
.tag:refining
⬥ **#1:** <:deci:535532879325822986> > <:havoc:535532879300526080>
    • <:deci:535532879325822986> deals more damage.
    • Net Gain: `+18.5%`

⬥ **#2: shorten <:gflurry:535532879283879977> , cancel with <:deci:535532879325822986>**
    • Shortening <:gflurry:535532879283879977> from 7t to 5t, 4 hits to 3 hits.
        - Allows <:deci:535532879325822986> to cancel <:gflurry:535532879283879977>
        - Net Loss: `-94.2%`
        - Gain 2t of time. Can squeeze in another ability; **#3**.

⬥ **#3: Add** <:cleave:535532878616985610>
    • Gains +8% adren.
    • Net Gain: `104.6%`
        - *Recall <:cleave:535532878616985610> is casted with t80 damage <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872>*

⬥ **#4:** **Cancel <:assault:535532853979512842> with** <:cane:535532878969438210>
    • This places <:cane:535532878969438210> inside <:Berserk:513190158468907012>
    • Retain all <:assault:535532853979512842> hitsplats
    • Net Gain: `+256%`

__**Results:**__
*Average Ability Damage, (AAD) = Average ability % of rotation*
.
⬥ **Rough vs Final** AAD: 1804% vs 2089.1%
    • ~16% DPS increase
⬥ **Final** rotation deals ~16% more damage
    • `(2089.1/1804) - 1 ~ 0.158`,
⬥ **Net Gain** from these 4 fixes:
    • `256% + 18.5% + 104.6% - 94% = 285.1%`
⬥ Total AAD difference between Final and Rough:
    •  `2089.1% - 1804% = 285.1%`
