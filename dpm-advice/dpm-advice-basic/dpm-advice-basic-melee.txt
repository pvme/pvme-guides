## __Ability Themes__
.tag:abilitythemes
.
Melee abilities vary in their themes. Some are strong individually, some comboed and some provide powerful utility values. Currently, they can be interpreted into four different classes with three different damage types.

### Classes:
⬥ **Power**: Deal high damage.
⬥ **Auxiliary**: Deal equal or less damage but enhance other abilities.
⬥ **Utility**: provide low or non-damaging utility buffs.
⬥ **Subpar**: as we know of lack any serious use. Generally avoid unless theorycrafted in advance.

### Damage Types:
⬥ **Instant**: Deal damage immediately
    • *Examples:* <:cleave:535532878616985610> <:cane:535532878969438210> <:deci:535532879325822986> <:sever:535532879577612298> <:havoc:535532879300526080> <:gbarge:535532879250456578> <:punish:535532879439069184> <:slice:535532879594258432> <:fury:535532879510372352>

⬥ **Channelled**: Deal channelled damage.
    • Channels lock the player into using the ability.
    • After 1.8s or 3t, it can be **freely cancelled** by casting another ability.
    • *Examples:* <:gflurry:535532879283879977> <:flurry:864492981763702834> <:destroy:535532879330148352> <:assault:535532855191928842>

⬥ **Bleed/Burn**: Deal damage that is spread over time.
    • This is isn't boostable in <:zerk:535532854004678657> <:zgs:626465964325601290> <:spec:537340400273195028>
    • But is boosted by <:DBA:603979368850653216> <:spec:537340400273195028> <:berserker:643505116347105290> <:mahj:643148943856762890> <:Vuln:537349530551582720>
        ⬩ Full list here: **Damage over Time** - <https://runescape.wiki/w/Abilities#Damage_over_Time_abilities>
    • *Examples:* <:dismember:535532879376023572> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> <:slaughter:535532879237873666>

## __Threshold Abilities__
.tag:thresholds
.
Melee thresholds are stronger than basics however are more taxing to use. These abilities have 2 purposes:
⬥ Sacrifice 15% adrenaline.
⬥ Deal higher damage.

⬥ __**Power:**__
    • <:assault:535532855191928842> <:destroy:535532879330148352> <:cane:535532878969438210> <:bloodtendrils:535532854327640064> <:slaughter:535532879237873666>
        - <:destroy:535532879330148352> *does stun however is more renowned for its damage.*

⬥ __**Utility:**__
    • <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> <:gflurry:535532879283879977> <:quake:535532879506309150>

⬥ __**Subpar:**__
    • <:stomp:535532879552315412>

### Auxiliary/Utility Examples:
⬥ <:gflurry:535532879283879977> : Reduces remaining cooldown of <:zerk:535532854004678657>
⬥ <:quake:535532879506309150> : Lowers the enemy's defences for 60s.
⬥ <:fbackhand:535532879346794516> : Stuns target for 3.6s.

## __Ultimate Abilities__
.tag:ultimates
.
Melee Ultimates deal maximum damage and or elevate other abilities. They typically have 1-3 purposes:
⬥ Sacrifice 100% adrenaline.
⬥ Deal devastating damage.
⬥ Provide a longstanding buff.

⬥ __**Power:**__
    • <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633>

⬥ __**Auxiliary:**__
    • <:zerk:535532854004678657> <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439>

⬥ __**Utility:**__
    • <:pulverise:535532879053062146>

⬥ __**Subpar:**__
    • <:balancedstrike:535532854336290826> <:frenzy:535532879279554581> <:massacre:535532879384543253> <:overpower:535532879334080517>

### Sample Weapon Breakdown:
⬥ **Dragon Rider Lance, Dark Ice Shard and Dark Ice Sliver:** <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677>
    • **__Accuracy__**: This is assigned from the **Main Hand**'s accuracy value.
        - <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> has a t85 accuracy value of 2178.
        - <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> has a t90 accuracy value of 2458.

    • **__Damage__**: Auto attack damage from the respective weapon.
        - <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> has a t88 damage value of: 844.5.
        - <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677> has an off-hand t88 damage value of: 422.4.
        - <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> has a t80 damage value of 1788.

    • **__Attack Speed__**: Auto attack speed of each weapon.
        - <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677> each auto attack once every 2.4s (4t).
        - <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> auto attacks once every 3.6s (6t).

    • **__Tier__**: Requirement to wield.
        - Both Require 85 Attack.

.
    • **__Passive__: <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677> **
        - <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> and <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677> have a 5% and 1% respective chance to apply <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> **Primordial ice.**
        - With <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677>, gaining stacks of <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> triggers <:frostblades:1219291286578794627> Frostblades for 9s.
        - <:frostblades:1219291286578794627> adds bonus ability damage to your abilities during the duration.

    • **__Special Attack: Icy Tempest:__** <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677>
        - Deals 100-120% and 150-180% aoe damage, consuming <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> stacks.
        - Damage is increased per <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> stack.

    • **__Passive__: <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872>**
        - Halberd class 2H weapon.
        - Halberds gain +1 attack range.
        - *Example: <:cane:535532878969438210> normally strikes in a 3x3 aoe; strikes in a 5x5 with*<:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872>

.
⬥ **__Ability Damage__**:
    • This is damage dealt with **abilities.**
    • It is calculated with Auto attack damage value(s), weapon attack speed, strength level and other boosts.
        - <:darkiceshard:915232154160594974> <:darkicesliver:915232153787334677> have Tier 88 ability damage: **~1637.65. **
        - <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> has Tier 80 ability damage: **~1523.25**
        - *With 99 strength, no boosts.*

## __Refining each mistake__
.tag:refining
⬥ **#1:** <:deci:535532879325822986> > <:havoc:535532879300526080>
    • <:deci:535532879325822986> deals more damage.
    • Net Gain: `+18.5%`

⬥ **#2: shorten <:gflurry:535532879283879977> , cancel with <:deci:535532879325822986>**
    • Shortening <:gflurry:535532879283879977> from 7t to 5t, 4 hits to 3 hits.
        ⬩ Allows <:deci:535532879325822986> to cancel <:gflurry:535532879283879977>
        ⬩ Net Loss: `-94.2%`
        ⬩ Gain 2t of time. Can squeeze in another ability; **#3**.

⬥ **#3: Add** <:cleave:535532878616985610>
    • Gains +8% adren.
    • Net Gain: `104.6%`
        ⬩ *Recall <:cleave:535532878616985610> is casted with t80 damage <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872>*

⬥ **#4:** **Cancel <:assault:535532855191928842> with** <:cane:535532878969438210>
    • This places <:cane:535532878969438210> inside <:zerk:535532854004678657>
    • Retain all <:assault:535532855191928842> hitsplats
    • Net Gain: `+256%`

### Results:
*Average Ability Damage, (AAD) = Average ability % of rotation*
.
⬥ **Rough vs Final** AAD: 1804% vs 2089.1%
    • ~16% DPS increase
⬥ **Final** rotation deals ~16% more damage
    • `(2089.1/1804) - 1 ~ 0.158`,
⬥ **Net Gain** from these 4 fixes:
    • `256% + 18.5% + 104.6% - 94% = 285.1%`
⬥ Total AAD difference between Final and Rough:
    • `2089.1% - 1804% = 285.1%`
