### __Important Notes__
⬥ For <:backhandflank:867678153854025779> spam click the opposite side of the boss in order to walk underneath
⬥ Drink <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787> throughout the fight to juice <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714>
⬥ Use <:veng:543478434953822208> at <:war:1005552206302416986> so its off cooldown for P1
⬥ All floor AoE attacks are dodged appropriately using <:bd:535532854281764884>
⬥ <:consecratedkeris:1334318846202478615> is used for all bleeds, <:mwspear:694566917456789554> is only used for <:zekkil:903244090953588787> <:spec:537340400273195028> in P1 and <:cleave:535532878616985610> in the Shadowsands
