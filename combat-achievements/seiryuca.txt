### __Mage Melee Hybrid__
.tag:magemelee
⬥ This rotation is **NOT** recommended for standard kills, only for the achievement.
    • The setup takes extra time, thus lowering average kills per hour.

⬥ With this rotation it is possible to hit ~0:56.4 kill times.
⬥ For Melee you pray <:Piety:1215399597581533264> + <:eclipsedsoul:1285372688457797672> + <:divinerage:1285372247665803264>
⬥ For Magic you pray <:overcharge:1344313901189431296> + <:eclipsedsoul:1285372688457797672> + <:divinerage:1285372247665803264>
    • You may also use <:augury:1084799720926478437> for the <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:eofspec:1257438999794946099> then switch back to <:overcharge:1344313901189431296>

Wars:
Sit and auto with <:lengmh:883134308146098227> <:lengoh:883134308070604870> for 8/9 stacks, Get max <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> stacks
⬥ You can possibly proc a stack on head for bonus damage while not losing spec damage
