## __Ranged-Melee Hybrid Grandmaster Speed Killer__ <:range:580168050121113623> <:melee:1096130867279171706>
.tag:rangemeleehybrid
⬥ Rotation strives for 1:10-1:14.
⬥ This rotation is NOT recommended for standard kills, only for the grandmaster achievement with a kill timer significantly faster than using Necromancy if desired.
    • Strongly recommended to only spend time building 10 leng stacks <:primordialice:1219291285358120990> at wars AFTER practicing rotations on each of the four hands individually.
⬥ Dragons slayer task. Kill dragons until about 5-10 left on task to maximize genocidal <:genocidal:689503091539705870> perk.
⬥ For combat dummy placement, click exact spot shown in the example video.
