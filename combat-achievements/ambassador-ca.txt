## __Introduction__
This guide will go over how to complete the Master and Grandmaster speed kill Combat achievements for The Ambassador.

## __General Information__ ##
⬥ Ring of Death <:rod:513190159462825984> can be equipped p4 for more safety.
⬥ [Tier 100 weapons](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Shard_of_Genesis_Essence>) <:shardofgenesisessence:1266418218264694954>
    • May be less consistent without
.
## __Disclaimer__ ##
⬥ These rotations are **NOT** recommended for standard kills, only for the achievement.
    • The setup takes extra time, thus lowering average kills per hour.
⬥ The 0 tick <:roarofawakening:1266417538045317121> <:spec:537340400273195028> in the Master rotation can be replaced with spamming <:ripperscroll:703581275155464203> throughout the fight, although results may not be consistent.
