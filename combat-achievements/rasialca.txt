## __Rasial Master Speed Killer__
.tag:masterspeedrot
⬥ This rotation is **NOT** recommended for standard kills, only for the achievement.
    • The setup takes extra time, thus lowering average kills per hour.

⬥ This rotation seeks to consistently hit under 1 minute kill times.
    • Will be slower without Innate Mastery enhancement <:shardofgenesisessence:1266418218264694954>
    • Switch from <:scriptureofjas:869284342839513108> to <:grim:568262896375824385> after either the 1st or 2nd Jas proc.
        ⬩ <:scriptureofjas:869284342839513108> is activated after loading preset.
⬥ Have Rasial as your left portal in wars retreat for easier movement.
⬥ At specific points during the encounter you may <:veng:543478434953822208>
⬥ **NOTE:** Using <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> too early will result in the first bounce becoming immune.
    • The correct timing of <:deathskulls:1159434663903899728> is the **same tick** as the "Ha! No more games!" message.

## __Connection Lost. Please Wait - Attempting to Reestablish__
.tag:connlost
⬥ This requires you to survive 45 seconds after Rasial casts <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> on phase 4.

.
### Phase 4
⬥ Starts on "Ha! No more games!"
⬥ All basic abilities can be any basics, only used to build adrenaline and get Rasial low to kill.
    • Be careful to not accidentally kill Rasial before completing the achievement.
⬥ Keep <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> alive to heal you.
⬥ Move and kill Rasial as soon as you get the achievement.
