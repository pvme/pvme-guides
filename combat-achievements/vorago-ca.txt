### __Clone Skip:__
.
{
  "embed": {
  "title": "",
    "description":"⬥ Clone Skip refers to skipping the Phase 4 clones. This is done by having the Base Tank walk Vorago all the way to the side of an arena, while the Bomb Tank stays all the way on on the opposite wall, outside of Vorago's attack range. Since Vorago can only give clones to the Bomb Tank, but the Bomb Tank is out of range of Vorago's attacks, the special is skipped entirely.\n\n⬥ There are several ways for the Base Tank to walk Vorago, but the easiest method is to turn your Run off and spam click the opposite side of Vorago in order to walk underneath him. You may have to adjust where you click depending on what direction Vorago starts walking.\n\n⬥Ceiling Collapse rocks can block Vorago from walking to a particular side of the Arena, so make sure to remove all non-corner rocks prior to Clone Skip.\n\n⬥ One thing to note is that Vorago will never walk north. This can be helpful to know as it means north ceiling rocks do not need to be destroyed, and can be useful to save to give the Base Tank more options later for Bleeds Skip.",

.
{
  "embed": {
  "title": "",
    "description":"⬥ Bleeds Skip refers to skipping the Phase 4 bleeds. As soon as he applies the first bleed is when you want to trap him on the rock so he cannot apply any subsequent bleeds.\n\n⬥ Once Vorago is stuck, make sure nobody attacks him as it will break the skip.
