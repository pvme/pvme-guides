**__Room 1__**
Tag a slime with Fragmentation Shot <:frag:535541273755385885> and Corruption Shot <:corruptshot:535541306294796299> while luring the slimes together to use Mechanised Chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881>. With Mechanised Chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881> use Snap Shot <:snap:535534127131394088> and utilize Binding Shot <:bindingshot:535541306563231790> and Tight Bindings <:tight:535541275957657600> in combination with Piercing Shot <:piercing:535541258538450944> to clear the slimes. 4 slimes must be killed to open the barrier. Stuns and binds should be utilized here as the slimes cannot multiply while stunned/bound.

.
**__Room 2__**
Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> + Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> and tag the nearest dragon with Corruption Shot <:corruptshot:535541306294796299>. Proceed down the steps and kill the dragon there in near melee distance to lure the other 2 dragons together. Proceed to Mechanised Chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881> Seren Godbow EoF Spec <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> the remaining 2 dragons and kill them to open the barrier. Diagonally Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> to the dragon next to the barrier and turn around to use Mechanised Chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881> on the now stacked dragons -- use Snap Shot <:snap:535534127131394088> and Shadow Tendrils <:shadowtend:642713547142332416> here. Lastly kill the 4th dragon to open the barrier.

.
**__Room 3__**
Run to this spot along the east wall just past the middle pillar to stack the magma golems to use Mechanised Chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881>.  Only 3 golems must be killed to open the barrier so Snap Shot <:snap:535534127131394088> and Rapid Fire <:rapid:535541270521708566> should do the trick.
.img:https://i.imgur.com/qzt0o4X.png
**__Room 4__**
Diagonally Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> + Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> towards the next barrier and run around to behind the celestial dragon to stack the slimes behind it.  From here stun the celestial then target a stacked slime for a Mechanised Chinchompa <:mechchin:641669268722810881> Seren Godbow EoF Spec <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>.  Clean up the remaining slimes with further stuns until the barrier opens.

.
**__Room 5__**
Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> and run up against the west wall.  Use Deaths Swiftness <:DeathsSwift:513190158812839936> here against the wall and Chin <:mechchin:641669268722810881> down the celestials. Diagonally Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> down the steps and tag the closer celestial.  Run up against the east wall to use the remaining Deaths Swiftness <:DeathsSwift:513190158812839936> timer to kill this celestial to open the barrier. Run down the steps and use Rapid Fire <:rapid:535541270521708566> and basics to kill 1 of the celestials.

.
**__Room 6__**
Stack the 2 nearest celestials and Chin <:mechchin:641669268722810881> them down to open the barrier.

**__Room 1__**
Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> + Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> and run past the onyx dragons as they do not need to be killed.  Tag the 1st dragonstone dragon with Fragmentation Shot <:frag:535541273755385885> and run back towards the pole to lure the dragons together.  A Mechanised Chinchompa <:mechchin:641669268722810881> Seren Godbow EoF Spec <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> and a Mechanised Chinchompa <:mechchin:641669268722810881> Dark Bow EoF Spec <:dbow:643848618553507843> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> should be used here to clear the stacked dragons.

**__Room 2__**
Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> + Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> up the stairs and target the dragonstone closest to the barrier for another Mechanised Chinchompa <:mechchin:641669268722810881> Seren Godbow EoF Spec <:sgb:626466665848242186> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>. A Snap Shot <:snap:535534127131394088> and some basics should be adequate to finish them off.

**__Room 3__**
Drink your Acceleration Powerburst <:accel:756236265472524418> to Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> + Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> past the hydrix dragons to reach to 2 dragonstones at the barrier. (Note: you can cut every corner here) Kill the dragonstones with thresh and an Seren Godbow EoF Spec <:sgb:626466665848242186><:eofspec:746403211908481184>.

**__Room 4__**
Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> and tag the farthest north dragonstone.  Use Fragmentation Shot <:frag:535541273755385885> then run back to where the wall juts out to lure them together to Chin <:mechchin:641669268722810881> them down.  Try to end on full adren for BSD.
