> **__Introduction__**
The Rex Matriarchs are a trio of dinosaur bosses found on Anachronia. They drop components required to make the <:reaverring:839903943018283050>, <:stalkerring:839903943601029120>, <:championring:839903943630520350> and <:channellerring:839903943404027914> rings. Additionally, they also drop the 4 spear parts used to craft <:lanispear:839903893177106454>, a halberd-range stab weapon with T90 damage, T75 accuracy and a passive that increases weapon poison frequency and damage by 5%.

.
**__General Information__**
The Rex Matriarchs have high <:Defence:689509250979987525> levels of 96, making them usually not worthwhile doing for mid-level players. Thus, this guide will be focused on high level strategies for end game players farming logs.

⬥ Mid-level players trying out matriarchs for Reaper tasks are encouraged to come up with their own strategies after going through mechanics or refer to beginner friendly youtube guides.
    • Rex Matriarchs with mid level weapons and T70 armour by <@!135502088049393664> - <https://youtu.be/FqLB0iwkUYk>
⬥ While <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792> bonuses are applicable to them, enhanced <:Tuskas:513201065513058306> is not.
⬥ It is recommended getting <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004> before going for the matriarch logs for a huge accuracy and damage boost of 15%.

**__Hybrid Rotations__**
Below are suggested rotations based on what was used in the example videos. Basics usage are mostly omitted.

⬥ Due to the number of variables present feel free to come up with your own rotations based on your gear.
    • You will likely still be able to get ~85 KPH or more even if not bridding optimally.
⬥ The <:Magic:689504724159823906> rotations for Mage-Ranged and Mage-Melee are usable for both methods, although the naked <:fsoa:869284271595069451> rotation generally works better to start Rathis/Pthen on 100% adren.
⬥ Use <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> on <:gdeathsswift:994644354536837121> to use your desired ability for <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> if killing Pthentraken.

**__Relics and Passives__**
⬥ This method strongly relies on the use of the following passive effects. **Missing any of these may detriment the effectiveness of this method.**
    • Fury of the Small <:furyofthesmall:697808773917573233>
    • Conservation of Energy <:conservationofenergy:697808773711921195> and/or Ring of Vigour Passive <:vigour:615613235512737792> <:warpedgem:968908526955167774>
    • Adrenaline Retention (Persistent Rage <:persistentrage:739965637056659567> / Adrenaline Urn <:adrenurn:656426717413507074>)
    • Dinosaur Slayer Task <:slayerhelm:798285340460449792>

.
__**Explanation and Notes**__
Consistency and safety are attained via <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> damage and healing through specs, taking advantage of <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004>, and proper postion of <:escape:535541258832052231> on the ability bar.

⬥ **Slayer task (with Slayer Helm on your Anachronia slayer lodge stand) is required for accuracy unless using <:hexhunterbow:581180369911414794>**
    • <:hexhunterbow:581180369911414794> passive ensures neither <:necklaceofsalamancy:970657245312467004> or a Dinosaur Slayer task are required to reach 100% accuracy.
    • Suggested to be paired with **Splintering Arrows** <:splinteringarrows:900758234246299649>, but any other T80+ arrow will work.
    • Other viable weapons:
        - <:upgradedboneblowpipe:925794591830056961> **Upgraded Bone Blowpipe** - Viable budget weapon.
        - <:ascmh:513190158468775936> <:bbc:626714879218155521> **Dual-wield Crossbows** - Ascension or better works. Use **Emerald or Onyx Bakriminel Bolts (e)** <:emeraldbakri:866551637714665484> <:OnyxBakri:565726489362956308>.
        - <:superiormorriganjav:860891495033077810> **Superior Morrigan's Javelin** can be used as a budget mainhand weapon, but it cannot utilise Bakriminel Bolts.
        - <:superiormorriganaxe:860891494969376798> **Superior Morrigan's Thrownaxe** - Viable budget weapon. If using this weapon, **RUN MUST BE TURNED OFF**.

⬥ <:vamp:643505653079343144> provides safety through healing and extra poison damage through the <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> passive.
.
⬥ If using <:Desolation:513190159018098713>, you may need <:ancientshard:656426717505650708> to upkeep Prayer.
⬥ <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> is used to heal your <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228>. If it dies, summon another and load it with scrolls.
⬥ To pick up loot, use <:SBSStandards:565726489526534154> <:telekeneticgrab:866551637970255872> to not move from your position.
    • Moving can cause you to get killed on the next Overcharge.

**__Method and Setup__**
⬥ Upon entering arena, click towards Pthentraken's spawn (southeast) and <:surge:535533810004262912> **twice** to be south of the boss, and walk closer by one tile at a time if not immediately attacked (watch from 3:15 onwards in guide video below for an example).

⬥ If using <:superiormorriganaxe:860891494969376798>, **TURN OFF RUN**.
⬥ Ensure all buffs/prayers are active, along with auto retaliate.
    • Make sure <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> is summoned, stocked with scrolls set to the proper autofire rate.
    • Cast <:prismofrestoration:938475261366784070> *at least* once every 5 minutes.

.
⬥ **If you move too close to Pthentraken, __walk away one tile at a time and quickly click Pthentraken each tile__.** This will secure the kill and ensure <:escape:535541258832052231> activates correctly.
    • If any revolution ability gets delayed, you must manually activate <:escape:535541258832052231> just before the Overcharge indicator bar depletes.
