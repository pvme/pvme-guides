.tag:Intro

> __**Mechanics**__
.tag:Mechanics
**__Attack Rotation__**
Mushroom gas → 2 Autos → Cleave → 2 Autos → Frenzy → 2 Autos → Minion Spawn → 2 Autos → Repeat

.
**__Mushroom Gas__**
Avoided by just running out of it. Avoid standing directly on top of a mushroom to decrease the distance needed to move out of the gas. Standing in the mushroom gas will give you a 250 damage bleed every tick, and if standing in the gas for a prolonged time will bind you.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/KHMLekU.mp4
.
**__Cleave__**
Hits in a frontal cone for up to 3k and apply a bleed. Do not freedom this bleed. If you are standing a distance away from Helwyr, he will first lunge towards you before the attack. There are a few methods to handle this attack.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/OKDS0F5.mp4
.
⬥ If using <:Magic:689504724159823906> or <:Range:580168050121113623>, move out of the damage cone. Can also be done with <:melee:615612332521029632> but is DPS waste

⬥ A well-timed Resonance <:res:535541258844635148> will heal a nice chunk. Note you will still acquire the bleed. This method is best if you are struggling to Soul Split <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> your HP back up and surviving the whole hour. This method can become difficult with the Cywir Alphas alive, but it can also be a good chance to practice some tick Resonances <:res:535541258844635148> with little risk as long as you are above 3kish HP.

⬥ Lastly, while praying Soul Split <:soulsplit:615613924506599497>, cast <:disrupt:535614336207552523> / <:Veng:543478434953822208> from the Lunar Spellbook.

.
**__Frenzy__**
Helwyr’s frenzy attack has him leaping around doing rapid melee damage while applying a bleed stack with each attack. Again, while using Range or Magic, you can just run away from Helwyr’s Frenzy attack, but with Melee it is better to stay put and continue doing damage.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/G5CPPoC.mp4
.
If you are getting smacked around, you can always pray melee <:DeflectMelee:544195488447201300> and Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857> or Debilitate <:debil:535541278264393729>. After this attack has concluded, feel free to Freedom <:freedom:535541258240786434>the bleed if you wish.

.
**__Minion Spawn__**
Helwyr’s minion spawn attack has him howl and summon two 3k HP Cywir Alphas. Easily taken out with AoEs.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/EB1ss4g.mp4
.

*Note: A possible setup is bringing <:orangepouch:690848914685034507> with <:Bloodrune:536252658970001409> <:Waterrune:536252660165115905> <:Firerune:536252659850674186> and then filling <:runepouch:583430011868938283> with <:Soulrune:536252660333019136> <:Deathrune:536252659586433024> <:Earthrune:536252659808731137>. This way you can switch between **Blood Burst/Blitz** <:bloodblitz:535616247807868938> and **Exsanguinate** to see which one works best for you.*

⬥ <:GuthixBook:574290439356809216> **Book of Balance** - Decent passive pocket item, provides extra poison procs and damage when activated. The damage contributed by this is actually quite massive, and should be used to ensure the most efficient kills. Reflecting Blood Essence can be used as a budget alternative.
