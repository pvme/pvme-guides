⬥ **Chincend**
Chincending <:incend:535541258429661215><:mechchin:641669268722810881> is stalling Incendiary Shot <:incend:535541258429661215> and releasing it with your Mechanized chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881>. This method and rotation if done right will clear minions in a short time period and can result in minions not even using their first stun.

You should not stand melee-distance or you will release with 2H. 4 spaces away allows you to not lose any ticks. An example vod is provided below.
https://youtu.be/voNcLdo7TT0

.
⬥ **Positioning**
Stand south of where AoD spawns so you can get the Kalgerion demon buff from the base.

> __**Chinner**__
.tag:chin
**__Role Responsibilities__**
.tag:chinrr
The Chinner is the back-up base, responsible for clearing minions in phase 3 with Mechanised Chinchompas <:mechchin:641669268722810881> as well as applying the Statius Warhammer <:swh:641670143197446182> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> debuff at the very beginning of the fight.

They are also responsible for backup basing should the base die after minions have been killed.

.

**__Rotations__**
.tag:chinrot
⬥ There are 3 rotations available to a Chinner, called:
    • **1/3:** Involves the Chinner using their <:deathsswiftpf:867678153949708309> phase 1 and phase 3.
        - Used for slower teams.
    • **2/4:** Involves <:deathsswiftpf:867678153949708309> on phase 2. Phase 4 only if teams are slow enough.
        - Used for faster teams to ensure a timely Chincend <:mechchin:641669268722810881> <:incend:535541258429661215>.
    • **Deathspore:** Involves using Deathspore arrows <:deathsporearrows:900758234527301642> to get a free <:deathsswift:535541258924326912> at the beginning of phase 3 before Chincending <:mechchin:641669268722810881> <:incend:535541258429661215>.
        - Gives the most damage if done correctly.

⬥ **Pillars**
    • Pillars and Zaros phases have no set rotations for rangers, due to adren variation between kills caused by Incendiary's <:incend:535541258429661215> buff and Hydrix bolts <:HydrixBakri:550834403136503822> as well as pillar kill speed varying hugely from team to team or even kill to kill.

    • You should aim to be in <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> on all pillars, using it on the third pillar for it to last throughout the fourth pillar. Finish AoD with <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> active.

    • When travelling between pillars, minimising tick loss is different to other roles due to wanting to keep your ECB <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:eofspec:746403211908481184>, so you can't use Bladed Dive <:bd:535532854281764884> to do this.
        - To work around this, you're going to need to double Surge <:surge:535533810004262912>. Start spam clicking towards the next pillar before doing your ability, then the same tick you do the ability, Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> and then Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> again.
        - By doing this will get you within range of the next pillar. If you aren't able to attack from that new position, make sure to stall an ability on the pillar for once you do run to it.
