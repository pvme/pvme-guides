> __**Chinner**__
.tag:chin
**__Role Responsibilities__**
.tag:chinrr
The chinner is responsible for clearing minions in p3 with mechanised chinchompas as well as applying the statius warhammer debuff in p1 at the very beginning of the fight. They are also repsonsible for backup basing should the base die after minions have been killed. Should base die after minions are killed, it is the chinner's job to take over.

.
**__Preset__**
.tag:chinpre
.img:https://i.imgur.com/fWijZmF.png
.
It is also beneficial for chinners to use the Heightened Senses Relic <:heightenedsenses:697808773573771344> as the 110% adren cap is very useful since the role overcaps on adren a lot. For the 2/4 rotation, it is **required**.

.
**__Rotations__**
.tag:chinrot
There are 2 rotations available to a chinner. The 1/3 rotation involves the chinner using their <:deathsswiftpf:867678153949708309> p1 and p3, while the 2/4 rotation uses their <:deathsswiftpf:867678153949708309> on p2 and, depending on team speed, p4 as well. 1/3 rotation should be used for slower teams, while 2/4 should be used for faster teams to ensure a timely chincend.

By doing this will get you within range of the next pillar. If you aren't able to attack from that new position, make sure to stall an ability on the pillar for once you do run to it.
