> __**Magic Minion Tank**__
.tag:mageent
**__Role Responsibilities__**
.tag:mageentrr
Magic minion tanks are usually umbra. They are responsible for applying <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:eofspec:746403211908481184> in phase 1 as well as vulning umbra and pillars 1, 2 and 3 (if on umbra). They should lure their minion to mid after p2 and relure if necessary.

.

⬥ <:sgb:626466665848242186> - Used on pillars. If comfortable with less food, a sirenic switch can be brought, otherwise unequip tectonic for the spec. ⬥ Armadyl Battlestaff EoF <:eofyellow:780401412902223892> should only be brought if you have a Fractured Staff of Armadyl <:soa:869284271595069451>.

It is beneficial for mages to use the persistent rage relic <:persistentrage:739965637056659567> to ensure that they do not lose adren during their pre-detonate.

> __**Magic Base**__
.tag:magebase
At the time of writing (12/10/21), solo mage base is considered meta at AoD.

.
**__Role Responsibilities__**
.tag:magebaserr
The responsibilities of a mage base are as follows:
⬥ Tank boss for the duration of the fight.
⬥ Apply the vulnerability debuff on phase 1, phase 3, last pillar and zaros.
⬥ Lure <:aod:580167371365548042> at an appropriate time at the end of p3 so as to avoid giving the team shadow traps at pillars.
⬥ Apply Statius' Warhammer debuff during pillars.

.

.tag:beginner

It does not make use of 4ticking or spell swapping and does not require a Fractured Staff of Armadyl. The rotation is best suited for teams whose kills take longer than 2:30 - 2:40 minutes. As with all beginner guides, this should only be used as your stepping stone into basing at AoD.
