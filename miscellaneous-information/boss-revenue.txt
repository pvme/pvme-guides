# Boss Profits
.tag:intro
This channel displays calculated GP/kill and related values for many commonly farmed bosses. The calculations use GE list prices, and auto update as the GE does. Drop rates are sourced from the Runescape Wiki.

.
### How To Use This Guide
To calculate your expected hourly profits <:coins:698816156961603654> for a given boss, take the listed GP/kill total and multiply it by your kills per hour (KPH). Any listed KPH rates below are based on the current meta farming methods, to allow for comparison between bosses. Click [here](<https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1OGM9MBUG2bQVbHxlm86Xfs60soFcjINSOrPv1WL3Vgw/edit?gid=517330451#gid=517330451>) for an overview of all featured bosses with kph per mastery rank.

*Note: The numbers provided do not take into account supply costs, with the exception of Magister and Legiones where key costs have been accounted for.*
