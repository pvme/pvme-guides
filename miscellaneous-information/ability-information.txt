> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
*Note: Use the __**Table of Contents**__ present at the bottom of the channel, or in the pinned comments, to quickly navigate through this channel and find the information you are looking for.*

This channel is intended to provide generic information for all auto attacks/spells/abilities for all 3 combat styles.

Some general notes:
⬥ The game runs on an internal tick system: **1t (tick) = 0.6s (seconds)**.
⬥ The minimum time between abilities is called the global cooldown: **1 gcd = 3t = 1.8s**.
⬥ Auto attacks will only give adrenaline if they hit (splash = no adrenaline gain).
    • Debuff spells like Vuln <:Vuln:537349530551582720> or Enfeeble <:Enfeeble:567727986409734145> or Smoke Cloud <:smokecloud:856635090641879050> do not given adrenaline.
⬥ The following abilities give an **additional 2 + (Invigorating Perk <:invigorating:1009422698633502881> rank * 0.2) %** adrenaline if they hit multiple targets:
    • Dragon Breath <:dbreath:535533833391702017> , (Greater) Chain <:chain:535533833056026624> / <:gchain:787904334495088672> , Detonate <:deto:535533833358016512> and Tsunami <:tsunami:535533809995874304>
⬥ Special attacks may incur a delay when used back-to-back consecutively - see __**Back-to-Back Special Attacks**__ in <#689206023675838471> for more details.
⬥ Some useful sheets and resources are given below.
