# Revolution Bars
.tag:intro
⬥ This channel is dedicated to listing various generic Revolution, Revolution++, and Revolution AoE bars
    • **Revolution** assumes __manual__ threshold and ultimate usage
    • **Revolution++** assumes little-to-no-manual input on thresholds/ultimates
    • **Revolution AoE** are Revolution++ bars designed with AoE in mind (e.g. Slayer <:slayer:797896049548066857>)
⬥ To easily jump to a specific section of bars, check the Table of Contents which are found in the pins and at the end of the channel
⬥ If you have a bar or bar improvement to suggest, use <#1020853673317908500>
