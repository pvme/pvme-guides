# __Revolution Bars__
.tag:introduction
.
> *PvME is open-source! If you have a suggested improvement, use <#1020853673317908500>*

This channel lists the recommended Action Bars to use in Revolution combat mode (see section **Configuring Revolution+** later in this channel for more info).

The different combinations of bars are:
⬥ Single-target <:singletarget:1137770977506369596> (e.g., most bosses)
⬥ Multi-target <:multitarget:1137770974431944835> (e.g., slayer mobs)
⬥ 2-handed (only one weapon can be equipped)
⬥ Dual-wield (where two weapons can be equipped, one in each hand)

If a combination is not displayed, it means the bar scored poorly in tests and is not worth using.

.
## __Disclaimer__
.tag:disclaimer
No revolution bar is perfect for every situation - unless you have accessibility needs, we strongly recommend learning full manual to gain total control over which abilities are fired when. You can find more information in <#1020548216258834512>

Variances may include:
⬥ Single-target <:singletarget:1137770977506369596> bars performing differently based on duration of the fight
    • Testing was conducted at both War's Retreat dummies and Vindicta
⬥ Multi-target <:multitarget:1137770974431944835> bars performing differently based on the number of enemies surrounding you, and their life points
    • Multi-target testing was conducted at Abyssal Beasts in the Sunken Pyramid

.
