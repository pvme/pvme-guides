# Auto Attacks
.tag:intro
Auto attacks, also known as Attack <:meleebasic:1478062907295010906> , Magic <:magicbasic:1478063104146411713> , Necromancy <:necrobasic:1482849360462086244> , and Ranged <:rangedbasic:1478061858622537809> , are the abilities which are automatically cast when no other abilities are cast during combat. Some of these abilities have special properties.
.
## __Attack__ <:meleebasic:1478062907295010906>
.tag:attack
⬥ Generates 1 stack of Bloodlust <:bloodlust:1478080383554945044>

## __Necromancy__ <:necrobasic:1482849360462086244>
.tag:necro
⬥ While Living Death is active, each use of this ability generates two stacks of Necrosis <:necrosis:1139452791878856805>
⬥ While the Omni guard is equipped, each use of the ability generates 1 Death Spark <:deathspark2:1143642268193009724> stack. Upon reaching 5 stacks <:deathspark:1143642264850141205>, the next basic attack is empowered and deals double damage
⬥ By default, <:necrobasic:1482849360462086244> is used whenever other abilities are not being cast, replacing the traditional auto-attacks from other combat styles
⬥ It can be toggled off from being used automatically
