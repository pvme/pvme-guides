## __Introduction__
.tag:Introduction
Welcome to our Feats System channel, the purpose of these challenges is to give you fun objectives to aim for at various encounters, aiming to provide a challenge disregarding any meta whatsoever of farming kills. Happy hunting!

All submissions require supporting evidence to be reviewed by staff.

.

.tag:Submissions
