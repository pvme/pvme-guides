## __Introduction__
.tag:intro
A powerful three-headed dragon residing north-west of the Edgeville lodestone. The King Black Dragon (KBD) <:kbd:513213018054852648> is an iconic lower-level boss suitable for players with tier 50-70 gear. His attacks involve dealing with high-damage Dragonfire and other elemental breath attacks.

## __Tips and Tricks__
.tag:tips
The King Black Dragon's mechanics are mostly accounted for via good preparation such as bringing protection from dragonfire. During the fight you'll be largely focused on dealing good damage.
⬥ If you have access to <:protectfrommelee:1052551452788269127>, use it and stand in melee distance.
⬥ Don't forget to use any damage-boosting prayers you have available!

Your options for dealing damage will vary greatly depending on your gear and unlocked abilities. The below are guidelines, assuming no expensive unlocks.

Build adrenaline (and <:necrosis:1139452791878856805> / <:residualsoul:1139453152169558046> for <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>) and use strong threshold abilities when available:

⬥ Magic <:magic:689504724159823906>: wild magic <:wm:535533809978966037> and asphyxiate <:asphyx:535533833072672778>
    • Try combust <:comb:535533833098100745> and stepping under KBD!
⬥ Melee <:melee:1096130867279171706>: assault <:assault:535532855191928842> and destroy <:destroy:535532879330148352> or hurricane <:cane:535532878969438210>
    • Try Slaughter <:slaughter:535532879237873666> and stepping under KBD!
⬥ Ranged <:range:580168050121113623>: snapshot <:snap:535534127131394088> and rapid fire <:rapid:535541270521708566>
    • Try fragmentation shot <:frag:535541273755385885> and stepping under KBD!
⬥ Necromancy <:necromancy:1148995625896120460>: finger of death <:fingerofdeath:1159434801938432010> and volley of souls <:volleyofsouls:1159435029592686642>
    • Having your skeleton <:conjureskeleton:1159434516658651147> , ghost <:conjureghost:1137809206423060632> , and bloat <:bloat:1159433682403201044> active is also a good idea.
