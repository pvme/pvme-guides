> **__Basic Guide: Shadow Reef__**
.
.img:https://imgur.com/W5h47SR.png
.
ED3 is considered the hardest Elite Dungeon in the trilogy. The requirements to feasibly solo it both in terms of gear and skill is generally considered higher than that of ED1 and ED2.

⬥ Use the zerker aura <:berserker:643505116347105290> <:reckless:643505179378974748> <:mani:643505153709965322> variants for the dungeon. Otherwise, <:vamp:643505653079343144> is fine for learning.

⬥ Use a <:reaverpouch:839903693837959228> familiar if using <:vamp:643505653079343144>

__**Crassian Leviathan (Fight tips)**__
.tag:crassiantips
⬥ <:surge:535533810004262912> in and immediately ult at either safespot to begin the fight.

⬥ Camp <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> on all styles.

⬥ Outline of rotations:
    • <:melee:615612332521029632> <:zerk:535532854004678657> <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> → <:assault:535532853979512842> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977> (if owned) → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> after zerk → cycle thresholds again → move to other side and finish off
    • <:Ranged:689504724403486920> <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> → <:snap:535534127131394088> → <:rapid:535541270521708566> → <:shadowtend:642713547142332416> → <:snap:535534127131394088> → <:rapid:535541270521708566> → <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> → good thresholds → move to other side and finish off
    • <:Magic:689504724159823906> <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> <:smokecloud:856635090641879050> → <:wm:535533809978966037> → <:asphyx:535533833072672778> → <:omni:535533809664262179> → <:wm:535533809978966037> → <:asphyx:535533833072672778> → <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> → move to other side and finish off
    • Use <:zerk:535532854004678657> <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> again on the other side if boss has >100k HP left.

⬥ Sometimes your familiar gets stuck at the entrance, remember to call them if this happens.

⬥ Use channelled abilities such as <:asphyx:535533833072672778> <:destroy:535532879330148352> or <:snipe:535541258425204770> right after moving for headsweeps or during auto attacks to ensure getting them off without the need to cut them short to dodge headsweeps.

⬥ <:freedom:535541258240786434> and move out immediately if stunned while moving to the other side to avoid being killed by breath.

__**Taraket, the Necromancer (Fight tips)**__
.tag:tarakettips
⬥ For the first rift, players may do the following lure when the fight begins to stack Taraket together with the rift when it spawns to AoE both targets easily.
    • There's no need to do this if confident in phasing Taraket and then dealing with the portal or tanking his attacks while killing the portal.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/pukl6qm.mp4
.
⬥ Because Taraket's auto attacks are slow and obvious, <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> flicking is strongly recommended.
    • When he starts using <:deathsswift:994921434633744455>, zoom in to see if he is using <:Magic:689504724159823906> or <:Ranged:689504724403486920> autos.

⬥ <:melee:615612332521029632> rotation outline:
    • Start: Lure Taraket + <:zerk:535532854004678657> <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> → <:assault:535532853979512842> → <:destroy:535532879330148352> → <:gflurry:535532879283879977>
    • Portal spawn: Use strong AoEs with halberd <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> <:cane:535532878969438210> <:cleave:535532878616985610>
    • Bloated spawn: Hit Bloated upon spawn, target Taraket upon landing and priortise AoEs to also hit Bloated.
        - Finish Bloated once Taraket is phased.
    • Carrier spawn: <:zerk:535532854004678657> and kill Corpse Carrier.
        - <:bd:535532854281764884> with halberd <:lanispear:839903893177106454> to clear skeletons if needed.
    • Finish off Taraket.

.
⬥ <:Ranged:689504724403486920> rotation outline:
    • Start: Lure Taraket + <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> → <:snap:535534127131394088> → <:rapid:535541270521708566> → <:shadowtend:642713547142332416>
    • Portal spawn: Equip <:mechchin:641669268722810881> + <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> → good thresholds until Taraket is phased.
    • Bloated spawn: Hit Bloated, (tc) Taraket upon landing and equip <:mechchin:641669268722810881> once Bloated walks together with Taraket.
.
    • Carrier spawn: <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> and target Carrier until Taraket stops flying.
        - Do what you did for Bloated but on Carrier.
        - You can use <:corruptshot:535541306294796299> or <:ricocaroming:867678153635004447> to get rid of skeletons in the vicinity.
    • Finish off Taraket.
        - Don't stand in melee distance if not confident of avoiding the melee hit from **Spirit Wall**.

⬥ <:Magic:689504724159823906> rotation outline:
    • Start: Lure Taraket + <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> <:smokecloud:856635090641879050> → <:wm:535533809978966037> → <:asphyx:535533833072672778> → <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200>

        - Do what you did for Bloated but on Carrier.
        - You can use <:corruptblast:513190159194259467> or <:chaincaroming:867678153962684426> to get rid of skeletons in the vicinity.
    • Finish off Taraket.
        - Don't stand in melee distance if not confident of avoiding the melee hit from **Spirit Wall**.

__**The Ambassador (Fight tips)**__
.tag:ambitips
⬥ Although it is not easy, <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> flicking is extremely recommended during this fight.
    • Consider bringing more food if unable to <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> flick.
    • This is more so the case for <:Ranged:689504724403486920> as it does not have inbuilt damage reduction unlike <:Magic:689504724159823906> with <:animatedead:856635090453135382> or sustain like <:melee:615612332521029632> with <:VampScrim:513201294262009874>.

⬥ You can use <:res:535541258844635148> occasionally if having trouble sustaining HP.
    • This is not feasible when Crassian Ritual Keepers are active.

⬥ Using Target Cycle when Ambi flaps open his arms will allow you to hit spinners before you can click on them.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/dVoH6Re.mp4
.
⬥ Save <:excal:641337999170207763> after Ambi spawns Crassian scouts or during last phase as you will take more damage.

.
⬥ For <:melee:615612332521029632>, follow the general pattern of <:zerk:535532854004678657> rotation → <:dismember:535532879376023572> → <:bloodtend:513190158431158274> → <:overpowerigneous:959089455640215633> → good thresholds
    • Use <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> for <:zerk:535532854004678657>
    • Repeat the general rotation as many times as you need post spinners.

.
⬥ For <:Ranged:689504724403486920>, follow the general pattern of <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518> rotation → <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> → good thresholds
    • Use <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> for <:deathsswiftpf:994921597813137518>
    • Drop smoke at the edge of <:deathsswift:994921434633744455> and move to the other side.
    • Repeat the general rotation as many times as you need post spinners.

.
⬥ For <:Magic:689504724159823906>, follow the general pattern of <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927> rotation → good thresholds → <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200> outside of <:sunshine:994921119381463070>
    • Use <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> for <:sunshinepf:994921595489488927>
    • Drop smoke at the edge of <:sunshine:994921119381463070> and move to the other side.
    • Repeat the general rotation as many times as you need post spinners.

.
⬥ For the last phase focus on blocking the healing smoke, sustaining your HP, and surviving the Magical Onslaughts.

⬥ If uncertain of the timing of second Magical Onslaught, avoid ulting or using igneous abilities to avoid being unable to <:reflect:535541258786177064> (and <:debil:535541278264393729> if not using <:vitality:654618235097972737>).

__**Mobs and movement**__
.tag:mobs
Although it appears easy, moving and clearing mobs efficiently can greatly affect the quality of a run. Clearing slowly may allow many mobs to catch up to you and damage you in an Elite Dungeon, increasing the amount of food required. Be mindful of your adrenaline and captilise on AoE abils whenever feasible.

⬥ AoEs by style, generally from most to least viable
    • Melee: <:cane:535532878969438210> > <:quake:535532879506309150> > <:flurry:864492981763702834> > <:cleave:535532878616985610> > targeted <:bd:535532854281764884>
        - <:meteorstrike:535532879359377439> is viable at places with >3 enemies.
    • Ranged: <:mechchin:641669268722810881> <:rapid:535541270521708566> > <:mechchin:641669268722810881> <:snap:535534127131394088> > <:bombard:535541306391265284> > <:corruptshot:535541306294796299> > <:mechchin:641669268722810881> basic > <:ricocaroming:867678153635004447>
        - <:mechchin:641669268722810881> <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> is good on enemies with high HP such as Warped Skeletons and Sea Horrors.
    • Magic: <:tsunamiincite:958989794757730307> > <:deto:535533833358016512> > <:corruptblast:513190159194259467> > <:dbreath:535533833391702017> > <:chaincaroming:867678153962684426>
        - 4TAA using <:bloodbarrage:537338981747261446> or <:icebarrage:537340400185245701> is very helpful for clears.
        - <:gchaincaroming:867678153882861568> should be used whenever possible if owned for clears, in combination with powerful thresholds or even <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200>

⬥ If running and not hitting mobs, be sure to use protection prayers against the mobs hitting you and run quickly through them.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/vMUfgFi.mp4
.
⬥ You can move behind mobs or back yourself into corners to prevent melee mobs from attacking you.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/hqzM2R3.mp4
.
⬥ One of the most dangerous parts in ED3 is the bridge before Taraket with skeleton archers.
    • The Sea Horrors have a spec that does high typeless damage which can catch you off guard.
    • Skeleton Archers will attack you rapidly with ranged, as such, <:devo:513190158728953857> and pray <:DeflectRange:544195488317046812>
    • For <:melee:615612332521029632>, back yourself into a corner to prevent a Horror from attacking you and use your halberd to AoE.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/jNUYyVz.mp4
.
    • For <:Ranged:689504724403486920>, use <:mechchin:641669268722810881> and target the Necromancer, then use <:bindingshot:535541306563231790> → basics → <:deadshotigneous:959089455283728386> → kite out the Horrors.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/Tp0aI9S.mp4
.
    • For <:Magic:689504724159823906>, use <:dbreath:535533833391702017> from afar targetting the Necromancer, then <:corruptblast:513190159194259467> → <:deto:535533833358016512>, and kite out the horrors.
.
.img:https://imgur.com/YnkBiUx.mp4

⬥ Take advantage of diagonal movement to move quicker and avoid attracting unwanted aggression.
