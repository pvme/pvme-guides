# Vindicta & Gorvek – Boss Guide
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/sHIVTBZvZU.png

.
## __General Information__
.tag:generalInfo
⬥ Vindicta & Gorvek is a multi-phase fight in the Heart of Gielinor.
⬥ In Normal Mode, Vindicta starts at 200,000 LP and mounts Gorvek at 100,000 LP.
⬥ In Hard Mode, Vindicta starts at 300,000 LP and mounts Gorvek at 150,000 LP.
⬥ After Vindicta is defeated in Hard Mode, Gorvek continues the fight alone with 300,000 LP.
⬥ Level 80 Attack <:attack:689509250472476758> is required to create an instance, which you can boost with any attack potion.

Other guides: <#1154899710495166574> <#1251377312730058833> <#1487858611253284945>

## __Fight Mechanics__
.tag:fightMechanics

.
### __Attack Rotation__
**Normal Mode:**
⬥ P1: 2 auto attacks → *Hurricane* → 2 auto attacks / *Ranged Attack* → *Dragonfire* → 3 auto attacks → *Hurricane* → Repeat
⬥ P2: 1 auto attack → *Ranged Attack* → 1 auto attack → *Dragonfire Wall* → Repeat

**Hard Mode:**
⬥ P1: 2 auto attacks → *Hurricane* → 3 auto attacks → *Dragonfire* → 3 auto attacks → *Hurricane* → Repeat
⬥ P2: 1 Melee auto attack → *Ranged Attack* → 1 Melee auto attack → *Fly + Dragonfire Wall* → Repeat
⬥ P3: 10 rapid Melee auto attacks → *Fly + Dragonfire Wall* → Fly back to the middle → Repeat

.
### __Auto Attacks__
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Vindicta's auto attacks deal Melee <:melee:1096130867279171706> damage
⬥ In Hard Mode Phase 3, Gorvek attacks rapidly with Melee auto attacks, hitting up to around 4,500 damage per hit

**Solution:**
⬥ Pray against Melee <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> **or**
⬥ Pray <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> once you are comfortable surviving mechanics
⬥ In Hard Mode Phase 3, use defensive abilities if you are taking too much damage from Gorvek's rapid attacks

.
### __Phase 1 - Hurricane__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/1tatA2X.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Vindicta uses a 5x5 Melee AoE when you are within melee distance
⬥ If you are outside melee distance, *Hurricane* is replaced by a Ranged attack instead

**Solution:**
⬥ Use Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857> to mitigate the damage **or**
⬥ Tank the attack
⬥ Stay outside melee distance if you want to force the Ranged attack instead

.
### __Phase 1 - Ranged Attack__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/Y43i9Dnwrr.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Vindicta fires a single Ranged hit if you are outside melee distance
⬥ In Hard Mode, this deals moderate Ranged damage to a random target

**Solution:**
⬥ Briefly switch to pray against Ranged <:deflectrange:544195488317046812>

.
### __Phase 1 - Dragonfire__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/TG1pugT.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Vindicta raises her arms to signal Gorvek
⬥ Gorvek fires a straight line of flames across the arena
⬥ This deals rapid Magic damage if stood in

**Solution:**
⬥ Move out of the fire
⬥ Avoid standing directly on the dragonfire line, as the damage hits rapidly

.
### __Phase 2 - Mounted Gorvek__
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Vindicta mounts Gorvek once she reaches the phase transition threshold
⬥ In Normal Mode, this happens at 100,000 LP
⬥ In Hard Mode, this happens at 150,000 LP
⬥ Once mounted, Vindicta and Gorvek gain a new attack pattern

**Solution:**
⬥ Prepare for *Ranged Attack* and *Dragonfire Wall*
⬥ Keep moving out of dragonfire lines while continuing to damage the boss

.
### __Phase 2 - Dragonfire Wall__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/flnK9zh.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gorvek flies to a random corner and breathes a straight line of flames across the arena
⬥ This works the same as *Phase 1 - Dragonfire*

**Solution:**
⬥ Move out of the fire **or**
⬥ Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> towards Gorvek and past the fire

.
### __Phase 2 - Ranged Attack__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/h5uwYYH.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gorvek fires a high-damage Ranged hit

**Solution:**
⬥ Use Resonance <:res:535541258844635148> to heal from the hit **or**
⬥ Pray against Ranged <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> and use Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857>

.
### __Phase 3 - Gorvek (HM Only)__
**Mechanic:**
⬥ After Vindicta is defeated, Gorvek continues the fight alone with 300,000 LP
⬥ Gorvek uses rapid Melee auto attacks, then flies away to create a dragonfire wall before returning to the middle

**Solution:**
⬥ Pray against Melee <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> during Gorvek's rapid auto attacks
⬥ Use defensive abilities if needed, as the Melee hits can deal heavy damage
⬥ Be ready to move when Gorvek flies away for *Dragonfire Wall*

.
### __Phase 3 - Dragonfire Wall (HM Only)__
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gorvek flies away and breathes a straight line of flames across the arena
⬥ This works the same as *Phase 1 - Dragonfire*
⬥ In Hard Mode Phase 3, dragonfire lines stay active until three are present
⬥ When the next dragonfire wall is used, the oldest line despawns

**Solution:**
⬥ Move out of the fire
⬥ Track active dragonfire lines and avoid boxing yourself in
⬥ Use Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> or Dive <:dive:1049378668197195808> to reposition safely

.
