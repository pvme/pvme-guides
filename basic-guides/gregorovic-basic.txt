# Gregorovic – Boss Guide
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/befXbfQX0n.png

.
## __General Information__
.tag:generalInfo
⬥ Gregorovic is located in the Heart of Gielinor.
⬥ Gregorovic has 200,000 LP in both Normal Mode and Hard Mode.
⬥ Level 80 Prayer <:prayer:1005450651347730533> and 40 Sliske kill count are required to enter.
⬥ Gregorovic can hit hard and accurately, making him one of the harder GWD2 bosses for learners.
⬥ In Hard Mode, Gregorovic is immune to poison and gains the *Join Me* Wight phase after reaching 0 LP.

Other guides: <#1251160427090542753> <#1251160423093239808> <#1251377265368109076>

## __Fight Mechanics__
.tag:fightMechanics
⬥ Gregorovic deals both <:melee:1096130867279171706> and <:range:580168050121113623> auto-attacks
    • Force Ranged hits by standing at 2+ squares away where possible
    • If using Melee, use halberd-range weapon e.g., Laniakea's Spear <:lanispear:839903893177106454>, Dragon Rider Lance <:dragonriderlance:643161541931171872> or Noxious scythe <:noxscythe:513190159341322240>
⬥ In Hard Mode, flick between Melee <:deflectmelee:544195488447201300> and Ranged <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> protection prayers

.
### __Attack Rotation__
⬥ Normal Mode: 3 auto attacks → *Trick Knife* → 3 auto attacks → *Summon Spirit* → 3 auto attacks → *Glaive Throw* → Repeat
⬥ Hard Mode: 3 auto attacks → *Trick Knife* → 3 auto attacks → *Summon Spirit* → 3 auto attacks → *Glaive Throw* → Repeat
    • At 0 LP in Hard Mode, Gregorovic uses *Join Me*, then resumes his attack rotation after all Wight Hunters are killed

.
### __Trick Knife__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/aFZzLQF.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gregorovic throws a blade after 3 auto attacks
⬥ The blade hits players 3 times total
⬥ In solo, all 3 hits will usually hit you

**Solution:**
⬥ Pray against Ranged <:deflectrange:544195488317046812> / <:protectfrommissiles:1052551455078367313>
⬥ Use <:devo:513190158728953857> if you have low Defence

.
### __Summon Spirit__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/BVvu3F8.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gregorovic summons a spirit that walks towards him, based on whichever mask you are nearest
⬥ If it reaches Gregorovic, he gains a buff
    • **Spirit of Mania** increases attack speed (south-west)
    • **Spirit of Rage** increases damage (south-east)
    • **Spirit of Delirium** increases poison damage (north)
⬥ In Hard Mode, 2 spirits spawn and move faster

**Solutions:**
⬥ Kill or ignore the spirit, or;
⬥ Force the Spirit of Delirium by standing in the north-west
    • Use either Irit Incense Sticks <:IritSticks:690987265371144202> or Venomblood perk <:venomblood:841419289714229258> and completely ignore the spirit

.
### __Glaive Throw__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/ZPw1hZR.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gregorovic marks shadow spots on the floor, including under you
⬥ Standing in shadows causes a large Magic hit

**Solution:**
⬥ Step onto a tile with no shadows

.
### __Shadows__ [(Example)](<https://img.pvme.io/images/4ce8fSc.mp4>)
**Mechanic:**
⬥ Gregorovic summons shadows at 140,000 LP and 60,000 LP and may teleport to them
    • Normal Mode: 2 shadows, then 3 shadows
    • Hard Mode: 3 shadows, then 4 shadows

**Solution:**
⬥ Kill shadows with AoE abilities <:cane:535532878969438210> <:spectralscythe2:1137809149690916945> <:tsunami:535533809995874304> <:bombard:535541306391265284> or simply ignore them
⬥ Note: you may need to ret-arget Gregorovic if he teleports

.
### __Join Me (HM Only)__
**Mechanic:**
⬥ At 0 LP, Gregorovic becomes invulnerable and turns you into a shadow clone
⬥ You will not be able to use damaging abilities
⬥ Gregorovic heals while 6 Wight Hunters spawn over time
⬥ Wight Hunters have 9,000 LP each
⬥ Your auto attacks deal high damage and can bounce to nearby wights

**Solution:**
⬥ Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> to a corner to stack the Wight Hunters in a line
⬥ Click the closest Wight Hunter to deal damage
⬥ Avoid eating solid food, as it delays your auto attacks
⬥ Note: you can use defensive abilities to build adrenaline if low <:freedom:535541258240786434> <:anti:535541306475151390> <:prep:535541258546970624> <:voke:535541259465392143>

.
### __Final Phase (HM Only)__
**Mechanic:**
⬥ After all 6 Wight Hunters die, Gregorovic becomes attackable again, returning with around 65,000-95,000 LP
⬥ Greg will return to his attack rotation

**Solution:**
⬥ Deal damage & handle mechanics normally again

.
