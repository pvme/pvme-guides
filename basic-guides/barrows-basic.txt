> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
The Barrows Brothers <:dharokhead:513222840162582541> were originally composed of six wights, two more members were added after completing the [Ritual of the Mahjarrat](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Ritual_of_the_Mahjarrat>) and [Kindred Spirits](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Kindred_Spirits>) quests.

⬥ This guides aims to get you started on doing Barrows runs, and will focus on the original six brothers
    • It will be assumed that you're using Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> and the entry-level preset
⬥ War's Retreat is used for easy access to the Barrows Brothers <:dharokhead:513222840162582541>
    • War's Retreat Teleport <:warsretreatteleport:1032540079589163049> is unlocked via getting 10 boss kills (any boss)

.
> **__Strategy__**
.tag:strategy
__**Order the Brothers Should be Killed**__
Dharok → Ahrim → Karil → Guthan → Torag → Verac
⬥ You can use protection prayers to mitigate damage
        ⬩ Protect from Melee <:protectfrommelee:1052551452788269127> on Dharok, Guthan, Torag, and Verac
        ⬩ Protect from Magic <:protectfrommagic:1052551456835768351> on Ahrim
        ⬩ Protect from Range <:protectfrommissiles:1052551455078367313> on Karil
⬥ Try to make good use of Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> and Dive <:dive:1049378668197195808>
