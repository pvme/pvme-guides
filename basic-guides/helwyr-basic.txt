> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Helwyr is the Seren general residing in the Heart of Gielinor, and requires 80 Magic <:magic:689504724159823906> and 40 Seren kill count to fight.

⬥ Each of the generals in the Heart of Gielinor have a [reputation system](<https://runescape.wiki/w/Reputation_(Heart_of_Gielinor)>) which gives various rewards for progressing it.
    • Invention <:invention:689509250946695292> materials at 2500
        ⬩ Cywir components <:cywircomp:583430147538026496> may now be discovered.
    • 50% kill count reduction at 4500
    • 100% drop increase at 5000

> **__The Fight__**
.tag:fight
1) Drink an Extreme Magic Potion (or better) before going in, with prayers on.
    a) Pray Protect from Melee <:protectfrommelee:1052551452788269127> and Augury <:augury:1084799720926478437>
2) Spam Target Cycle + <:Vuln:537349530551582720> + <:sonicwave:1087373972443775056> (or <:dbreath:535533833391702017>) while going into spot displayed in image below.
    a) The spot is 1 tile southwest of the middle mushshroom.
.
.img:https://img.pvme.io/images/56qcTQy6BO.png
.
3) Cast Sunshine <:sunshine:994921119381463070> after you have targeted Helwyr <:Helwyr:513213019635843100>, then follow the general approach to rotations outlined below.
    a) If Helwyr mushroom gases your <:sunshine:994921119381463070> area at the start, teleport out and restart.

*Note: when trying to time Target Cycle, it is **important** to run while you spam it, otherwise you cannot spam Target Cycle. If you Target Cycle properly, <:Helwyr:513213019635843100>'s HP box will not show.*

.
> **__Rotations__**
.tag:rotations
⬥ While in <:sunshine:994921119381463070> aim to do 1 <:wm:535533809978966037>, 1 <:asphyx:535533833072672778>, and 1 <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:spec:537340400273195028>
⬥ Use Resonance <:res:535541258844635148> to heal when <:Helwyr:513213019635843100> cleaves (ideally **without** Protect from Melee <:protectfrommelee:1052551452788269127> on).
⬥ Use Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857> or Debilitate <:debil:535541278264393729> when <:Helwyr:513213019635843100> does his frenzy.
    • Alternatively, run away if <:devo:513190158728953857> is on cooldown.
    • Use Freedom <:freedom:535541258240786434> after the frenzy.
    • You can only Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857> every other frenzy.
⬥ Use <:gstaff:513203008608141314> <:spec:537340400273195028> whenever possible (costs ~23 adrenaline with <:rov:513201065877831680>).
    • Ensure you still have adrenaline for <:devo:513190158728953857> etc. when you need it (needs >50% adrenaline).
⬥ Avoid moving <:Helwyr:513213019635843100> unless absolutely necessary as he skips autos when he moves.
    • Only scenarios you should move <:Helwyr:513213019635843100> are:
        ⬩ Walking <:comb:535533833098100745>
        ⬩ To avoid the frenzy when <:devo:513190158728953857> is on cooldown
        ⬩ To avoid the mushroom
⬥ Optionally, whenever you cast <:devo:513190158728953857> / <:freedom:535541258240786434>, you may try to weave in an <:AirSurge:543465115870035999> or <:Vuln:537349530551582720> with it.
    • Be aware, this will not work after <:gconc:869285393223254107> / <:conc:1087373968731811880> / <:asphyx:535533833072672778>
    • If using 2H, input <:AirSurge:543465115870035999> / <:Vuln:537349530551582720> at the same time as your next ability.
    • If using DW, input <:AirSurge:543465115870035999> / <:Vuln:537349530551582720> at the same time as <:devo:513190158728953857> / <:freedom:535541258240786434>
