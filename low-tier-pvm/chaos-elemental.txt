> **__Introduction__**
Chaos Elemental is another old boss, and also one with the distinction of being located in the wilderness. There is not much to this boss, as the only interesting mechanic present is his ability to unequip your gear.

More specific information is available here:
<https://runescape.wiki/w/Chaos_elemental>

> **__Strategy__**
.tag:strategy
Use summer pies and disable *Destroy empty pie dishes when eating* to avoid it's **Madness** attack; making sure you maintain a full inventory.
.img:https://i.imgur.com/ulfMvZk.png
You will rotate between these two rotations:
⬥ `1st kill:` <:gconc:869285393223254107> → <:omnipowerigneous:959089455560540200>
⬥ `2nd kill:` <:gconc:869285393223254107> → <:wm:535533809978966037> → <:dbreath:535533833391702017>

You can build adrenaline on the nearby **Abyssal Savages** - <https://runescape.wiki/w/Abyssal_savage>.
