> __**Introduction**__
.tag:intro
Dagannoth Kings are generally only camped by high level players as a more interesting version of a Dagannoths slayer task (if for some reason you're not skipping Dagannoth tasks), or in an effort to get the pets or the collection log.

More information is available here: <https://runescape.wiki/w/Dagannoth_Kings>

> __**Preset**__
.tag:preset
⬥ For maximum efficiency, bring all 3 styles, hybrid gear in every slot possible.
    • Fremmenik Sea Boots 4 to note the bones, and tribrid the kings.
    • <:hexhunterbow:581180369911414794> is best-in-slot for Dagannoth Prime.
        - The quiver contains araxyte arrows, at least arrows of T80 should be stored in the quiver.
⬥ <:Cinderbanes:513190158355660812> are used as the Kings are poisonable.
⬥ <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714> is used for its passive effect and autos, if opting to use <:ripperscroll:703581275155464203> replace <:restore:642708657825644554> with <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787>.
⬥ <:ancientshard:656426717505650708>  **Ancient elven ritual shard** restores prayer overtime, 5 minute cooldown

__**Optimal Strategy Breakdown**__
Camp <:soulsplit:615613924506599497> and switch to the respective Prayer as you kill the Kings. Cancel channels with basics to gain free adren if the King is about to die.
